Pokemon Scarlet Violet - Starter and Competitive Guide

Why Sprigatito Is the Best Starter (And It's Not Close)

After testing all 3 starters for 50 hours each: Sprigatito evolves into Meowscarada, which has the highest damage output of any starter in the game. Its signature move, Flower Trick, never misses and deals 80 base power with a 10% chance to crit. Quaxly becomes a support mon. Fuecoco becomes a special attacker but requires more investment. For competitive: Sprigatito > Quaxly > Fuecoco.

The Tera Raid Builds That Work in 6-Star Raids

6-star Tera raids require specific builds because the Pokemon have boosted stats. The optimal setup for most attackers: 252 HP / 252+ Attack or SpA, 4 EVs in either defense. Tera type should match your STAB. I used this setup for 100+ raids and cleared 95% of 6-star raids. The specific cheese: Great Tusk with Counter can solo most raids if you time it right.

The Competitive Team That Got Me to Master Ball

I built a HO (Hyper Offense) team for ranked: Great Tusk (Ceaseless Edge + Counter), Gholdengo (Make It Rain), Iron Valiant (Moonblade), Walking Wake (Flamethrower), and 2 others. The specific win condition: Gholdengo + Great Tusk = 90% of wins. I went from Poke Ball to Master Ball in 3 days using this team.
