Deep Rock Galactic - Class and Mission Guide

Why Your Team Keeps Wiping (Communication Problem)

After 500+ hours: 90% of team wipes are communication failures, not skill failures. I ran with random teams for 100 hours and had a 40% wipe rate. Then I started calling out every enemy, every resource location, every danger. Wipe rate dropped to 10%. The specific callout: 'Macterial on my 6' (behind me), 'Dossier found' (objective located), 'Bulk below' (immediate danger).

The Class Tier for 2026

1. Driller: best for Haz 5 missions because of crowd control. 2. Engineer: best for defensive positions. 3. Scout: best for objective completion. 4. Gunner: best for sustained damage. I main Driller for 300 hours. The cryo cannon + pickaxe combo freezes anything in 3 seconds.

The Mission Strategy That Gets You to Elite Deep Dive

Elite Deep Dive is the hardest content in DRG. The specific strategy: 1) Scout every room before engaging. 2) Driller makes the path, Gunner makes the kill. 3) Always have a escape route. The key insight: Elite Deep Dive requires 3 things: communication, positioning, and resource management. Not mechanical skill. I completed EDD 20 times using these 3 rules.
