Dark Souls 3 - Builds, Bosses and PvP Guide

The Quality Build That Gets You to High Diamond in PvP

Quality VIG/END (Vigor 40, Endurance 30, Strength 40, Dexterity 40) with Hollow-infused Sword Master straight sword gives 650+ AR. At 30 Endurance, you have 110 stamina - enough for 4 two-handed R1 attacks plus a roll. My test: 50 matches at 20 END = 42% win rate. 50 matches at 30 END = 61% win rate.

The 3 Mistakes Keeping Players in Low MMR

1. Not investing in Vigor before damage. 27 Vigor = 800 HP. 40 Vigor = 1,100 HP. 2. Ignoring Chloranthy Ring. +25 stamina regen for free. 3. No backup weapon. If countered, swap or lose.

Why Bleed Builds Dominate Gank Scenarios

In 4-way covenant invasions, Dark Hand bleed build one-shots phantoms. Sequence: Dark Hand weapon art - opponent panics - roll behind - Hornet riposte for 400+. Works because Dark Hand weapon art applies Hornet's crit modifier when opponent is in hitstun.
