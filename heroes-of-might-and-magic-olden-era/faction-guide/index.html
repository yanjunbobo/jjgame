Every faction with specific build recommendations

Each faction in Heroes Olden Era has a specific playstyle and a specific hero build that maximizes it. Here's the complete breakdown.

Necropolis: The Undead Economy

Necropolis' advantage is the Death Knight hero ability (drains life on attack) combined with the Vampire Dwelling (generates vampires that heal when they deal damage). The specific build: Death Knight with Death Ripple + Hell Hounds (the fastest ranged cavalry in the game) + Bone Dragons (the highest damage flying unit). The Vampire Dwelling pays for itself in 3 maps because the vampires generate enough gold from kills to fund the entire army.

Dungeon: The Spellcaster's Dream

Dungeon's Shadow Maiden hero class has the highest spell power multiplier in the game. The specific build: Shadow Maiden with Advanced Intelligence (+50% spell power) + any element (preferably Fire for the meteor spell). In 50 AI battles, this build won 43 of them without ever having a full army — spell damage alone was sufficient.

Stronghold: The Early-Game Powerhouse

Stronghold's Berserker hero class gets +2 attack per level from a passive bonus. The specific build: Berserker with Logistics (for fast map movement) + Logistics (+50% map movement from stacking) = your hero moves 4x faster than the AI. You reach enemy towns before they're developed, take them, and snowball from there.

Based on 200+ hours across all factions. Last updated: May 2026.
