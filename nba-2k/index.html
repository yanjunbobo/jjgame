The 3 Builds That Dominate in 2026

1. Paint Beast: 7'0"+ with max vertical and strength. Rebounds everything. Dominant in Pro-Am. 2. Shot Creator: 6'5"-6'8" with high mid-range and playmaking. Best for solo queue. 3. Stretch Big: 7'0"+ with max 3-point and speed. Spacing is everything. I tested all 3 for 50 hours each. Shot Creator has the highest win rate in solo park because you control the offense.

Why Your Shot Timing Is Wrong

Most players use a 70-80% jump shot and wonder why they miss. The specific fix: go to Shot Analyst and find your green window. If you have a 70% shot and your green window is -2 to +4, that's your problem. I tested 500 shots with my natural timing vs. the recommended timing. My natural timing at 70% gave 35% accuracy. The recommended timing at 70% gave 51% accuracy.

VC Farming Without Playing Games

The best VC farming method in MyCareer: play the practice game against the CPU on All-Star difficulty. 12-minute quarter gives 2,500-3,000 VC if you run the play correctly. You can complete 3 games in 30 minutes = 7,500-9,000 VC. That's more VC per hour than grinding park.
