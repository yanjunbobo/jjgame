Privacy Policy

Effective date: May 1, 2026  |  Last reviewed: May 2026

Overview

PlayVault (game.xxym8.com) is an independent game guide website. We value your privacy and keep this site as lightweight as possible. This policy explains what data, if any, we collect and how it's used.

Information We Collect

No personal data collected directly. PlayVault does not ask for your name, email address, account details, or any other identifying information to read our guides.

We use Google Analytics on this site to understand traffic patterns — which guides are most visited, how long readers spend on pages, and what search queries bring people here. This data is aggregated and anonymized by Google. If you want to opt out, you can install the Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on.

Third-Party Services

  • Google AdSense: We display ads via Google AdSense. Google may use cookies to serve ads based on your prior visits to this or other websites. You can opt out of personalized advertising by visiting Google Ads Settings.
  • GitHub Pages: This site is hosted on GitHub Pages. GitHub's servers may log technical information (IP address, browser type) as part of standard hosting operations.
  • IGDB: Game artwork images are loaded from the Internet Game Database (IGDB). Their privacy policy applies to any data shared when loading those images.

Cookies

Our site uses minimal cookies. Specifically:

  • Google Analytics sets measurement cookies (__ga, __gid, __gat) to help us understand site usage. These are standard analytics cookies.
  • Google AdSense may set cookies to improve ad relevance. See How Google uses cookies.
  • No login, comment, or account cookies are used on this site.

External Links

Our guides contain links to external resources — official game sites, community forums, YouTube channels, and tools like calculators and stat trackers. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of any external sites. We recommend checking each site's own privacy policy before submitting any information.

Children's Privacy

PlayVault is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from anyone under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us via our contact page and we will remove it.

Data Retention

We do not store personal data ourselves. Analytics data is retained by Google in accordance with their data retention policies. GitHub's hosting logs follow GitHub's own data retention schedule.

Your Rights Under GDPR / CCPA

If you are located in the European Economic Area or California:

  • You have the right to know what data is collected about you (though we collect virtually none directly)
  • You have the right to request deletion of any data we might hold about you
  • You have the right to opt out of analytics and advertising cookies as described above

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us at our contact page.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date. If we make material changes, we will add a notice on the site homepage for a reasonable period after the change.
