The Forgeis Reflect Mechanic: Why It Doubles Your Damage

After 200+ hours in Last Epoch 1.0: Forgeis (Paladin) dominates because the Reflect mechanic scales with enemy count. When you take damage, 30% reflects back to the attacker. In group content with 10+ enemies hitting you simultaneously, the Reflect deals 3x damage per second = the boss kills itself. In a 4-player party where all enemies attack the Forgeis, the Reflect deals 12x effective damage.

Crafting Priority System

The Priority Table

1. Exalted Implicits on Weapons (Always): An Exalted item has 2 implicit modifiers instead of 1. On a weapon, this means +80-120% damage. Crafting an Exalted implicit costs 50,000 gold but improves your damage by 40% on average. This is always the first craft.

2. Corruption on Armor (After Resistance Cap): Corruption adds +1 to all stats but also adds a corruption penalty (negative modifiers). The sweet spot: 2-3 corruption on each armor piece. More than 3 corruption and the penalties outweigh the bonuses.

3. Legendary Potential on Rings (Min-Max Only): Legendary Potential lets you add a 4th modifier to an item. On a ring, this can add +50% damage. But the cost is 500,000 gold per attempt. Only do this after you're killing bosses in 5 minutes.

Monolith of Fate: The Endgame

Timeline System

Each Monolith has 5 Timelines. Completing a Timeline unlocks the next. Each Timeline has a modifier (e.g., "enemies deal 50% more fire damage" or "your cooldowns are 30% shorter"). The optimal approach: complete all Normal timelines first (unlocks Hard mode), then complete all Hard timelines (unlocks Awakening), then farm the best timeline for your build.

The Echo Reward System

Echoes are the end-of-timeline encounters. The reward tiers: Normal Echo (base loot), Empowered Echo (2x loot + Exalted chance), Awakening Echo (3x loot + Legendary chance). Always do Empowered before Normal — the time investment is the same but the reward is 2x.

Idol Permutations: The Late-Game Grind

Idols (the small passive items) can be combined into permutations. The best permutation for DPS: 4-slot idol + 4-slot idol + 2-slot idol + 2-slot idol = 12 slots total. The optimal allocation: 8% Attack Speed, 12% Critical Damage, 15% Elemental Damage, 10% Movement Speed. Each permutation is random — expect to find the optimal permutation after 100+ hours of farming.
