The callouts and smoke spots that win rounds

Valorant's map knowledge is as important as aim. After 600+ hours, here are the callouts and strategies that matter at Diamond+.

Essential Callouts for Every Map

Ascent: "Mid is open" means both teams have access through the middle. The specific A-site callouts: "CT" (the back corner near the default plant), "Short" (the right entrance from mid), "Lobby" (the left entrance from mid). Every team has different names — learn your team's specific callouts.

Bind: "TP" and "UV" (the two teleporters). "A Short" and "A Long" (the A approach routes). Bind is a pistol-round map — whoever wins pistol usually wins the half because the teleporters give the attacking team a 5v4 advantage if defended correctly.

Split: The map most players get wrong. The specific callouts: "Ramp" (the left entrance to A from mid), "Mid" (the center), "B Ramp" (the right entrance to B from mid). Split favors defenders because the B site is very difficult to retake if the attackers get control of "B Ramp."

The Smoke Spots That Dominate Diamond+

A-Site smokes on Ascent: Smoke CT (blocks the CT angle), smoke Short (blocks the right angle), smoke Lobby (blocks the left angle). This triple-smoke on Ascent A-site is the standard — it forces defenders to either push through smoke (and die) or hold from the site (and get flashed).

The specific advanced smoke: One-way smokes (smoking a position where the enemy can see through the smoke but you can't). On Haven, smoking "C Long" from the C window position creates a one-way that lets you see enemies crossing while they can't see you.

Why Split Is Secretly the Best Map for Cypher

Cypher's camera and tripwire are most effective on Split because the map has two main mid entrances (A Ramp and B Ramp) that funnel enemies into predictable paths. Placing a tripwire in "B Ramp" catches 90% of B rushes because every team uses B Ramp for B-site attacks. On maps like Bind (with teleporters), tripwires are less effective because teleporters create unpredictable angles.
