Valorant Map Guide

Callouts, Angles & Utility Lineups

Valorant maps are the most strategically complex in any tactical shooter — every corner has multiple angles, every agent's utility creates unique crossfires, and the best players know the hidden lineup spots that the enemy doesn't expect. This guide covers the callout system, the essential angles, and the utility lineups that pros use on every map in the 2026 competitive rotation.

🗺️ Ascent — The Most Balanced Map

Key Callouts:
A Main (also called "Alt A" or "Mid-Connect"), B Main (or "B Short"), Market (the covered area between A and B, also called "Elbow"), CT Spawn, T Spawn. When calling in Asc, always specify "A Main" or "B Main" — just saying "A" is ambiguous because A site has multiple entry points.
Best Angles:
A site: CT cubby (holds A Main AND Market), A Ramp (holds the connector). B site: B Market window (holds B Main and CT rotation). The CT cubby on Ascent is the most valuable piece of real estate on the map — whoever controls it controls half the rotations.
Utility Lineup — Omen Paranoya into A Site:
From T Spawn, stand at the far left near the truck, aim at the roof edge above CT Spawn, throw. The paranoia travels across the entire A site and blinds anyone facing away. Combine with a Jett dash into A Main for maximum chaos.

🗺️ Haven — Three Sites, Maximum Chaos

Key Callouts:
A Site (or "A Long" and "A Short"), B Site (the courtyard — also called "Mid" or "B Mid"), C Site (or "C Long" and "C Short"). Haven has 3 sites which means it's the most rotation-heavy map in Valorant. The key to Haven is understanding that the enemy team can rotate from any of 6 directions at any time.
Best Angles:
A Long Window (holds A approach from B rotation), C Long Link (holds C and mid simultaneously), B Site Courtyard (exposed but critical — holding it means no free rotation through mid).
Utility Lineup — Sage Wall on B from T Spawn:
Stand in T Spawn near the left rock, aim at the middle of the B arch, place wall. This wall cuts B site in half and forces the enemy to destroy it before taking site. If you combine with a Raze Satchel on the wall, you can boogie-board (Satchel + jump) onto site.

🗺️ Pearl — The Newest Map (2024)

Key Callouts:
A Site (or "A Ramp" and "A Main"), B Site (or "B Lobby" and "B Mid"), Mid (the flowing water area — also called "Piazza"). Pearl's tight corridors reward team coordination more than individual aim. The A Sub (between A and Mid) is the most contested area on the map.
Utility Lineup — Brimstone Stim Beacon in B from A:
From A Lobby, aim at the top of B Mid arch, place stim. The stimBeacon covers the entire B site entrance — anyone entering B site gets +12% movement speed and +15% damage. Perfect for execute rounds.
