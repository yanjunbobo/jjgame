Economy Guide — The Math Behind Every Buy Round | 2026

Valorant economy is the most misunderstood mechanic in low-ranked games. 73% of Gold-Platinum players make buy round mistakes that cost their team 200+ points per round. This guide covers the exact math and decision-making process used by Radiant players.

📐 The Loss Bonus Formula

  • 0 consecutive losses: +$1,400 loss bonus
  • 1 loss: +$1,900 (total $1,900)
  • 2 losses: +$2,400 (total $4,300)
  • 3+ losses: +$2,900 (caps at $4,900)

After 2 losses, a team can afford full buys even from an 0-2 start — this is why comeback rounds are possible.

⚠️ The 5 Economy Mistakes That Lose Games

Mistake 1: One Player Buying Rifle on Eco (griefing the team)
If 1 player in a $2,000 eco round buys an AK ($2,700), they leave the team with $3,000 total for 4 players. That's $750 per person — impossible to full buy. If your teammate does this, mute them and focus on your own game.
Mistake 2: Saving When You Have $4,800 (force buy territory)
$4,800 = full SMG buy + armor + abilities. At this amount, you have enough for a competitive force. Saving here hoping for a full rifle next round only works if 3+ teammates also saved. Solo saving at $4,800 means you arrive with $6,800 next round while your team has $3,000 — a 3v5.
Mistake 3: Not Tracking Enemy Economy (you're guessing)
By round 8, you should know if enemies can full buy. If they won 3 rounds straight with kills, they have $5,000+ each. If they eco'd the last 2 rounds, they have $2,000. Adjust your aggression accordingly — push aggressively if they eco, play slow if they full buy.
Mistake 4: Armor on Eco With Less Than $1,200
Light armor costs $400. If you have $1,200 and buy armor + pistol, you have $800 left. This means even 2 kills = $400 bonus won't be enough for a rifle next round. Better to go pure eco ($800) and build toward a force buy next round.
Mistake 5: Full Saving After a Win (over-saving)
After winning a round, you get +$3,000 base + loss bonus. If you have $5,000, a full buy this round + $3,000 next round = $8,000 next round. Going $7,000 into the bank and forcing your team to eco because only you can afford rifles is selfish team play.

🎯 Pro-Level Economy Plays

  • The Hero Buy: When you're the best player and have $6,000, buy full rifle while teammates eco. You get 1 extra kill with better gun = worth more than 4 light buys.
  • The Spread Buy: In force rounds, distribute buys: 2 players get SMGs, 2 get light armor + abilities, 1 saves. This spreads firepower while keeping options open.
  • The Terminal Buy: In the final round before half, if you're 3-8 or worse, buy everything. You're already losing — the economy resets at halftime, so spend it all.
