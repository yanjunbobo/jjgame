🏆 Best Agents for Solo Queue Climbing (Data Breakdown)

Solo queue requires agents who can create value without team coordination. Based on Tracker.gg data from 180,000 solo Diamond players:

  • Sova (Rank #1): Recon bolts give instant information. In Diamond and below, 68% of enemies panic and reposition predictably after being revealed. Drone provides safe recon without risking your life. His ultimate charges in 7 kills or 100 seconds — it wins rounds.
  • Omen (Rank #2): Paranoya is the best blind in the game. His teleport enables rotations no other controller can match. In solo queue, having a teleport means you can always escape bad situations.
  • Killjoy (Rank #3): Turret + alarmbot holds sites without requiring active play. The ultimate provides post-plant denial. Best for players who prefer supporting from safety.

⚠️ Avoid These Agents for Climbing (Low Win Rates)

Yoru: 44% win rate in solo queue. His teleports are predictable and his flash requires precise timing that solo queue doesn't allow.

Reyna: 46% win rate in Diamond solo. Too dependent on self-sufficient kills. Without a team to create openings, her kit becomes limited.

Harbor: 45% win rate overall. Low utility compared to other controllers. His walls are easily countered by Sova recon.

🎯 Role Composition Guide — What Your Team Needs

The optimal team composition has: 1 Duelist + 1 Controller + 1 Initiator + 1 Sentinel (or 2nd Controller) + 1 Flex

If you're last picking and see your team lacks: smokes (Omen/Clove) > info (Sova/Gekko) > site hold (Killjoy/Vyse)
