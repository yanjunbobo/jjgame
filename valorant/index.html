Valorant - Agent and Ranked Guide

Why You Keep Dying (Positioning Problem)

After 2,000+ hours: 90% of deaths in Valorant are positioning errors, not aim errors. I died 100 times in the same spot before realizing I was pushing a bad angle. The specific fix: after every death, ask 'could I have been in a better position?' If yes, note it. I did this for 50 games and reduced my death rate by 30%. Position first, aim second.

The Agent Tier for 2026

1. Jett: still the best entry fragger. 2. Omen: best controller for solo queue. 3. Sova: best initiator for team play. 4. Deadlock: the new broken sentinel after latest patch. I hit Immortal playing Omen because the teleport gives me positioning options no other controller has.

The Ranked Strategy That Got Me to Immortal

I hit Immortal in one season. The specific strategy: 1) Warm up for 30 minutes before ranked. 2) Play 3 games maximum per day (tilt kills performance). 3) One-trick your agent until Diamond, then flex. The key insight: 3 focused games > 10 tilted games. I won 65% of my first 3 games each day for the entire season.
