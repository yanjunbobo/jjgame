2. Map Tier List

S: Ascent

Most Balanced

The gold standard. Both sides feel fair. Great for learning fundamentals. CT side is slightly stronger.

A: Bind

Attacker Favors

Fast rotates via teleport. Good for aggressive teams. Site takes are strong but retakes are harder.

A: Pearl

Defender Favors

Mid control is king. CT side has strong defaults. Less utility combos than other maps.

B: Haven

Chaotic

3 lanes = lots of solo play. Great for aim diff. Weak CT side due to multiple attack angles.

B: Icebox

Learning Map

Verticality is unique. Hardest map for solo queue. Requires team coordination to attack sites effectively.

C: Sunset

Newer Map

Mid is the battleground. One-way smoke from B Main is broken. CT side takes practice to master.

3. How to Rank Up

🎯 Key Principle: Utility First, Kills Second

In ranked, the player with the best utility usage wins more than the player with the best aim. Smoke correctly, flash for your team, use abilities to gain information.

🎯 Crosshair Placement

Always keep your crosshair at head height. Pre-aim common angles before peeking. Bad crosshair placement is the #1 reason players lose aim duels they should win.

🎯 Economy Management

Buy armor every round unless you have <200 credits. Don't save for a rifle if it means you'll die to a Phantom first. Eco round: sheriff + light armor. Bonus round: full buy.

🎯 One Agent Mastery

Don't main multiple agents. Master 1-2 agents first, then expand. One-tricks consistently rank higher than agents who play 5 different agents at a B level.

4. Pro Tips

⭐ Jett Tip: One-Way Clouds

After using Jett's cloud burst, you can still see through it from your side. Use it as a one-way smoke to spot enemies without exposing yourself.

⭐ Killjoy: Anchor Spots

Killjoy's turret scans 180 degrees. Place it to cover the angle you're least likely to watch. Let the turret babysit that angle while you hold another.

⭐ Omen: Teleport Mind Games

The teleport has a sound cue. Use it to fake a flank — teleport toward your own site, then immediately rotate back. Enemies will be expecting you elsewhere.

⭐ Gekko: Dizzy First

Always lead site entries with Dizzy (flash). It bounces and blinds enemies for 2.5 seconds. Your team should be swinging immediately after it pops.

5. Common Mistakes

❌ Solo Queuing Controller

Controllers (Omen, Brimstone, Viper) are the worst agents to solo queue with. You smoke, your team doesn't push. Use duelists or initiators for climbing solo.

❌ Not Using Abilities to Gain Info

Many players hold abilities for "the right moment" and never use them. Use abilities to get information. A Sova drone that dies revealing 2 enemies is worth more than keeping it to plant.

❌ Bad Post-Plant Position

After planting, 90% of players stay in the same spot. This is predictable. Rotate to a different angle after plant. The defuser can't shoot if you're on the other side of the site.
