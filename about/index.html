About PlayVault

Last updated: May 2026

Who We Are

PlayVault is a small, independent project run by a group of gamers who got tired of clicking through ten different sites just to find one solid build guide. We started building this in early 2025 as a side project — one person writing a guide, a few friends testing it and pointing out everything wrong with it, and a gradual accumulation of pages that eventually became a site covering 70+ games.

What We Do

We write free, detailed guides for games we actually play. Each guide starts with a simple question: what do players Google at 2am when they're stuck? Tier lists come from parsing in-game data and community-tracked stats. Build recommendations come from hands-on testing across hundreds of hours. If we haven't personally tried it, we say so.

Our coverage spans competitive shooters (Valorant, CS2, Apex Legends), open-world RPGs (Elden Ring, Genshin Impact, Baldur's Gate 3), mobile games (Pokemon GO, PUBG Mobile), and indie gems (Hades II, Slay the Spire 2). We add new games based on what our readers are actually searching for — not what we think is cool.

Why Free?

No paywall. No "premium" section. No 30-second ad before you see a build. The site runs on AdSense, and that's it. We believe the information should be where the players are. If a guide takes three seconds to load and you find what you needed, we've done our job.
