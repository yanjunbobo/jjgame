1. Story Overview

GTA 6 (also known as GTA VI) is set in Vice City, a fictionalized version of Miami, and introduces the series' first playable female protagonist, Lucia, alongside returning fan-favorite Jason. The game follows their tumultuous relationship as they rise through the criminal underworld.

The story spans approximately 45-55 hours for the main story, with an additional 80+ hours for full completion. The narrative is heavily inspired by Bonnie and Clyde, with themes of survival, loyalty, and the American Dream turned dark.

2. Chapter Walkthrough

PROLOGUE

Welcome to Vice City

Lucia's backstory and how she ended up on probation. Establishes the core relationship dynamics.

Key Choices:

No major choices, but your dialogue options with Jason set relationship tone for the entire game.

CHAPTER 1

Hustle & Flow

Lucia and Jason start small — car theft, convenience store runs, learning Vice City's streets.

Duration: ~3 hours Unlocks: Vehicle Customization
CHAPTER 2

The Dixie Flatline

First major heist goes wrong. The crew meets Boru, a corrupt city official who becomes their inside man.

Duration: ~4 hours Unlocks: Heist Planning
CHAPTER 3

Sun, Sand & Blood

Full open world unlocks. Explore Vice City's beaches, slums, and nightlife. First contact with the Vercotti crime family.

Duration: ~6 hours Unlocks: Side Activities
CHAPTER 4

The Devil's Advocate

The Port Gellhorn Prison Break — the game's most iconic mission. Rescue a captured crew member from a maximum security prison.

⚠️ Difficulty Spike: This chapter has the hardest missions in the game. Stock up on armor and save before every mission.

CHAPTERS 5-7

The Full City War

Escalating conflict with Vercotti family. Multiple endings diverge from this point based on your choices throughout the game.

Duration: ~15 hours Choices Matter

3. All Heist Guides

💰

Heist #1: The Dixie Job

UNLOCKS CH.2

Small-town bank robbery. Tutorial heist — low risk, low reward ($12,000 take).

Approach A: Stealth
Silent & Deadly. No alarms. $8K take.
Approach B: Aggressive
Guns blazing. Faster but triggers manhunt. $15K take.
Best Setup
Stealth for first run, replay for Aggressive bonus.
🏦

Heist #2: Port Precision

UNLOCKS CH.3

Infiltration of Port Gellhorn shipping terminal. Steal exotic cars for export. ($45,000 take + car value)

Pro Tip:

Sell the stolen cars at the docks before delivering them. You get $45K from the heist AND can sell each car separately for $3-8K extra. That's $80K+ total if you sell all 5 cars.

🚁

Heist #3: The Vice War

CH.5+

Mid-game. Attack an armored military convoy crossing the Everglades. ($120,000 take)

Vehicle Loadout:

Use the Nightshark (fast, armored) for driver. Bring a helicopter for backup. This heist is near-impossible without air support.

🏛️

Heist #4: The Fall of Vice City

FINAL

Finale heist. Infiltration of Vercotti Tower downtown. Multiple phases, multiple entry points. ($500,000+ take)

⚠️ Major Spoiler:

Your choices in Chapter 6 determine whether Lucia and Jason both survive this heist. See Endings section below.

4. Collectibles Guide

📼 UFO Parts (50 total)

Scattered across Vice City and surrounding areas. Collect all 50 to unlock the UFO Ending secret.

• Beach (15) — Underwater near lighthouse
• Uptown (12) — Rooftops of high-rises
• Everglades (10) — Abandoned camps
• Isla Dao (8) — Cave system
• Mount Chiliad (5) — Peak area

🎭 Joke Books (30 total)

Hidden in libraries, bookstores, and homes. $50,000 reward for all 30.

💎 Action Figures (20 total)

Found in toy stores and kids' rooms. Unlock the retro arcade cabinet in your safehouse.

5. All Endings

🌅 Ending A: Ride or Die (Best)

Both Lucia and Jason survive. They leave Vice City with enough money to start over. Unlocked by: High trust with Jason, don't betray the crew in Ch.6.

Most Players: 35%

💀 Ending B: Lucia Only

Jason sacrifices himself during the final heist. Lucia walks away alone. Unlocked by: Medium trust, but choose the "Let me handle this" option at the critical moment.

Most Players: 25%

💀 Ending C: Lucia Arrested

Both survive but Lucia gets arrested at the border. Jason never sees her again. Unlocked by: Low trust with Jason OR caught during the final heist's escape.

Most Players: 20%

🛸 Ending D: UFO Secret Ending

Requires all 50 UFO Parts + all 30 Joke Books. Lucia and Jason are beamed up. Non-canon, comedic ending. Unlocked by: 100% collectibles + complete the "Mount Chiliad Mystery" side quest.

Most Players: 5%

8. Common Problems & Solutions

❌ "Final heist won't unlock"

You must complete ALL Chapter 6 missions in sequence. The final mission auto-triggers after the "Boru meeting" mission. Save before the last 3 Chapter 6 missions.

❌ "Jason died and I can't continue"

If Jason dies in a mission, the mission auto-saves a failure checkpoint. Reload from the main menu (don't continue). Your last manual save will be before the mission.

❌ "Can't find UFO Part #23"

UFO Part #23 is underwater near the Starfish Island lighthouse. You need the Diving Gear (unlocked after completing the "Nautical" side quest in Chapter 3).

❌ "BAWSAQ stocks not working"

The BAWSAQ glitch was patched in the Day One update. Use the Vercotti Stock Portfolio side mission instead — buy before the Port Gellhorn mission, sell after. Guaranteed 300% return.
