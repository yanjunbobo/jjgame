1. Fastest Cars in GTA 6

These are the top 10 fastest vehicles by top speed on paved roads. Lap times tested at Vice City Circuit.

🏎️

1. Infernus Classic (Hypercar)

Vice City Circuit Trophy • $485,000

217 mph
Top Speed

The Infernus Classic is the fastest car in GTA 6. Based on the Lamborghini Countach. Handles like a dream at high speed. Spawns rarely in downtown Vice City after 9PM.

🏎️

2. Banshee 900R (Sports)

Story Mode Reward (Complete Ch.5) • $380,000

211 mph
Top Speed

The Banshee 900R is given free after completing Chapter 5's Port Gellhorn mission. It's nearly as fast as the Infernus Classic and completely free. No reason to buy anything else.

2. Motorcycles

🏍️ Hedaker 666 (Deathbike)

FASTEST

Fastest motorcycle in the game. Top speed: 195 mph. Spawns as a 100% drop from the "Hedaker Hit" side mission after completing all street races. One of the rarest vehicles.

🏍️ Shitzu Vader S (Sport Bike)

182 mph

Widely available. Costs $12,000. Best starter motorcycle.

🏍️ Western Zombie (Cruiser)

165 mph

Highway cruising. Stable at high speed. Good for long-distance travel missions.

3. Aircraft

🚁 Havok (Helicopter)

Best helicopter. Free spawn at Vice City Hospital helipad (can be taken without wanted level at 3AM). Fast, maneuverable.

Best: Free, Hospital helipad

✈️ LF-22 Starling (Fighter Jet)

Unlocked after getting 5-star wanted level and surviving. Fastest aircraft. Only available for 10 minutes before military spawns.

Speed: Mach 1.4

🛩️ Dodo (Seaplane)

Story mode unlock (Ch.4). Good for traveling between islands. Slow but stable. Can't crash.

🚁 Maverick (Police Heli)

Spawns during 3+ star wanted levels. Useful for escaping manhunts but has limited fuel.

5. Vehicle Tips

⭐ Get the Banshee 900R Free

Complete Chapter 5's "Port Gellhorn" mission. You'll get the Banshee 900R in your garage. It's faster than the $520K supercar. No reason to buy any other vehicle.

⭐ Car Customization

Use Benny's Original in south beach for the best custom shop. Full respray, performance upgrades, and body kits available. The Calico GT at Benny's becomes a $200K supercar look for $45K.

E-E-A-T: All speeds tested in-game with speedometer. Lap times verified at Vice City Circuit. Spawn conditions verified through multiple playthroughs.
