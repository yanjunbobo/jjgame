2. Heist Replay (Best Steady Income)

Port Precision — $95K in 15 Minutes

After completing the game, replay Heist #2 (Port Precision) using the export exploit. This is the most consistent money-making method.

Step-by-Step:
  1. 1. Replay Heist #2 (Port Precision)
  2. 2. Complete heist normally ($45K base)
  3. 3. Don't deliver cars immediately
  4. 4. Drive each car to Benny's Original (south beach)
  5. 5. Apply basic customization (respray = $500, any mod = $1000)
  6. 6. Sell at Export Dock — each car = $8-12K bonus
  7. 7. Total: $45K + $50K export bonus = $95K

Time efficiency: Once you know the route, this takes 12-15 minutes per run. That's $380K+ per hour. Do 5 runs, then switch activities to avoid burnout.

4. Stock Market (BAWSAQ)

The BAWSAQ stock market responds to in-game events. Buying low and selling high after story missions can yield 300-800% returns.

Key Stock Opportunities:
Vercotti Stock (VCP) Buy before Port Gellhorn mission, sell after: +300%
Grotti Auto (GTA) Buy after buying Auto Shop: +150%
Dust Holdings (DST) Buy before "Construction" mission: +200%

⚠️ The BAWSAQ Exploit (Patched)

The old GTA 5 "invest before final mission, sell after" glitch was patched on Day One. BAWSAQ now correctly tracks your in-game actions. No more 8000% returns.

5. Vehicle Export

Steal high-end cars and sell them at the Export Dock. Different cars have different values. The higher the street value, the more you get.

Infernus Classic $12,000
Banshee 900R $10,000
Turismo Omologato $9,500
Krieger GT $8,000
Calico GT $6,500

Best spot: Spawns in downtown Vice City after 9PM. Drive to export immediately — damage reduces value.

6. Misc Income Sources

$20K per 20 minutes. Low effort but slow. Use as a break between intensive activities.

$500-2K per store. Quick in-and-out. Accumulate small amounts that add up.

7. Optimized Daily Route

If you have 1-2 hours per day, here's the optimal money-making sequence:

1. Login → Collect Nightclub passive ($50K)
2. Complete Auto Shop customer mission ($80K)
3. Replay Heist #2 with export exploit (x3 = $285K)
4. Export any high-end cars you find ($10-30K)

Total per session: ~$500K in 90 minutes. Daily discipline = $3.5M per week.

8. Early Game Money Tips

  • • Don't buy expensive cars early — you'll get the Banshee 900R free after Chapter 5.
  • • Complete side missions — they give $10-50K each and unlock better activities.
  • • Use taxis and buses instead of stealing cars — saves time for missions.
  • • Complete "Collector" side quest (50 UFO parts) — gives $500K bonus at the end.
  • • Don't waste money on casino until you have $2M saved. The penthouse is worth it.
