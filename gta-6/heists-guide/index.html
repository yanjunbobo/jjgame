1. Heist #1: The Dixie Job

🏦

The Dixie Job (Tutorial Heist)

Chapter 2 • Unlocks after "The Dixie Flatline"

The tutorial heist. Low risk, low reward, but teaches you the heist system. You rob a small-town bank in Dixie County.

Minimum Take

$8,000 (stealth)

Maximum Take

$15,000 (aggressive)

Difficulty

Easy

🎯 Stealth Approach (Recommended)

  1. 1. Enter bank through back door (accessible via alley)
  2. 2. Tie up manager first — he has the vault code
  3. 3. Use thermal drill on vault (30 seconds)
  4. 4. Grab cash bags + manager's briefcase
  5. 5. Leave through roof exit before police arrive
  6. 6. Lose police tail using boat at the dock

Result: $8,000 + No wanted level at exit = Clean getaway

💥 Aggressive Approach

  1. 1. Walk in front door with weapons drawn
  2. 2. Threaten staff — manager opens vault under pressure
  3. 3. Grab all cash (timer: 90 seconds)
  4. 4. Police arrive in 3 minutes
  5. 5. Fight through to parking garage escape car
  6. 6. Lose tail at highway — triggers 3-star wanted

Result: $15,000 + 3-star manhunt = Higher risk

Best Crew Selection

This heist has no crew costs. Choose anyone — they'll join for the experience. Pick Boru as your getaway driver for the later missions.

2. Heist #2: Port Precision

🚢

Port Precision

Chapter 3 • Steal exotic cars for export

Infiltrate Port Gellhorn shipping terminal. Steal 5 exotic cars for export. This is where the real money starts.

Base Take

$45,000

Car Export Bonus

$30-50K extra

Total Potential

$95K+

📍 Pre-Heist Setup

  1. 1. Meet with Boru at the docks — he provides inside access
  2. 2. Visit Benny's Original to modify your escape vehicles
  3. 3. Purchase 2 Insurgent Pickups for crew members
  4. 4. Scout the port at night before — reveals guard routes

🎯 Execution Walkthrough

  1. 1. Enter through service entrance (use Boru's access code)
  2. 2. Split team: Lucia on foot, Jason drives truck
  3. 3. Disable security cameras in server room (10 seconds each)
  4. 4. Load 5 cars onto transport trucks
  5. 5. Fight off security team (10 guards)
  6. 6. Exit through north gate — triggers police response
  7. 7. Lose police at highway tunnel

💰 Secret Export Exploit

After the heist, don't deliver all cars to the fence immediately. Instead, drive each car to Benny's first, customize it (even just a respray), then sell. The export value increases by $3-8K per car. Total bonus: $40K+ extra.

3. Heist #3: The Vice War

🚁

The Vice War

Chapter 5 • Attack military convoy

The most action-intensive heist. Intercept a military convoy crossing the Everglades. High risk, high reward.

Difficulty Spike: This heist is significantly harder than the first two. Recommended level: 40+ hours into story, fully upgraded weapons, max health/armor.

Take

$120,000

Requirements

Nightshark + Helicopter + 4 crew

🛡️ Vehicle Loadout

Nightshark (Armored SUV) — Main escape vehicle. Fast, bulletproof, holds 4 players. Upgrade: Military armor package at Benny's.

Buzzard Attack Helicopter — Air support. Deploys on rooftop in Phase 2. Essential for convoy assault.

Insurgent (backup) — If Nightshark gets destroyed.

🎯 Phase-by-Phase Walkthrough

Phase 1: Ambush

Position at the bridge crossing. Wait for convoy (3 trucks). First truck is decoy — explosives will disable it. Focus on the middle truck (contains the payload).

Phase 2: Air Support

Military sends 2 helicopters. Your co-pilot provides covering fire. Stay in Nightshark — it's bulletproof to helicopter guns.

Phase 3: Convoy Intercept

Destroy escort vehicles. Jason drives, Lucia shoots from roof. Target the fuel truck — explosion clears the path. Grab payload and speed to safehouse.

⚠️ Critical Tips

  • • If Nightshark explodes, you have 30 seconds to reach the Insurgent before fail.
  • • Helicopter support is non-negotiable. Solo players should use the invincibility glitch (exit to title at right moment).
  • • Bring body armor x3 — you'll take heavy fire during Phase 2.

4. Heist #4: The Fall of Vice City (Final)

🏛️

The Fall of Vice City

Final Heist • Infiltrate Vercotti Tower

The climactic finale. Infiltrate the Vercotti crime family's downtown tower. Multiple entry points, multiple phases, and your choices determine whether Lucia and Jason both survive.

Total Take (Both Approaches)

$500,000+ (split between characters)

🎭 Entry Approach Selection

Stealth Entry

Garage entrance → Elevator to floor 47 → Server room (data theft) → Rooftop extraction. Lower take (~$250K) but both characters survive guaranteed.

Aggressive Entry

Lobby assault → Escalator → Floor 47 → Vault. Higher take (~$500K) but Jason survival depends on story choices.

📍 Complete Walkthrough (Stealth)

  1. 1. Enter via garage ramp (blueprints show camera blindspot)
  2. 2. Disable elevator security (L1/LT near keypad)
  3. 3. Take elevator to Floor 47
  4. 4. Infiltrate server room — download data (45 seconds)
  5. 5. Security triggered — silent takedowns only until vault
  6. 6. Open vault — $500K in gold bars
  7. 7. Rooftop extraction via helicopter (Boru provides)
  8. 8. Choice point: Save Jason or leave him (determines ending)

📍 Complete Walkthrough (Aggressive)

  1. 1. Armored Kuruma rams lobby entrance
  2. 2. Fight through 20+ guards (checkpoints every 10 floors)
  3. 3. Elevator fight — use grenades at elevator doors
  4. 4. Floor 47: Protect Jason during vault cracking
  5. 5. Vercotti himself appears — boss fight
  6. 6. Escape via glass ceiling helicopter
  7. 7. Police pursuit — lose at Starfish Island

🔮 Ending Implications

Your choice at the rooftop determines the ending. See the Main Guide for full ending details. The aggressive approach is required for the "Ride or Die" ending, but requires high trust level with Jason throughout the game.

5. Crew Management

Hiring the right crew maximizes your take. Each crew member has a base cost and a performance bonus. Better crew = higher success rate = higher take. >

Boru (Inside Man) Cost: $0 • Bonus: Access to restricted areas

Required for Heists 2 and 4. No alternative.

Kai (Driver) Cost: $2,000 • Bonus: Faster escape

Best driver in the game. Takes fewer risks.

Chen (Hacker) Cost: $5,000 • Bonus: +15% take

Expensive but worth it. Higher take + unlocks security bypass.

6. Pre-Heist Planning Tips

  • • Complete side missions before each heist — unlocks better crew and vehicles.
  • • Upgrade weapons at gun locker — suppressed weapons reduce alert time.
  • • Scout the location — reveals entry points, guard patterns, escape routes.
  • • Stockpile armor and snacks — especially for Heists 3 and 4.
  • • Save game before heist — allows replay with different approaches.

7. Common Fail Reasons

❌ Police Called (Early)

You triggered an alarm. Check your map — red circles = alerted guards. Wait for them to reset or take them down silently.

❌ Crew Down

Your crew died. In multiplayer, this is fail. In solo, you have 30 seconds to reach them. Revive or mission fails.

❌ Timer Expired

You took too long. On stealth runs, move efficiently. On aggressive runs, don't waste ammo on unnecessary enemies.

8. Replay for Maximum Take

After completing the game, you can replay heists from the Pause Menu → Replay Heists. Each heist can be replayed with different approaches.

Pro Tip: Replay Heist 2 (Port Precision) using the export exploit for easy money. Each replay with car exports = $95K in 15 minutes. Best money-making method in the game.
