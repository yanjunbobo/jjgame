🎮 Universal Combo System

Basic Combo Route:

  1. Ki Blast → Ki Blast → Ki Blast (3-hit starter)
  2. Rush Attack (hold square) → Ki Burst → Rush Attack
  3. Vanish Attack (after enemy breaks guard) → Ultimate
  4. Sparking Zero Mode → Instant Transmission Ultimate

Vanish Cancel Timing:

Vanish has a 5-frame window after being hit. If you're comboing: press vanish at frame 3-4 of the enemy's hit stun. If you're being comboed: tap vanish at frame 4. The window is 0.08 seconds — this is muscle memory, not reaction.

⚠️ The 5 Combo Mistakes

Mistake 1: Not Using Ki Control Mid-Combo
Between every 3-rush combo, tap ki blast to restore ki. Players who don't ki control run out of ki at 50% combo. Ki management = 2x longer combos.
Mistake 2: Using Ultimate Too Early
Ultimate does massive damage but locks you in animation for 2 seconds. If you use it at 30% combo and the enemy Sparks, you'll die. Save Ultimate for after Sparking Mode activates.
Mistake 3: Not Knowing Your Character's Infinite Combo
Every character has at least 1 infinite combo route. Goku (SSGSS) has: Rush ×3 → Ki → Rush ×3 → Vanish → Rush ×3. This deals 80% of health. Search your character's name + "infinite combo" on YouTube before ranked.
Mistake 4: Using Vanish on Wake-Up
If you're knocked down and the enemy is approaching, DON'T vanish immediately. Vanish on wake-up is predictable and punishable. Wait for the enemy to attack first, THEN vanish to counter.
Mistake 5: Holding Sparking Mode Too Long
Sparking Mode drains 10% per second. Holding it for "the right moment" = losing 40% of its duration. Activate Sparking Mode right before your combo starter, not during.
