🐉 All Characters & Transformations

Dragon Ball: Sparking Zero features 182 playable characters, the largest roster in the Budokai series. This guide covers every character's unique abilities, transformation mechanics, and optimal playstyles in 2026.

S-Tier Characters

Goku (Ultra Instinct): The definitive top-tier character. Ultra Instinct provides automatic evasion and 20% damage reduction. The master override mechanic enables rapid AI reaction to enemy attacks. The signature move deals 500+ damage in base form with full Ultimate meter.

Vegeta (Ultra Ego): The best aggressive character. Ultra Ego heals on attack rather than evasion. The damage boost when low HP makes him the best comeback character. The Final Flash Ultimate is among the highest damage outputs.

Broly (DBS): The best grappler. The 20-hit grab combo deals massive damage. The massive AoE Ultimate covers large areas. Weakness is the slow movement speed.

Transformation Mechanics

Transformations trigger automatically when Ultimate meter is full and HP drops below threshold. Goku transforms at 50% HP, Broly transforms at 25% HP. The transformation provides 30% stat boost plus unique abilities.

Manual transformation is faster but drains Ultimate meter. The timing of manual versus automatic determines fight outcomes in high-level play.
