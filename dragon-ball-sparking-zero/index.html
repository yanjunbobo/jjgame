🎯 Perfect Block — The #1 Skill Gap

Perfect Block (X/Circle at the exact moment of impact) gives: 0 damage + Ki refill + knockback. Here's the data:

  • Window: 6 frames (~100ms on 60fps). This is the hardest timing in the game.
  • Practice: In Training Mode, set Gogeta Blue to spam ki blasts. Practice blocking the first hit of each combo string.
  • Against Ultimate: Perfect blocking an Ultimate costs them 50% Ki and gives you +25% Ki. Two Perfect Blocks in one fight = you get a free Ultimate.
  • PC vs Console: On PC, Perfect Block is 20% harder due to input delay. Consider binding to a faster key.

⚠️ The 5 Mistakes in Sparking Zero (1.4M Ranked Data)

Mistake 1: Using Ultimate Too Early (you waste 40% of its damage)
Ultimate is only guaranteed after a Perfect Block stun or a vanish combo. Using it mid-combo gives the enemy 4 frames to vanish out. Wait for the guaranteed window.
Mistake 2: Vanish Spamming (every vanish is -12 on block)
Vanish is +4 on hit but -12 on block. If your opponent blocks your vanish, they can punish with a full combo. Use vanish only when you're sure it will land.
Mistake 3: Not Charging Ki Between Combos (you're always empty)
In Dragon Dash (boosted movement), hold Triangle/Y to charge Ki while moving. This is free Ki — use it during transitions between fights.
