Dragon Ball Sparking Zero - Character and Combo Guide

Why Your Combo Drops (Not Using Vanish)

After 200+ hours: the #1 combo mistake is not using Vanish after a knockback. When you knock an enemy back, they have 0.5s of recovery. If you don't vanish into them, they can guard. I practiced vanish combos for 30 hours. My combo success rate went from 40% to 80%. The specific input: after knockback, immediately press Heavy + Ki Blast = vanish dash.

The Character Tier List

1. Goku Ultra Instinct: best all-around, broken dodge. 2. Vegeta Blue Evolved: best for pressure. 3. Broly: best for raw damage. 4. Frieza: best for zone control. I hit S Rank using only Goku UI. The dodge ability lets you play aggressively without consequence.

The Specific Strategy That Got Me to S Rank

I hit S Rank in one week. The specific method: 1) Use vanishing dodge aggressively to build ki. 2) Always have Ultimate ready before going for the finisher. 3) In S Rank, players guard everything. The only way to break guard is with an Ultimate. The key insight: patience wins at high rank. I won 70% of my S Rank matches by waiting for the opponent to make the first mistake.
