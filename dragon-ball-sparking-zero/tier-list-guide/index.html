🐉 Character Tier List 2026

Sparking Zero's balance has evolved through multiple patches. This tier list reflects competitive performance and solo play effectiveness for all character archetypes in 2026.

S-Tier

Goku (UI) - Unmatched evasion and consistent damage. The auto-dodge negates projectile characters. A2-matchup favorability against most of the cast.

Vegeta (UE) - Best comeback potential. HP-based damage boost creates snowball scenarios. B1-mirror match requires precise resource management.

Broly (DBS) - Highest burst damage potential. The 20-hit grab combo is unavoidable. Slightly worse neutral than S-tiers but superior damage output.

Gogeta (SS4) - The best all-rounder with no meaningful weaknesses. The Brave Slash is plus on block. The Kamehameha provides corner carry. Slightly lower ceiling than UI Goku but more consistent.

A-Tier

Jiren - Exceptional damage with mediocre mixups. The Power Impact is plus on block. Weakness is the lack of true 50/50 pressure.

C17 (DB Super) - Strong neutral with barrier utility. The barrier cancels on block making him plus indefinitely. The Energy Cage ultimate is the best zoning tool in the game.

Goku (SSB) - The best neutral tool in the game. Kamehameha creates safe pressure. Weakness is the transformation timing requirement.
