⚠️ The 5 Team Building Mistakes

Mistake 1: Building 3 DPS Characters
No healer = your team dies in 10 seconds. No buffer = your DPS does 40% less damage. The correct formula is 1 DPS + 1 Support + 1 Flex. Never 3 DPS.
Mistake 2: Not Matching Element Types
Honkai Star Rail has a 30% elemental damage bonus when 3 team members share the same element. A mono-element team gets +30% damage vs +15% mixed. Always prioritize element matching.
Mistake 3: Investing in Characters Who Get Vaulted
Some characters are limited-time only. If a character hasn't appeared in 6 months, assume they're vaulted. Don't invest your rare resources in vaulted characters — wait for their re-run first.
Mistake 4: Ignoring the Free Character From Story Mode
Story mode characters are often unexpectedly strong and receive extra story-related buffs. The free story character is usually viable to at least A-tier and costs nothing to build.
Mistake 5: Building a Team Before You Have All Characters
Level all potential team members to level 40 minimum BEFORE investing in any one character. This way you can switch if you pull a better character. Fully investing in one character before exploring alternatives = wasted resources.
