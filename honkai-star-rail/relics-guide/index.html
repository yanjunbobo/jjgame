🚀 Best Relic Sets Per Character

Honkai: Star Rail's relic system is the primary source of character power. This guide covers the optimal relic sets for every major character archetype, saving you weeks of farming in 2026.

DPS Relic Sets

Firefly - Passerby of Wandering Cloud (4pc) + Glamoth (2pc): The definitive Firefly set. Passerby 4pc provides 16% Break Effect, increasing to 32% when HP is full. Firefly's healing mechanics keep her at full HP consistently. Glamoth provides additional SPD and Attack bonuses based on enemy count.

Jingliu - Pioneer Diver of Dead Waters (4pc) + Inert Salsotto (2pc): Pioneer Diver 4pc increases damage against slowed enemies. Since Jingliu applies Freeze through her skill, Pioneer Diver consistently triggers. Inert Salsotto provides the standard 8% Crit Rate for non-Quantum characters.

Acheron - Prisoner in Deep Confinement (4pc) + Duran (2pc): Prisoner 2pc provides 15% Lightning Damage, 4pc increases DoT damage and applies a stacking SPD debuff. Combined with Acheron's Jiaoqiu, this creates maximum DoT pressure.

Black Swan - Passerby of Wandering Cloud (4pc) + Inert Salsotto (2pc): The standard Break Effect focus works for Black Swan's Arcane Pulse DoT. Her stack mechanic benefits from the sustained damage approach that Break Effect enables.

Support Relic Sets

Robin - Swoosh of the Survival (4pc) + Fleet of the Ageless (2pc): Swoosh 4pc provides ATK% to allies based on Robin's ATK stat. The 4pc triggers on follow-up attacks, synergizing perfectly with Robin's ultimate. Fleet provides additional ATK% and HP% for survivability.

Ruan Mei - Passerby of Wandering Cloud (4pc) + Forge of the Bound (2pc): The universal support set. Passerby 4pc provides Break Effect that scales Ruan Mei's teamwide Break Effect buff. Forge provides 15% Break Effect for additional scaling.

Sparkle - Messenger Traversing Hackerspace (4pc) + Sprightly Vonwacq (2pc): Messenger 4pc provides 12% SPD, enabling Sparkle to hit critical SPD thresholds. Sprightly provides additional SPD and ERR for ultimate uptime.

Stat Priority Guidelines

Body Piece: Crit Rate or Crit Damage for DPS. ATK% for supports. Effect Hit Rate for DoT characters.

Boots: SPD for everyone without exception. Speed is the most valuable stat in Honkai Star Rail.

Rope: ATK% for standard DPS. Break Effect for Break-focused characters. Energy Regeneration for supports who need faster ultimates.

Sphere: Elemental DMG% for DPS. ATK% for supports without specific elemental scaling.

Sub-Stat Farming Priority

SPD boots mean you only need 2 substats of SPD minimum. Focus primary stats on the body/boots/rope/sphere slots, then maximize substats on everything else. Crit Rate > Crit DMG > ATK% > SPD for substats on DPS. SPD > ATK% > Break Effect for substats on supports.

The cavern ofcorrosion is more efficient than substats on accessories. Always prioritize getting the correct main stat on relics before worrying about sub-stat rolls.
