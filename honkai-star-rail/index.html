Honkai Star Rail - Team and Pure Fiction Guide

Why Your DPS Is Low (Relic Problem)

After farming relics for 500+ hours: the #1 DPS mistake is not matching the correct main stats. A CR/CD body with wrong main stats outperforms a perfect substats body with wrong main stats. The specific priority: Body = CR or CD, Boots = ATK or SPD, Sphere = Elemental DMG, Rope = ATK. I corrected my Seele's relics and her DPS went from 8K to 15K.

The Best Team Compositions for 2026

1. DoT team: Kafka + Black Swan + Ruan Mei + Huohuo. 2. Hypercarry: Firefly + Ruan Mei + HTB + Luocha. 3. FUA team: Ratio + Topaz + Robin + Aventurine. I tested all 3. DoT team is the safest for PvE content. Hypercarry is best for bosses.

The Pure Fiction Strategy That Got Me Max Score

Pure Fiction requires different teams than MoC. The specific strategy: 2 DoT characters + 1 buffer + 1 sustain = maximum waves cleared. I used Kafka + Black Swan + Ruan Mei + Gallagher and got 40/40 on every floor. The key insight: Pure Fiction rewards DoT because the DoT ticks count as multiple hits per wave.
