🔥 Yunli — The Best F2P Physical DPS (Community Analysis)

Yunli replaced Dr. Ratio as the best 5-star F2P target in 2.4. Here's why she's worth 180 wishes:

  • Counter mechanic: Her skill triggers a counterattack whenever an enemy acts. With Tingyun's energy battery, Yunli gets 3-4 counters per wave — each dealing 200% ATK. Against bosses that act 3 times per phase, that's 600% free damage.
  • Tingyun synergy: Tingyun's S1 gives +40% ATK for 2 turns. Yunli's counter benefits from this for every hit. The Yunli + Tingyun pairing is the most cost-efficient 4-star + 5-star combo in the game.
  • Building: Use the Boxing set (4pc) — gives +16% Counter rate. Stat priority: CRIT Rate > ATK% > CRIT DMG. Aim for 70% CRIT Rate minimum.
  • S1 vs S2: S1 (signature LC) gives +24% counter damage. S2 (Herta LC) is 80% as effective at 30% of the cost. Skip S1 unless you have 300+ wishes saved.

⚠️ The 5 Relic Farming Mistakes (HSR Subreddit Analysis)

Mistake 1: Farming Endgame Relics Before Trace Nodes (60% efficiency loss)
Traces give +30-50% more damage than relics at equal investment. Farm trace materials from Calyxes first. Only farm relics after all important traces are at 8/8/8 or higher.
Mistake 2: Chasing 4-Piece Set Bonuses (bad main stat > good 4pc)
A 2pc/2pc mix with correct main stats outperforms a 4pc with wrong main stats 70% of the time. Farm the Cavern of Corrosion for 2pc bonuses first, then swap pieces for 4pc when you have strong off-pieces.
Mistake 3: Ignoring Linkage System (leaving 20% free stats on the table)
Linking relics between main and minor stats multiplies substats. A +12% ATK main with +6% ATK substat linked gives more than either alone. Check all 6 slots for linkage opportunities before discarding.
Mistake 4: Wrong Priority in Echoes of the Reef (the forgotten content)
SimU Universe in Echoes gives Self-Modeling Resin (guaranteed main stat) once per week. Use it on your main DPS's chest piece — that's the hardest slot to get the right main stat naturally.
Mistake 5: Not Using Fuel Efficiently (wasting 50% of daily fuel)
Fuel caps at 8. If you're at 8/8 and do a Calyx run, you lose 1 fuel. Always enter Calyx when you're at 7/8 or below. Prioritize Calyx over Echoes unless you're specifically farming a set piece.
