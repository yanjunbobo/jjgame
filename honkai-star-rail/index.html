🚀 Beginner Guide & Reroll Tips

Honkai: Star Rail is a turn-based gacha RPG with complex team building and progression systems. This beginner guide covers the essential knowledge needed to maximize your early-game efficiency in 2026.

Early Game Priorities

The first 20 hours focus on story progression and character leveling. Do not spend resources on characters you won't use long-term. Every material used on a character you replace is wasted. Research before you invest.

Calyx (Cavern) Farming: Start farming relics only when your characters are max level for their current ascension. Relic farming is the longest grind—don't rush it. Focus on the planars first as they're shared across characters.

Trailblaze Power: The stamina system is the primary gate. Every piece of content costs Trailblaze Power. Prioritize daily任务 and weekly bosses. Use spare power on Calyx for ascension materials.

Reroll Recommendations

Reroll for a 5-star Harmony character (Ruan Mei, Robin, Sparkle) in your first 50 pulls. These supports enable any DPS character to clear content. 5-star DPS characters are replaceable—5-star supports define team archetypes.

The best reroll target is Ruan Mei for Break teams or Robin for hypercarry teams. Both are universally useful regardless of your eventual DPS choice. Robin's party-wide damage buff is the most impactful support ability in the game.

Character Investment Priority

Tier 1: 5-star Harmony supports. Level to 80 immediately. These characters remain relevant in every team you build. Their contribution scales with your entire team's damage output.

Tier 2: Your primary DPS. Level to 80 and max traces. This character carries your damage through all content. Their ascension phase determines your team's peak performance.

Tier 3: Secondary DPS and sustains. Level to 70-75. These characters fill team slots but don't need full investment. Sustain characters (tanks and healers) only need enough stats to survive.

Trailblaze Tips

Always auto-play story content on the first clear to unlock it for manual replay. Story gives excellent early resources and world EXP. Don't skip the weekly UE gift code distribution for free currencies.
