🚀 F2P Progression & Version 3.0 Update

Honkai Star Rail version 3.0 introduces new characters and mechanics. This guide covers the optimal F2P progression path, version 3.0 changes, and how to maximize jades as a free-to-play player in 2026.

F2P Monthly Income

Expected Jades: 70-80 pulls per patch as F2P. This includes: daily tasks (60/stamina day), Abundance bonuses (weekly), simulated universe, memory of chaos, events, and maintenance compensation.

Monthly Pass: The $5 monthly pass is the best value purchase. It pays for itself through the additional jades alone. The self-modeling resin is valuable for relic farming.

Battle Pass: The $10 battle pass provides meaningful progression acceleration. The additional trace materials reduce farming time by 2-3 weeks per character.

Version 3.0 Character Priority

Fugue: The new Break-focused nihility character. For F2P, she's a skip unless you already have Ruan Mei. The Break team requires all three pieces (Ruan Mei + Fugue + Firefly) for maximum effectiveness.

Mydei: The Destruction fighter with self-healing. His solo damage is competitive with Firefly in single-target scenarios. He's a solid F2P investment if you lack a strong single-target DPS.

Relic Farming Efficiency

The 80-resin cavern cost means you should only farm relics for characters at max level. Never farm relics before ascending your characters to max current level. The character level increase provides more damage than any relic upgrade.

Target Planar Ornament sets first—these are shared across characters and have 100% drop rates for the correct set. Leave character-specific relic farming for when you have surplus trailblaze power after building your core team.
