🎯 What Is Reroll and Why It Matters

Rerolling means starting a new account to get better starting characters. In Honkai Star Rail, your first 50-100 pulls determine whether you can compete in competitive modes. A bad start = 2-3 weeks of catch-up. A great start = immediate meta team.

⚡ How to Reroll in 10 Minutes (Fast Method)

  1. Download the game and complete the tutorial (skip all dialogue)
  2. Claim all starting codes — search "Honkai Star Rail codes 2026" for active redemption codes worth 10-20 free pulls
  3. Complete first 3 missions — takes 5-8 minutes, unlocks the gacha system
  4. Spend all F2P currency on the current limited banner — limited banners have 2x the value
  5. If you get a top-tier character (S-tier or 5-star), bind the account and keep it
  6. If you don't get a top-tier in 30 pulls, delete the account data and start over from Step 1

⚠️ The 5 Reroll Mistakes That Waste Your Time

Mistake 1: Rerolling on the Standard Banner
Standard banner has 0.3% S-tier rate. Limited banners have pity at 90 pulls AND bonus 50% rate-up. Always reroll on limited banners only.
Mistake 2: Not Using Beginner Banner First
Most gacha games give a 50-pull beginner banner with 50% reduced pity. Always do beginner banner first — it shares pity with the limited banner.
Mistake 3: Binding Account Before Getting a S-Tier
Once you bind to email/Google, you can't delete the account. Keep the account unbound until you get your target character.
Mistake 4: Rerolling for the Wrong Character
Some S-tiers are only good in specific teams. If your reroll target requires 2 other limited characters to work, it's not worth chasing on a fresh account. Pick a character that works in solo/f2p teams.
Mistake 5: Spending Money on Reroll Accounts
Rerolling costs nothing but time. Spending $5-10 to reroll faster defeats the purpose. The moment you pay to reroll, you've lost the F2P advantage.

💡 Pro Tips for Day 1

  • Save ALL first-week currency — don't pull on anything except your main character's banner
  • Join the official Discord — they give free currency codes worth 5-20 pulls monthly
  • Don't level everyone evenly — focus 100% of resources on your rerolled S-tier for the first 2 weeks
