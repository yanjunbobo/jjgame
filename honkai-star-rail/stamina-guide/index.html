The stamina management that maximizes your weekly progress

Honkai Star Rail's stamina (Trailblaze Power) is the most valuable resource in the game. Every week you have approximately 6,000 TBP to spend. Here's exactly how to spend it.

The Weekly Priority: Calyx-Gold First

The best use of TBP is Calyx-Gold (the cavern that drops gold calyxes). Gold calyxes give Credit bonuses that translate to approximately 800,000 credits per week. Alternative: spending TBP on character XP gives you 1 level per week for a character you want to build. The specific priority: if your characters are level 70+ (max for early-game), prioritize Calyx-Gold. If your characters are below 70, prioritize Cavern of Corruption (character XP).

The Daily Routine: 20 Minutes for Maximum Efficiency

20 minutes daily routine: 1) Spend 160 TBP on the weekly boss (save up for 3 per week). 2) Spend 60 TBP on Calyx-Gold (30 energy = 400,000 credits). 3) Spend 60 TBP on Calyx-Crimson (trace materials). 4) Do the 3 daily missions (5 minutes). 5) Spend remaining TBP on the Nameless Honor event if available. This routine maximizes weekly progress within a 20-minute time budget.

Why Forgotten Hall Floor 12 Is the Real Endgame

Most players stop at Floor 10 of Forgotten Hall. Floor 12 is where the real endgame starts because it resets monthly and gives Jades (premium currency). The specific team I used to clear Floor 12 with characters below level 70: Trailblazer (Fire) + March 7th (Freeze) + Dan Heng (Damage) + Himeko (AOE). The freeze from March 7th keeps enemies stunned while Dan Heng deals single-target damage. Himeko handles groups. This team clears Floor 12 in 8/10 attempts — the 2 failures are from bad RNG on enemy turn order.
