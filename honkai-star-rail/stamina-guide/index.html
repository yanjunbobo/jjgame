Stamina / Stamina Guide — Daily Routine & Endgame Farming | 2026

"Honkai Star Rail stamina" has 920K monthly searches. HSR uses "Stamina" (also called "Trailblaze Power") as its resource gating system. This guide covers optimal daily routine and how to maximize every stamina point.

📊 Stamina System

  • Stamina cap: 180 (F2P), 240 (Monthly Pass), 300 (Battle Pass)
  • Regeneration: 1 Stamina per 6 minutes (180 minutes = full recharge)
  • Stamina overflow: Cannot exceed cap — must spend daily

⏰ Optimal Daily Routine

Morning: Spend 120 Stamina on Calyx (Best stat gains). Spend 60 on world quests.

Weekend: Spend all Stamina on Calyx-Golden for Relic enhancement mats.

Events: Check event tab — events often give 200-400 free Stamina equivalent rewards.

⚠️ The 3 Stamina Mistakes

Mistake 1: Not Using Stamina Every Day
Stamina caps at 180 and stops regenerating. If you're full at midnight, you lose all regeneration until you spend some. Spend Stamina every day — even if it's just 60 on world quests.
Mistake 2: Farming Relics Before TB 60
Relic farming before Trailblaze Level 60 gives 50% worse drops. Focus on Calyx (character materials) until TB 60. Relics are endgame — don't waste Stamina on them early.
Mistake 3: Skipping Echo of War
Echo of War gives double rewards weekly. Each boss gives 2x ascension materials. Always do Echo of War every week — it costs 60 Stamina and saves you 120 Stamina equivalent.
