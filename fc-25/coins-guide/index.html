FC 25 Coin Making Guide — Earn 100K+ Coins Per Week F2P | 2026

The gap between a free player and a paying player in FC 25 Ultimate Team grows every year. But with the right methods, a free player can build an 85+ squad within 6 weeks and compete in Gold 2 in Weekend League. Here's the F2P coin-making system.

💰 The F2P Coin Economy

Weekly F2P Coin Income Sources:

  • Squad Battles (40-50 matches): 50-80K coins per week. Position yourself in Elite 3 (top 500) for maximum rewards. The difference between Elite 3 and Gold 1 = 30K coins per week.
  • Rivals (20 matches): 15-25K coins per week. Focus on getting 7 wins (FUT Champions qualification) for the bonus pack.
  • SBC Reward Packs: When you complete SBCs, the packs you don't open can be sold. Even common players in pack rewards are worth 500-2,000 coins.
  • Transfer Market Sniping: Bid on 68-72 rated players at 400 coins. Sell at 800-1,200. Requires 30 minutes/day but yields 20-50K per week.

Total F2P weekly income: 100-200K coins = one 85+ player every 2 weeks, or one 87 player every month.

📊 The Sniping Method (Detailed)

Sniping on the Transfer Market is the highest ROI activity for F2P players. But it requires a system — random sniping yields almost nothing.

  1. Find the "snipe tier": Players rated 72-75 sell for 400-800 coins but are bid at 200-400. These are the snipe targets.
  2. Set filters: Chemistry style: N/A. Nation: Popular (Brazil, France, Spain). League: Premier League, LaLiga. Position: ST, CAM, CM. Max price: 400 coins.
  3. The timing: Sniping is best during: Tuesday 6pm-8pm (when SBCs drop and demand spikes), Thursday 7pm-9pm (after TOTW leaks), Sunday 2pm-5pm (before WL starts).
  4. The bid strategy: Bid at 350 coins on 72+ players. If you get undercut, immediately relist at the new undercut price. Never bid above 450 coins — above that price, the margin is too thin.

Result: 20-50 snipes per hour, 10-20% win rate = 2-10 profitable flips per hour = 2,000-10,000 coins per hour. 2 hours of sniping per week = 5,000-20,000 coins extra.

⚠️ The 5 Coin-Making Mistakes

Mistake 1: Opening Packs Instead of Selling Them
Pack odds in FC 25: 1 in 10 packs has a player worth more than 15K. The other 9 packs = 5-20K in player value vs selling the pack for 8-12K. If you're not a luck-based player, selling packs unopened is +20% expected value.
Mistake 2: Doing SBCs with Valuable Players
SBCs often require specific player ratings. If you put an 85 player worth 30K into an SBC that gives you a pack worth 15K, you've lost 15K. Always check the expected value of the SBC pack before putting in high-value players. Most SBCs have a negative expected value — only do them for fodder players.
Mistake 3: Not Playing Squad Battles in the First Place
Many players ignore Squad Battles because they're "boring." This is a mistake. Elite 3 Squad Battles rewards = 80K coins + packs. That's 2/3 of an 85+ player every week for 2 hours of play. If you only play Rivals, you're leaving 60K coins on the table.
Mistake 4: Panic Selling After TOTW
TOTW (Team of the Week) drops on Wednesday and crashes the market for 24 hours. If you sell your players before checking TOTW, you sell at the bottom. Wait until Thursday morning to sell — prices recover by 30% within 24 hours of TOTW.
