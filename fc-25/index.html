⚽ Advanced Tactics & Set Piece Guide

EA Sports FC 25's advanced tactics system controls team behavior beyond basic formation. This guide covers set piece strategies, player instructions, and the specific tactical tweaks that separate Elite Division players from everyone else in 2026.

Advanced Tactical Instructions

Defensive Width 35-40: Creates a narrow defensive block that clogs the center. Use this against 4-3-3 formations that attack through the middle. Counter with wide formations against teams using this.

Defensive Depth 65-75: Triggers aggressive pressing from your forwards. Use this when you're confident in your defender's pace. Risk: vulnerable to long ball over the top.

Build Up Play - Long Ball: Bypasses midfield entirely. Use against teams with high midfield presence. Risk: isolated striker becomes easy to defend.

Chance Creation - Direct: Maximum pace from transition to attack. Use with fast strikers against slow defenders. Risk: high turnover rate in midfield.

Set Piece Strategies

Corner Kicks: Set to "Short" as default. Take 2-3 short corners to draw defenders forward, then whip it to the back post. The "Low Cross" near post run is the highest conversion opportunity.

Free Kicks: Position your best shooter at 25-30 yards. For inside the box, use "Low Driven" with a power bar 70% shot. Always have a second attacker making a dummy run.

Penalty Kicks: Aim for the lower corners at 70% power. The top corner is harder to save but easier to miss. Stutter-step before the kick is now detectable—use caution.

Player Instructions Deep Dive

Set your CDM to "Stay Back While Attacking" and "Cut Passing Lanes." This creates a defensive anchor that prevents counter-attacks while your fullbacks push forward.

Set your fastest winger to "Get in Behind" and "Conservative Interceptions." The conservative interceptions prevent them from overcommitting and leaving space behind.
