1. How to Join Coop

🌐 In-Game Matchmaking

  1. 1. Open the Embernole Chalice at any rest point (bonfire equivalent)
  2. 2. Select "Form Party" to create a session, or "Join Session" to search
  3. 3. Set your session to Public so others can join. Private parties are invite-only.
  4. 4. Wait for 2-4 players. The host starts when ready.

💬 Discord & External LFG

The in-game matchmaking is unreliable. Use the Elden Ring Nightreign Discord (#coop-lfg channel) or r/Nightreign for consistent partners.

Pro Tip: Form a fixed 4-stack for Cycles 5+. Random matchmaking falls apart at this difficulty.

3. Boss Fight Coordination

The biggest mistake in coop: everyone attacking the boss at once. In Nightreign, aggro is shared. If all 4 players stand in front of the boss, the boss telegraphs attacks randomly and you get one-shot.

🛡️ Tank (Ironside) — "I have aggro"

Ironside uses Vow of the Bear immediately when the boss appears. The boss locks onto Ironside. Everyone else attacks from behind or sides.

🗡️ DPS (Iga) — "Attacking from behind"

Iga stays behind the boss. Shadow Dance crits deal double damage from behind. Call out when you're about to use Shadow Dance so Wylder doesn't knock the boss over.

🔥 AOE (Wylder) — "Adds are mine"

Any adds or minions spawned during the boss fight are 100% Wylder's job. Fire Tornado + Flame Spit keeps adds stunlocked. Never let adds touch the tank.

🏹 Ranger (Recluse) — "Stagger timing"

Recluse watches for boss wind-up animations. When the boss raises a weapon for a big attack, call "STAGGER" and hit with Charged Shot. 3 staggers = boss stunned for 5 seconds = free DPS window.

4. Communication Protocol

Voice chat is essential for bosses. Use these callouts:

"BUFF UP"

Use before boss doors. Everyone pops their damage relics/buffs.

"TAUNT NOW"

Ironside pops Vow of the Bear. Everyone get into position.

"STAGGER"

Recluse sees wind-up. All DPS hold attacks for 2 seconds.

"BOMB" or "ARTS"

Iga is using Shadow Dance. Don't knock enemies over.

"HEAL" / "DIE"

Someone is down. Ironside taunts away from the body. Wylder picks up.

"PUSH"

Final phase. All relics active, go all-in.

5. Summoning Signs (Solo to Coop)

If you're playing solo and want help, use Summoning Signs (orange glowing runes on the ground).

  1. 1. Find a rest point (bonfire)
  2. 2. Use Coop Fountain item (craftable, costs 10 Crystal Shards)
  3. 3. Your sign appears in other players' worlds for 5 minutes
  4. 4. You get a notification when someone summons you

Best places to put your sign: Right before boss fog gates. Before hard mob rooms. Near shortcuts.
