⚠️ Common Mistake: Wrong Stance

Most players spam Fleeting Shadows and ignore the Shadow Dance stance. When you hold L2 (stance button), Iga enters a counter-stance. Perfect dodge during Shadow Dance = instant crit on all enemies in range. This is what separates good Igas from great ones.

3. All Boss Strategies

🗡️

Nightlord (Tutorial Boss)

Cycle 1-8 • Weakness: Lightning

Teaching boss. Telegraphed attacks, slow combos. Watch for the overhead slash — deflect it for free damage.

Strategy: Stay at medium range. Punish recovery frames. Iga can solo in under 60 seconds.
🐍

Corruption Serpent

Cycle 2+ • Weakness: Fire

First real challenge. The tail sweep → poison breath combo is deadly. Always dodge LEFT — right side has tighter timing.

Strategy: Wylder should keep Fire Tornado up during phase 2 when it flies. Boss stays grounded longer = more burn ticks.
👹

Infernoble Champion

Cycle 3+ • Weakness: Holy

Two-phase boss. Phase 1 is a standard knight. Phase 2 summons two shadow clones. This is a run-ender if you don't coordinate.

Strategy: Coop: Ironside taunts the clones. Solo: Save your burst for phase 2 entry. Iga's Shadow Dance can kill both clones in one window.
🔥

Nightmare Incarnate (Final Boss)

Cycle 5+ • Weakness: Varies by phase

Three phases. Phase 1: Sword. Phase 2: Magic. Phase 3: True Form. This boss tests your entire loadout.

Phase 1 (Sword)
Lightning → dodge right
Phase 2 (Magic)
Fire → stay mobile, don't tank
Phase 3 (True)
Holy → interrupt the beam

⚠️ No-Summon Challenge: Ironside's Vow of the Bear taunt breaks Phase 3's lock-on beam. If solo, you need to iframe through the beam with perfect timing.

3.

5. All Endings

🌅 Ending 1: Night's Dawn

Defeat Nightmare Incarnate without using any Terrified Relics. The natural ending. 66% of players get this.

💀 Ending 2: Become the Nightmare

Use 5+ Terrified Relics during the final fight. You absorb the Nightmare's power but lose yourself. Dark ending. 24% of players.

🌀 Ending 3: The Cycle Continues

Collect all 8 Remembrance Relics and offer them at the Altar of the Eclipse before the final boss. Secret true ending. 10% of players.

6. Relic Tier List

S: Moonveil Signet

Best in slot for Iga and Recluse. Always pick.

S: Ashen Remains

Wylder's Fire Tornado lasts 8 seconds longer. Game-breaking AOE.

A: Vow of the Bear

Taunt all enemies for 6 seconds. Essential for boss phases.

A: Charged Arrowheads

Recluse's charged shot staggers bosses. 3 staggers = stun window.

B: Thornweaver's Mark

Poison spreads to nearby enemies. Mediocre without Thornweaver class.

AVOID: Terrified Relics

High risk, marginal reward. Only use if going for Ending 2.
