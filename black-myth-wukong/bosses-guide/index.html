🐒 All Bosses & Phase Strategies

Black Myth: Wukong presents 6 major bosses and numerous elite enemies across its campaign. This guide covers every boss phase, attack patterns, and optimal strategies to overcome each encounter in 2026.

Chapter 1 - Chapter 3 Bosses

Chapter 1 Boss - The Wandering Wight: This introductory boss teaches core combat mechanics. Phase 1 involves basic melee exchanges—dodge left to avoid the horizontal sweep. Phase 2 introduces spirit projectiles that require strafing. The key is patience: wait for the overhead slam, dodge through, and punish with a full spell combo.

Chapter 2 Boss - The Macaque Chief: This boss has three distinct phases. Phase 1 features ranged spirit attacks—use cloud step to close distance immediately. Phase 2 introduces clone summons—focus the real Macaque while avoiding the clones. Phase 3 combines both with faster attack strings. Save your gourd for Phase 3 healing.

Chapter 3 Boss - The Wolf Father: The fastest boss in early game. His leap attack has a long windup but massive tracking. Wait for the ground pound, roll backward, then punish the recovery. His Phase 2 adds ice projectiles—stay mobile between attacks. Rock Spirit transformation trivialize this fight.

Chapter 4 - Final Boss

Chapter 4 Boss - The Yellow Silk: Fire-based boss with area denial. The arena fills with flame pillars in Phase 2—position near edges to maximize safe space. His overhead hammer is a free hit opportunity. Heavy staff stance attacks deal massive spirit damage to his large health pool.

Chapter 5 Boss - The Bull King: Multi-phase boss with mounting mechanics. Phase 1 is standard melee combat with heavy hitboxes. Phase 2 adds a charge attack that fills the arena—run perpendicular. Phase 3 introduces a motorcycle sequence that requires perfect dodges. The Immobilize staff combo is essential here.

Final Boss - Erlang Shen: The ultimate test of player skill. Seven distinct phases with minimal healing windows. Phase 1-2 are melee focused—learn attack windows of 3-4 hit combos. Phase 3-4 add ranged celestial attacks—use cloud step aggressively. Phase 5-7 are pure execution tests with Erlang's celestial forms. The Destined Ruyi Reborn transformation is the intended carry for this fight.

Boss Preparation

Before each boss, upgrade your staff at the blacksmith. Max out your health gourd charges. Carry at least 3 spirit pills for emergency poise recovery. The Spirit Affinity system means certain transformations deal bonus damage—check which spirit matches the boss element.

The Cloud Step ability is your most important tool for boss evasions. Master the double-cloud for i-frames during fast attack strings. Spell combos deal significantly more damage than auto-attacks—always open with a bound spirit attack followed by heavy staff combos.
