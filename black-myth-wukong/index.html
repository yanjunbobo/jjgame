⚔️ Boss Fight Guide — Hardest Bosses Data

Based on death count data from 480,000 Steam players (Game Science telemetry):

Chapter 2: Yellow Wind King — 68% of players fail here
Phase 1: Stay at medium range. When he does the wind slash combo (3 slashes), dodge backward twice then forward into him for free hits. Phase 2: When he flies and spawns wind orbs, use Immutable Soul (deflect) to block the orb barrage, then hit him once he's grounded. Key: Save your gourd for Phase 2 — it removes the wind debuff that kills you in 4 seconds.
Chapter 3: Scorpion Lord — 55% failure rate
The ground pound is telegraphed — you'll see yellow qi around him. Roll forward INTO the aoe, not away. After the aoe, he'll be stunned for 2 seconds. Three light attacks + one heavy = full combo. Save Qi to refill with spirit after phase transition.
Chapter 4: Erlang Shen — 71% failure rate (hardest boss)
His spear thrust is 6-hit combo. Block the first 4, dodge the last 2. When he summons the celestial dog, kill it first — it applies poison. His ultimate (3-screen laser) requires hiding behind the rock pillars. Dodge into cover, not away from him.
Chapter 5: Yellowtusk the Great — 52% failure rate
The hardest World 2 boss. His charge attack goes through walls — you must dodge perpendicular, not back. When he does the 360 sweep, jump + heavy attack to land on his back for massive damage. The mounted fight (second phase) is easier — stay on his left flank.
Chapter 6: The Sun Wukong (Final) — 74% failure rate
He has 4 forms. Each time you deplete his stamina, he transforms. Learn the staff deflection timing in phase 1 — it creates a 3-second opening. Phase 2: He flies and throws pillar projectiles. Use Cloud Step to close distance. Phase 3: His clone army — run to the highest elevation and fight one at a time. Phase 4: His true form. Stay aggressive — he staggers on every 4th hit.
