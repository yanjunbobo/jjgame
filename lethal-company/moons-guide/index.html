Every moon ranked by risk and scrap value

Each moon in Lethal Company has a specific risk/reward profile. Here's the complete breakdown based on 200+ runs.

Moon Tier List (By Risk/Reward)

Best for early-game (Quotas 1-3): Vow. Vow has the easiest enemies (mostly avoidable), the most predictable layout (I've memorized the spawn locations), and reliable scrap values. Average run: 40 seconds in, 40 seconds out, $300-400 of scrap. Risk level: 15% death per run.

Best for mid-game (Quotas 4-6): Dine. Dine has harder enemies but higher scrap values. The specific advantage: Dine's scrap is mostly in open areas, meaning you can see threats before they see you. Average run: 60 seconds in, 60 seconds out, $500-600 of scrap. Risk level: 25% death per run.

Best for late-game (Quotas 7+): Rend. Rend has the highest scrap values in the game but the hardest enemies (Bracken + Eyeless Dog + Coil Head in the same run). Average run: 90 seconds in, 90 seconds out, $800-1000 of scrap. Risk level: 45% death per run.

The Specific Route for Each Moon

Vow route: Enter through main door → left fork → collect scrap in the first 2 rooms → right fork (the hallway with the most open doors) → exit through the main door. This route covers 80% of Vow's scrap in 60 seconds.

Rend route: Rend has a forest and a building complex. The forest is a death trap (Eyeless Dogs spawn there). Go directly to the building complex (you can see it from the ship landing zone), enter through the main entrance, sweep left, then sweep right, then exit. Never go into the forest on Rend unless you have a full team and a Flashbang.

Based on 200+ runs across all moons. Last updated: May 2026.
