⚠️ The 5 Moon Mistakes

Mistake 1: Doing Offense Before Level 20
Offense has 3x the enemy density of other moons. Before level 20, your equipment is too weak to handle Eyeless Dog packs + Bracken + Nutcracker all at once. You will die repeatedly and lose more money than you make.
Mistake 2: Not Rotating Moons Based on Quota
If your quota is $500 and you're at $400, go to Vow (safe, $400-500). If your quota is $1,000 and you're at $700, go to Assurance. Always match moon selection to your quota gap.
Mistake 3: Going Alone to High-Difficulty Moons
Offense and March require minimum 2 players. Solo = you handle all enemies while also looting. 2-player minimum, 4-player optimal for anything above Vow.
Mistake 4: Staying Past 3am (Inside)
At 3am inside the facility, enemies start spawning in packs of 3-5 instead of 1-2. The profit increase from staying past 3am is $50-100. The death risk is 10x higher. Leave at 2:50am.
Mistake 5: Not Checking Route Before Going
Every moon has a known loot route. Watch one YouTube video before going to a new moon. Going in blind = 30% less loot and 50% higher death rate.
