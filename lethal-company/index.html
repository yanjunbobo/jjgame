🚀 Best Moons & Quota Strategies

Lethal Company's moon selection and route planning determine quota success more than combat skill. This guide covers the most profitable moons, route efficiency, and enemy avoidance strategies for consistent quota fulfillment in 2026.

Best Moons by Profit

Embrion: The highest profit-per-item moon. The route through the 4-ship area yields 800-1200 credits per run. The high enemy density requires experience and flash grenade discipline. Best done with 3-4 players for item transport.

Rine Orch: The safest high-profit moon. The 2-ship compound is easily memorized. The bracken spawns at the back of the map, making avoidance straightforward with route knowledge. 400-600 credits per run consistently.

Vow Colony: The beginner-friendly moon. The store is right of spawn, enabling quick purchases before exploring. The coil head spawns in predictable locations. 200-400 credits per run with basic loot.

Quota Strategy

The minimum quota in early moons is 300-400 credits. The strategy is to overfund by 200 credits per quota cycle to build a reserve. The reserve enables quota failures on difficult moons without game-ending consequences.

Invest in shovels and flashbangs before attempting difficult moons. The equipment cost is amortized over multiple successful runs. A lost run due to lack of equipment costs more than the equipment investment.
