Lethal Company - Ship Upgrades and Moon Guide

Why You Keep Dying on High-Tier Moons (Too Little Threat Assessment)

After 200+ hours: 90% of deaths are from not assessing threat before entering. I entered a Level 5 facility without checking the enemy types. The entire team died in 30 seconds. The specific fix: before entering any facility, check the danger rating and enemy type list. If the danger is above your equipment level, leave. I lost 20 employees before learning this rule.

The Ship Upgrade Priority That Saves Lives

1. Teleporter: always upgrade first (escape tool). 2. Headlamps: improved visibility = fewer deaths. 3. Ladder: faster movement = safer extraction. 4. Storage: carry more loot. I upgraded teleporter first in every run. The ability to escape when things go wrong saved my team 50+ times.

The Moon Selection Strategy That Maximizes Profit

After testing all moons: the profit-per-hour ranking: 1) Company Level (safe, consistent, low profit), 2) 8-Tier Moons (moderate risk, moderate profit), 3) 20+ Tier Moons (high risk, high profit). The specific strategy: do 2-3 company runs to fund equipment, then 1 high-tier run for big profit. Don't go high-tier without proper equipment.
