🚀 Moon-by-Moon Profit Guide & Enemy Handling

Lethal Company's profit optimization is more important than combat skill. This guide covers every moon's profit potential, specific route strategies, and how to handle every enemy type in 2026.

Moon Profit Rankings

Embrion (Tier 3): 800-1200 credits per run. The 4-ship area provides the highest density of high-value items. Bracken spawns in the back—the route should systematically clear forward, then extract before reaching the back. Requires flashbang discipline.

Rine Orch (Tier 3): 500-800 credits per run. The 2-ship compound is easily memorized. Bracken spawns at a fixed location—learn the trigger zone and clear it with a door slam before collecting nearby loot.

Artifice (Tier 4): 600-1000 credits per run. The open terrain provides excellent visibility but less loot density. The Jester appears in the facility—bring a backup weapon in case you get jester'd.

Vow Colony (Tier 1): 200-400 credits per run. The beginner moon. Land to the right of spawn for the store. The coil head is predictable—always check behind you when exiting the front door.

Enemy Handling

Bracken: Flashbang when he enters grab range. The grab animation has a 1-second windup—flashbang during windup to cancel. Never run in straight lines.

Coil Head: Stand completely still. The coil head will walk past you if you're not moving. Sprinting triggers its sprint.

Jester: Immediately stop moving and wait. The jester kill radius is 15 meters. If you're inside the radius when the music stops, you're dead.
