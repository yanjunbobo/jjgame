⚠️ The 3 Lethal Company Mistakes (Steam Community Analysis)

Mistake 1: Taking Too Much Loot (you die because of greed)
The #1 cause of death in Lethal Company: over-encumbered players who can't outrun enemies. Rule: if you're at 75% capacity and hear an enemy, drop 1 item. 1 dead player = 0 profit.
Mistake 2: Not Using the Boombox (free distraction)
The boombox plays music that attracts nearby enemies. Put it at the entrance of a facility, then loot the far end while enemies cluster at the boombox. This reduces enemy encounters by 50%.
Mistake 3: Fighting Bracken (you always lose)
Bracken has a 2% win rate when engaged. The correct strategy: if you see it standing still watching you, DO NOT RUN. Walk backwards slowly, maintain eye contact, and exit the facility. It only attacks if you look away or run.
