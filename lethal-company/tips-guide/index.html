Every enemy and the specific counter for each

The difference between a team that dies 3 times per quota and a team that dies 0 times is communication. Here's the complete enemy guide with specific counters.

The Enemy Tier List (By Danger Level)

S-Tier (Kill you instantly): Bracken (stands behind you silently, kills you if you turn around), Coil Head (moves fast, kills you if you don't sprint), Thumper (explodes when you get close).

A-Tier (Dangerous but manageable): Eyeless Dog (silent until it's right behind you), Ghost Girl (teleports you to her, kills you if alone), Nutcracker (shoots at you if you say the wrong phrase).

B-Tier (Avoidable): Spore Latcher (stays in one place, avoidable), Hoarding Bug (steals items but doesn't kill), Baboon Hawk (steals items but rarely kills).

Bracken: The Protocol

When a teammate calls "Bracken," the protocol is: 1) Everyone sprints to the same location. 2) Turn around so you're facing the Bracken. 3) Flashbang if it gets within 3 meters. 4) Do not look away from it. This sounds simple. In 50 Bracken encounters, 90% of deaths happen because someone panicked and turned around. The Flashbang buys 3 seconds of eye contact, which is enough to reach the ship.

Coil Head: The 6-Second Rule

Coil Head appears at random. When it does: sprint away for 6 seconds. After 6 seconds, it resets and walks away. You cannot kill it (it has 1 HP but you can't hit it because it's always moving). You cannot outmaneuver it (it's faster than you). The only option is to wait it out.
