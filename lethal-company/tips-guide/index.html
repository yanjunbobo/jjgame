⚠️ The 5 Mistakes That Kill Teams

Mistake 1: Splitting Up Before Day 5
Before day 5, enemies are manageable as a group. Splitting up before you have good equipment results in 1 player dying, then another trying to save them.
Mistake 2: Not Bringing a Flashlight (or keeping it off)
Darkness = Nutcracker, Bracken, and HoardingBug all have increased detection range. Always have your flashlight on in caves. 90% of early deaths are from not seeing enemies in time.
Mistake 3: Running When You Should Be Walking
Bracken detection: running = instant aggro at 30m. Walking = aggro at 5m. Eyeless Dog: you can hear it from 40m. When you hear it, WALK.
Mistake 4: Taking Too Much Loot
Your ship holds $700 maximum. If you're at $650 and see a $100 item, it's not worth the risk. Take only what fits in the quota + $100 buffer.
Mistake 5: Not Setting Ship Alarm as a Signal
When a player calls the ship, the alarm sounds. Use this as a "get out now" signal.
