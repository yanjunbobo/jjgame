🃏 How to Win & Ante System

Balatro is a poker-themed roguelike where you build scoring combos to beat escalating antes. This guide covers the core loop, ante difficulty scaling, and fundamental strategy for new players in 2026.

Core Game Loop

Each round, you play a poker hand from your deck of 8 cards. The hand's score determines whether you beat the blind's score requirement. Scoring formula: (Base Chips + Card Chips) × Mult = Total Score. Understanding this formula is the single most important skill in Balatro.

You have 4 discards per run, reset each round. Use discards to draw into better hands. A discard is not wasted if it improves your next hand's score by more than the lost opportunity of playing immediately.

Ante System

The game has 8 antes, each progressively harder. Each ante consists of 3 blinds: Small Blind, Big Blind, and Boss Blind. Beating a blind earns money for Joker purchases. Higher blinds give more money but require higher scores.

Small Blind (Ante 1-4): Score requirements range from 200 to 800. These are warm-up rounds where you establish your Joker synergies. Always play these to establish economy.

Big Blind (Ante 1-4): Score requirements range from 300 to 1,500. These are the standard difficulty that tests your deck's consistency. Build toward these requirements during Small Blind rounds.

Boss Blind (Every ante): Score requirements range from 400 to 3,000. Boss blinds have negative effects that change your strategy. The Dealt club boss forces you to play clubs. The No face cards boss removes court cards from play. Adapt your Joker choices to each boss.

Economy Management

Money is used for Joker purchases, vouchers, and hand upgrades. Never spend all your money on Jokers—always maintain 4+ dollars for emergency voucher purchases. The interest cap at 5 dollars means 20+ dollars is wasteful.

Prioritize vouchers over Jokers in the first two antes. The economy vouchers multiply your income for the entire run. The extra hand and discard vouchers enable more consistent scoring.

Win Condition

Beat all 8 antes to win the run. The final ante requires 2,500,000+ cumulative score across all three rounds. This requires strong Joker synergies and consistent hand execution. The final boss blind tests your ability to score under pressure.
