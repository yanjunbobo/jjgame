One Piece Treasure Cruise - Team and TM Guide

Why Your Team Is Weak (It's the Cursor, Not the Units)

In One Piece Treasure Cruise, the cursor order determines your damage output. The specific fix: always put your highest ATK unit last in the cursor order. The last unit in the chain gets the highest multiplier. I watched my damage jump 30% simply by reordering my cursor. This single change made me clear content I was stuck on for months.

The TM Strategy That Maximizes Rewards

Treasure Maps are the endgame. The optimal strategy: run 30-minute sessions, don't burn out. The first 3 days of TM are for points, the last 2 days are for ranking. I aimed for top 5% consistently and never burned out. The specific team for TM Boss: use a 1.75x booster as captain, 2x specials, speed run strategy.

The Sockets That Actually Matter

Most players socket randomly. The priority order: 1) AH (auto-heal) - keeps you alive, 2) AD (auto-fail) - prevents bad map tiles, 3) CC (chain multiplier) - increases damage output. I spent 3 months socket farming in the right order. My survival rate in Forest raids went from 40% to 85%.
