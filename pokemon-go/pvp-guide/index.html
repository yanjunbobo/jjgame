🏆 Great League Meta — Top 10 Picks (Pvpoke Rankings, May 2026)

#1 Azumarill (57.3% Win Rate)
The most consistent pick in GL. Bubble + Ice Beam + Play Rough. Has play against 70%+ of the meta. Best buddy: raise to 51+ CP for extra coverage.
#2 Medicham (55.8% Win Rate)
Psychic + Punching combo. Requires 100% IV to hit 1,500 CP cap exactly. The #1 wall to Azumarill.

The best ghost type in GL. Shadow Ball destroys Azumarill and Medicham. Dragon Breath damage is deceptively high.

⚠️ The 5 PvP Mistakes That Lose Battles

Mistake 1: Not Using Shield baiting
If your opponent has 1 shield and you have 2, throw your low-damage charge move first. They WILL shield it. Then your nuke lands. In GL, 90% of players fall for this at least once per battle.
Mistake 2: Not Counting Fast Moves
Every fast move has a "turn" count (4 turns = 2 seconds at 500ms/move). Dragon Breath: 2 turns. Confusion: 4 turns. If you know their cooldown, you can catch moves. Dragon Breath users: wait 4 turns after they throw to catch safely.
Mistake 3: Switching Randomly
Switch has a 60-second cooldown. Switching into a bad matchup wastes it. Always have a reason: you're about to lose a Pokemon, or you see a hard counter in the back.
Mistake 4: Not Knowing CMP (Charging Priority)
If both players fire a charge move on the same turn, the one with higher CP wins. Always fire charge moves when you have energy advantage, not when you're even.
Mistake 5: Overfarming Energy Before Firing
In GL, 50 energy = your charge move. If you farm to 100 energy, you're sitting on 50 wasted energy. Fire at 50 and build again. Every extra fast move is a risk.
