⚠️ The 5 Egg Mistakes

Mistake 1: Using Infinite Incubator on 2km Eggs
Infinite Incubator (the blue one) should ONLY be used on 9km eggs. 9km eggs take the longest, so the infinite incubator provides maximum value. Using it on 2km eggs wastes its potential — you could have used a regular incubator instead.
Mistake 2: Not Equipping Starpiece During Hatch
Starpiece increases XP from hatch by 50%. On a 9km egg hatch, you get 3,000 XP + 3,000 stardust. With Starpiece = 4,500 XP + 4,500 stardust. Always pop a Starpiece before hatching high-value eggs.
Mistake 3: Walking in a Straight Line
GPS drift happens when you stand still but the game thinks you're moving. The trick: walk in small circles in your apartment, or use a treadmill. Straight-line walking triggers anti-cheat pauses. Circles keep the distance counter increasing.
Mistake 4: Not Knowing Which Eggs Are Worth Saving
9km eggs have the best Pokemon but you only get them from gifts. If you're low on egg slots, delete 2km eggs first. Never delete a 9km egg unless you already have a hundo of that species.
Mistake 5: Hatching During Events Without Using Lucky Egg
During 2x XP events, hatching gives 6,000 XP per 9km hatch instead of 3,000. Always have a Lucky Egg active during community days and events when hatching eggs.
