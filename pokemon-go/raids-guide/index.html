⚠️ The 5 Raid Mistakes

Mistake 1: Not Using the Right Type Counters
Using neutral damage = 40% fewer balls. Always use dual-typed counters that are super effective. Example: vs Mewtwo (Psychic), use Shadow Ball Machamp + Dynamic Punch. That's 160% effectiveness vs 100% neutral.
Mistake 2: Joining Raids Without Premier Balls
You get 6-14 Premier Balls per raid based on: damage dealt (up to 5 balls), gym control (up to 3 balls), team contribution (1 ball), and personal contribution (1 ball). Maximum = 14 balls. More balls = higher catch chance. Always do maximum damage.
Mistake 3: Using Golden Razz on 3% Catch Rate Legendary
Golden Razz Berry gives +2x catch multiplier. On a 3% base catch rate Legendary, Golden Razz = 6% catch rate. Silver Razz = 5.5%. Pinap = 4.5%. If you have less than 10 balls, use Golden Razz. If you have 10+ balls, you can afford to experiment with Pinap for extra candy.
Mistake 4: Not Inviting Friends for Remote Raids
Remote raid passes ($1.99 each) let you invite friends from anywhere in the world. Best friends give +3 damage bonus balls. Using 3 best friends = +9 extra balls = 9 extra catch attempts. This is the single biggest catch rate improvement available.
Mistake 5: Fainting Your Counter Pokemon Before the End
In raids, your Pokemon faints after taking damage. If your best counter faints with 10 seconds left, you lose damage output. Use 2 teams: Team 1 for first half, Team 2 (slightly weaker) for second half. This ensures you always have fresh counters for the final damage push.
