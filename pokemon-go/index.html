PvP IV Breakpoints

Great League (1500 CP cap): The 0/15/15 spread provides maximum bulk for most Pokemon. However, some Pokemon have specific attack breakpoints against key matchups that change the IV spread.

Example - Lickitung: With 0/15/15, Lickitung loses the 1-shield mirror to another Lickitung. With 1/15/14, Lickitung wins the 1-shield mirror due to a CMP tie. The attack IV determines who deals damage first in speed-tied scenarios.

Ultra League (2500 CP cap): Most Pokemon use 1/15/15 or 2/15/15. The attack IV contributes to CMP ties without exceeding the cap. The higher attack IV is always better if it doesn't reduce bulk.
