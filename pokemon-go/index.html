⚠️ The 5 Pokemon GO Mistakes (Niantic Behavior Data)

Mistake 1: Powering Up Before Level 30 (you waste stardust)
Wild Pokemon have CP proportional to your trainer level. At Level 30+, you catch Pokemon with max IVs at Level 30. Powering up before Level 30 means you pay extra stardust for lower-level Pokemon. Wait until Level 35+ to power up legendaries.
Mistake 2: Using Rare Candy on Non-Legendaries (never do this)
Rare Candy is the rarest resource in the game. It converts to any Pokemon's energy at 1:1. Never use it on Pokemon that you can walk for energy (like Kyogre Groudon). Save all Rare Candy for Mewtwo, Rayquaza, and other Mythical/Legendary.
Mistake 3: Not Doing Daily Research (you're leaving 2,000+ stardust per week)
Every day, complete the field research quest. It gives 100-500 stardust per day, and 7 days of completed quests = a legendary encounter. That's 3,500+ stardust + 1 legendary per week for free.
Mistake 4: PvP with Wrong IVs (you lose mirror matches)
For Great League PvP, you want 0/15/15 IVs (low attack, high defense/stamina). For Ultra League, 0/15/15 again. High attack IVs push your Pokemon above the CP cap, forcing you to use worse Pokemon. Use Calcy IV to check.
