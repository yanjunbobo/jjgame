⚡ Best Attackers & Raid Counters

Pokemon GO's meta evolves with each Community Day and raid rotation. This guide covers the current best attackers, raid counters, and PvP IV recommendations for Great League through Master League in 2026.

Best Raid Attackers

Mega Gengar: The best ghost-type attacker. Shadow Claw + Shadow Ball deals massive damage. The Mega Evolution energy cost is worth the 30% boost to team damage.

Mega Salamence: The best dragon-type attacker. Outrage provides consistent dragon damage. Double Legacy Move access makes it irreplaceable in dragon-weak raids.

Mega Diancie: The best rock-type attacker. The 30% team boost makes Mega Diancie mandatory for rock-weak bosses. Rock Slide coverage handles most neutral scenarios.

PvP IV Recommendations

Great League (1500 CP): Bulk matters more than attack. The 0/15/15 IV spread is standard for tanks. High-attack spreads (15/15/0) are for glass cannons with specific breakpoints.

Ultra League (2500 CP): The 1/15/15 spread provides the most bulk within cap. Attack IV contributes to CMP ties, making some attack valuable.

Master League (No Cap): Max IVs are mandatory. 15/15/15 is the only viable spread. Level 50 is the cap, so power up to 50 regardless of dust cost.
