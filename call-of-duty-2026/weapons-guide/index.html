🔫 Weapon Classes & TTK Analysis

Understanding weapon time-to-kill (TTK) separates good players from great ones. This guide breaks down each weapon class by TTK, optimal ranges, and attachment recommendations for COD 2026.

Assault Rifles

MCW: 289ms TTK at optimal range. The most forgiving AR with near-zero recoil. The muzzle brake + vertical grip combo is standard. Use the XRK Lightning Fire barrel for extended range.

AK-74u: 310ms TTK. The highest damage AR with corresponding recoil. The Compensator + Tac Laser combo manages the vertical recoil pattern. Use FSS Forge Tac for accuracy.

SMGs

Jackal PDW: 240ms TTK. The fastest SMG at close range. The 45-round mag is non-negotiable for Warzone viability. The Cor-45 REGDF barrel extends the effective range to AR distances.

Striker: 260ms TTK. The most accurate SMG. The JAK Annihilator bolt improves fire rate at the cost of sprint-to-fire. Use the JAK Throat microflechettes for extended range.
