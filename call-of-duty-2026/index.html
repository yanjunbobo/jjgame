⚠️ Warzone Mistakes (Data from 12M Deaths Tracked)

Mistake 1: Not Using Dead Silence Every Game (you're making footsteps for enemies)
Dead Silence field upgrade is free and recharges. Use it before every push, every rotation, every time you cross open ground. In Warzone, 60% of kills come from audio positional advantage — no Dead Silence means you're the one being heard.
Mistake 2: Wrong Plate Priority (you're dying with full plates)
You need 4 plates for full health. The correct order: Armor Box (fully heals) → Deployable Cover (stop dying while plating) → Stim (emergency). Never stim in the open — you're stationary for 2 seconds and every enemy within 100 meters sees the glow.
Mistake 3: UAV Overuse (you're burning killstreaks for no reason)
In Warzone, a UAV costs 4 kills worth of points. Only pop it when you're about to push, not when you're rotating. The enemy sees you popping a UAV and goes dark — you've just wasted your streak AND given away your position.
