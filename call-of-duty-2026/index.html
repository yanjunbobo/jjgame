🔫 Best Loadouts & Meta Weapons 2026

Call of Duty 2026's meta has stabilized with the Season 1 balance patches. This guide covers the strongest loadouts for Warzone and multiplayer in 2026.

S-Tier Loadouts

MCW Variant: The dominant AR after recoil nerfs to other meta weapons. 60-round mag with the COR-45 slide. The attachment combo provides zero-recoil at mid-range with competitive close-range TTK. Use the Tac-Lite barrel for maximum mobility.

Jackal PDW: The best SMG for aggressive play. Fast TTK at close range with manageable recoil. The COR-45 slide makes it feel like a laser at all ranges. The 45-round extended mag is mandatory for sustained engagements.

SR-7 Sniper: The most consistent one-shot sniper. The SP-X 80 bolt-action remains competitive but the SR-7's semi-auto capability gives it an edge in Warzone where follow-up shots matter.

Perk Packages

Ghost Proficiency: Ghost is mandatory in Warzone. The sensor highlight from Flacha Jacket makes it even more valuable. Stack with Cold Brigadiers for maximum survivability.

Overclock Field Upgrade: The faster scorestreak charge from upgraded Field Upgrade is underrated. Overclock enables Comlink Streaks faster than any other method.
