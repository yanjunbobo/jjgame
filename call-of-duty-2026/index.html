Why Your Loadout Is Wrong (Not Using the Meta Attachments)

After 1,000+ hours: the #1 loadout mistake is using default attachments instead of meta attachments. The barrel attachment alone adds 20% effective range. I tested default vs meta loadouts for 50 hours. Meta attachments increased my kill rate by 25%.

The Best Loadouts for Warzone

1. SMG class: MCW SMG build for mobility + damage. 2. AR class: AK-74 for range + control. 3. Sniper class: Longbow for holding angles. I used the SMG class for 100 hours and had a 1.8 K/D in Warzone. The mobility lets you reposition faster than AR users.

The Ranked Play Strategy That Got Me to Diamond

I hit Diamond in one season. The specific strategy: 1) Play with a 3-stack (not solo). 2) One person calls out targets. 3) Focus on obj over kills. The key insight: ranked is 70% teamwork, 30% gun skill. I won 60% of my ranked games by playing objectives consistently.
