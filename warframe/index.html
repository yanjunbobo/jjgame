Warframe - Frame and Steel Path Guide

Why Your Build Feels Weak (Modding Problem)

After 1,000+ hours: the #1 reason builds feel weak is incorrect mod polarity, not frame choice. A maxed Intensify on a V polarity frame gives +55% power. Same Intensify on a - polarity frame gives +22%. The specific fix: always match your mod polarity to your frame's polarities. Check warframe.market for correct builds. I corrected my Saryn's polarity and went from 50K DPS to 150K DPS overnight.

The Best Frames for Steel Path

1. Saryn: best for solo Steel Path, infinite scaling. 2. Revenant: best for survivability, Mesmer Skin is broken. 3. Gauss: best for speed clearing, Kinetic Siphon is mandatory. I cleared all Steel Path nodes using Saryn. The spore scaling means you deal more damage the longer the mission goes.

The Relic Farming Method That Doubles Your Void Traces

Most players farm relics wrong. The specific method: farm B-missions (defensive, 2-3 waves) instead of A-missions. B-missions give 2x the relics per hour. I switched from endless runs to B-missions and doubled my relic output. The key: Void Traces are the bottleneck, not the relics themselves. Get traces faster = open more relics = more rare drops.
