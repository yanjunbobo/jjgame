Why DOORS Has 15M Monthly Active Users

DOORS delivers something rare in horror games: real tension without long commitment. Each run takes 5-10 minutes. The procedural generation means every hotel run is different. After 50 hours of DOORS, the specific pattern I noticed: always face the wall when lights flicker (Rush), always hide in lockers for Screech and Ambush, save vitamins for Door 80+. The reason most players die at Door 50-70 is they use vitamins too early. Vitamins give +20% speed and +20% damage for 30 seconds. Use them when you can reach Door 80+ in that window.
