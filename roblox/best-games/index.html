Roblox's "most popular" list changes constantly, but some games have maintained top-5 status for years. Here's the 2026 list based on monthly active users.

📊 Top 10 Roblox Games (May 2026)

#1: DOORS (15M+ MAU)
The definitive Roblox horror game. 100 procedurally-generated doors with unique entity encounters. Best strategy: always face the wall when lights flicker, use lockers for Screech and Ambush, save vitamins for Door 80+.
#2: Blox Fruits (12M+ MAU)
One Piece-inspired action RPG. Best fruits for PvP: Buddha (defense + size), Leopard (speed + combo), Dough (parry + DPS). Top developers earn $100K+ per month.
#3: Brookhaven (10M+ MAU)
Open-world roleplay. "Cozy chaos" experience — no objectives, just explore with friends.
#4: Tower of Hell (8M+ MAU)
90-second obstacle courses. No checkpoints. Fall = restart. Perfect for players who like Super Mario Maker difficulty in multiplayer.
#5: Pet Simulator 99! (7M+ MAU)
Collecting gems and pets. Simple mechanics, addictive dopamine loop.
