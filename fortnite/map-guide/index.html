🏗️ Best Landing Spots & Map Guide

Fortnite Chapter 5 Season 2 map knowledge determines survival more than mechanical skill. This guide covers the best landing spots, rotation paths, and positional advantages across all POIs in 2026.

High-Loot POIs

Megagravelly: The largest named POI in the current map. Consistent 3-5 weapon spawns with multiple floor loot spawns. The underground section provides vertical combat options. Land the underground first, then fight upward for ring advantage.

Mount Kay: The premier sniper perch. High ground provides unmatched sightlines across the entire island. The loot pool is inconsistent but the positional advantage compensates. Land the peak, establish sightlines, and third-party every fight in the valley.

Grand Gulch: The best mid-map POI for rotations. Located centrally with fast access to all zones. The canyon provides natural chokepoints for defensive play. The loot pool is average but the rotation advantage is exceptional.

Off-Map Landings

UnnamedPOI-North: The smallest named location with guaranteed floor loot spawns. Safe landing away from hot drops. The vault provides guaranteed purple+ weapons. Use for passive early game with safe rotation options.

Gas Stations: Four gas stations scattered across the map. Small footprint with 2-3 weapon spawns each. Land here when you want minimal competition. The car spawns nearby enable fast rotation to circle.

Outposts: Named outposts with 2 weapon spawns and vault access. The consistent vault placement makes these reliable for guaranteed purple loot. Competition varies by zone—check circle position before committing.

Rotation Strategy

Early Zone: Always rotate toward the next zone before it starts closing. The 15-second warning gives exactly enough time to move from center positions. Late-zone rotations are the most common death cause in Fortnite.

Vehicle Usage: Cars provide armor upgrade efficiency. Park at blue zones for free armor pieces. The speed enables late rotations from edge positions. Always carry a car for final circles from edge drops.

High Ground Priority: In final circles, high ground is worth more than loot. Even a white-pistol player on high ground beats a gold-weapon player on low ground. Prioritize positioning over equipment in final zones.

POI Selection Based on Playstyle

Aggressive Players: Land Megagravelly or Mount Kay for maximum early-game fights. Accept the high-variance outcomes and embrace the chaos. Confidence in close-quarters combat is prerequisite.

Passive Players: Land unnamed locations or gas stations for guaranteed loot without competition. Rotate early and play for top-10 placements. Accept lower placement variance for more consistent survival.

Competitive Players: Land central outposts with rotation advantages. Build defensive positions before third-party threats arrive. The goal is 20+ elimination games through positioning rather than luck.
