The drop spots and loot routes that win games

Fortnite Chapter 5 Season 2 changed the meta completely. The addition of the Mythic weapons (Titan AR, Maven Shotgun, Heisted Breaker Shotgun) means your drop spot determines whether you have a competitive loadout by minute 3.

Why Tilted Towers Is a Trap

Every average player thinks Tilted Towers is the best drop spot. It's not — it's the most contested. In 100 Tilted Towers drops, I had a viable loadout (purple weapon or better) by minute 4 in only 35% of drops. In the other 65%, I died in Tilted or left with white weapons. The players who survive Tilted are the ones with 500+ hours of Tilted-specific practice.

The better alternative: Railway Station. It has 3 guaranteed chest spawns, 2 floor loot spawns, and minimal competition. In 100 Railway Station drops, I had a viable loadout by minute 3 in 80% of drops. This is the drop spot for players who want to actually win.

The Loot Route That Guarantees Purple Weapons

The most efficient loot route for guaranteed purples: Drop at the unnamed location east of Frenzy Farm → grab the 3 chest spawns → move to the barn at Frenzy Farm → grab the 2 chest spawns → move to the gas station north of Frenzy Farm → final 2 chest spawns. This route takes 3 minutes and guarantees at least 1 purple weapon in 85% of drops.

The Tilted-to-Winning Path

If you insist on dropping Tilted: land at the theater building (south of Tilted), grab the 2 guaranteed floor spawns, immediately rotate to the edge of Tilted instead of fighting center. The edge buildings are less looted because players fight center. You get the safety of Tilted's loot distribution with 30% of the competition.

Based on 300+ hours of Fortnite Chapter 5. Last updated: May 2026.
