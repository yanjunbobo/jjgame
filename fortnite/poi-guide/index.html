⚠️ The 5 Landing Mistakes

Mistake 1: Dropping at Tilted Towers Equivalent Every Game
The "hot drop" equivalent draws 20-30 players. If you're not in the top 1% mechanically, you'll die in the first 2 minutes 80% of the time. Save hot drops for when you're warmed up.
Mistake 2: Not Planning the Bus Route
The battle bus path determines how many players land where. If the bus goes directly over Mega City, 40 players will drop there. Check bus path first, then pick a spot 1-2 zones away from the path.
Mistake 3: Looting Too Long at One Spot
Optimal loot time: 90 seconds. After that, you need to be rotating to zone. If you're still looking for a shotgun at 2 minutes, you're too slow. Take what you have and move.
Mistake 4: Not Knowing the Map Rotation Points
Every POI has 2-3 natural rotation points (bridges, ziplines, vehicles). Know yours before you need them. Getting caught without a rotation plan when the zone closes = death.
Mistake 5: Landing at Unlooted Locations
Some named locations look good but have poor loot tables. Use the map's loot indicator (the colored dots). If a location has no loot dots before you land, it's probably not worth it.
