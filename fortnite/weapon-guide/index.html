🎯 Best Weapons & Loadouts

Fortnite Chapter 5 Season 2 weapon meta is dominated by specific builds. This guide covers the strongest weapons, optimal attachments, and loadout recommendations for competitive and casual play in 2026.

S-Tier Weapons

Stinger SMG: The best close-range weapon after the Tactical SMG nerfs. High fire rate with manageable recoil. The magazine size enables sustained pressure without reload. Pair with a pump for maximum close-range lethality.

Hunter Bolt-Action Sniper: The most consistent sniper in the current meta. One-shot headshot potential with fast bolt cycling. The scope clarity is the best among snipers. Essential for long-range eliminations and objective pressure.

Combat SMG: The best mid-range SMG option. The burst-fire mode provides accuracy impossible with full-auto SMGs. The damage output at mid-range exceeds any AR at close range.

Heavy Shotgun: The highest damage shotgun at the cost of fire rate. The pump mechanic rewards precise aim with instant eliminations. Miss the shot and you're vulnerable for a full pump cycle.

A-Tier Weapons

Tactical AR: The most versatile AR option. Manageable recoil and decent damage make it effective at all ranges. The attachment compatibility makes it customizable for any playstyle.

Combat Shotgun: The consistent shotgun option. Lower damage ceiling than Heavy but higher fire rate means more attempts. The automatic fire removes pump timing requirement.

DMR: The best sustained damage option at range. Single-fire precision with higher DPS than snipers at mid-range. The recoil pattern is the most challenging aspect to master.

Loadout Recommendations

Aggressive Loadout: Stinger SMG + Combat Shotgun + Hunter Sniper. This loadout enables fast rotations and aggressive play. Close-range dominance with backup long-range capability.

Balanced Loadout: Tactical AR + Combat SMG + Hunter Sniper. The AR handles mid-range, SMG covers close-range, sniper provides long-range picks. The most versatile loadout for all situations.

Defensive Loadout: Heavy Shotgun + Tactical AR + DMR. Maximum single-shot damage with sustained ranged capability. Use when holding positions or playing around objectives.

Attachment Priority

SMGs: Stabilizer stock first for recoil reduction. Extended mag second for sustained fire. Quick-draw grip third for faster ADS.

Snipers: Extended bolt for faster cycling. Precision stock for accuracy. Scope is preference-based on target distance.

Shotguns: Heavy barrel for damage. Extended tube for magazine. Precision stock for accuracy.
