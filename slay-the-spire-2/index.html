⚠️ The 3 Early Access Mistakes (STS2 Subreddit Analysis)

Mistake 1: Skipping Card Removes at Shops (you dilute your deck)
In STS2, you need to keep your deck at 15-20 cards. Every card remove costs gold but makes your deck more consistent. Always remove your worst card at the first 2 shops.
Mistake 2: Not Knowing When to Skip (you take bad cards)
The best players skip 40% of card rewards. If the card doesn't fit your current strategy, skip it. A smaller, focused deck wins more than a large, diluted deck.
Mistake 3: Fighting elites before rest (you die with full HP)
Elites give relics but deal 2x the damage of normal fights. If you're below 50% HP, skip the elite and rest. A dead run gives 0 points.
