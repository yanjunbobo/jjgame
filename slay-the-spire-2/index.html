🃏 Complete Character Card Tier Lists

Slay the Spire 2's card pool is significantly different from the original. This complete card tier list ranks every card by character, with explanations of why each card belongs in its tier and when to pick it in Act 1 in 2026.

Ironclad Card Tier List

S-Tier: Inflame, Hemokinesis, Bash, Cleave (upgraded). These cards define Ironclad's identity and win Act 1 without question.

A-Tier: Iron Wave, Shrug It Off, Pummel, Carnage. Strong cards with specific use cases. Iron Wave is excellent early, falls off in Act 3.

B-Tier: Body Slam, Clash, Ghostly Armor. Body Slam requires setup but is powerful. Clash is good early, dangerous later. Ghostly Armor is for Defect migrations.

C-Tier: Clothesline, Pommel Strike. Clothesline's damage is decent but expensive. Pommel Strike is card draw but replaces itself slowly.

Silent Card Tier List

S-Tier: Adrenaline, Blade Dance, Dagger Spray (upgraded), Backflip. Adrenaline is universally taken. Blade Dance enables the Dexterity archetype. Backflip provides card draw and block.

A-Tier: Poison Stab, Quick Slash, Acrobatic, Eviscerate. Poison Stab is the best early damage for Silent. Quick Slash is the reliable filler. Eviscerate requires setup but is worth it.

B-Tier: Survivor, Neutralize, Sucker Punch. Survivor is boring but effective. Neutralize is the starter with a small damage boost. Sucker Punch requires precision.

Act 1 Boss Strategies

The Guardian: Needs either Inflame + strength scaling or high burst damage. Block cards are mandatory—the Guardian's multi-hit attack will kill you without block.

The Hexaghost: Pairs well with Poison. The divide mechanic is negated by Poison's damage-over-time. Strong early defense is required for the turn 1 inflamedebuff.
