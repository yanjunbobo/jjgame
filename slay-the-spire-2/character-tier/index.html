⚠️ The 5 Character Mistakes

Mistake 1: Playing Ironclad Like a Damage Character
Ironclad's strength scaling is strong but his block is the key to Heart kills. Block + Strength = you take 0 damage while dealing massive damage. Pure damage Ironclad = dies to Time Eater.
Mistake 2: Taking Too Many Cards as The Silent
Silent's biggest mistake is over-drawing. Her discard synergy means she needs a small, efficient deck. 18-22 cards is optimal. 30 cards = you never draw your win condition.
Mistake 3: Not Prioritizing Evolutions as The Defect
Defect without orb evolution = 50% damage output. Always pick cards that generate dark orbs. The Defect's scaling is the best in the game — if you survive to Act 3, you win.
Mistake 4: Trying to Force Wrath Stance as Watcher
Watcher's Wrath stance gives 2x damage but makes you take 2x. In A20, enemies hit for 50+. Trying to stay in Wrath without Calm exits = instant death. Build Wrath as a finisher, not a strategy.
Mistake 5: Skipping Card Removes in Act 1
Every character needs to remove their starter cards by Act 2. Strikes and Defends dilute your deck by 20-30%. In Act 1, spend gold on removes before anything else.
