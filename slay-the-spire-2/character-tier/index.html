Character tier list with specific card recommendations

Each character in STS2 has a completely different game plan. Here's the complete breakdown with specific card recommendations.

Ironclad: The Strength Deck

The Ironclad's core mechanic: increase Strength, then multiply it. The key cards: Inflame (free Strength), Spot Weakness (Strength when enemy attacks), Demon Form (2 Strength per turn for the rest of combat). The specific win condition: get to 10+ Strength, then use Limit Break to double it to 20+, then use Barricade to make your Block permanent. At 20 Strength with Barricade, you deal 20+ damage per attack AND you can't be killed because you have infinite block.

Silent: The Poison and Weak Deck

The Silent's core mechanic: apply Poison, then apply Weak, then watch them die. The key cards: Catalyst (converts Poison stacks into a multiplier), Bane (poison that triggers immediately), Crippling Poison (Poison + Weak). The specific win condition: apply 10+ Poison stacks, then play Catalyst to multiply them to 30+, then watch the boss die over 3 turns. Against Act 3 bosses with 300+ HP, this deals 90 damage per turn for 3 turns = 270 damage.

Defect: The Orb Engine

The Defect's core mechanic: channel orbs, then evoke them for big damage. The key cards: Dark Orb (passive damage that builds up, evoke for massive single hit), Frost Orb (block that persists), Multi-Cast (evoke all orbs). The specific win condition: build 8 Dark Orbs over 8 turns, then evoke all of them for 200+ damage. This is called the "Darkfin" build and it's the most powerful Defect strategy.

Based on 300+ hours across A0 to A20 runs. Last updated: May 2026.
