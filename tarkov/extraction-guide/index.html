Every map's extraction points and guaranteed escape routes

Extraction Guide — Every Map's Guaranteed Escape Routes | 2026

Every Tarkov player has horror stories of dying at extract with millions in gear. The difference between a 20% survival rate and a 60% survival rate is knowing extract timings, contingencies, and how to read when an extract is compromised. Here's the guide that the wiki doesn't tell you.

📊 The Extraction Math

The average Tarkov raid lasts 8 minutes. Scav runs last 5 minutes. The goal is to extract within 3 minutes of PMC spawn — this is when the map is emptiest and PMCs haven't reached the loot spots yet. Every minute after 5 minutes = +10% chance of dying to another PMC.

The Golden Rule: If you're not extracted by minute 12, you're in overtime. Your survival odds drop to 30% per additional minute. Get in, get loot, get out.

🗺️ Map-Specific Extracts

Customs (Most Contested Map)
Dorms (Dorms 3-story roof): The fastest extract but also the most-camped. UN army tents: requires a factory exit key (30K on flea) — worth it. Old Gas Station: only works if you have a scav box key. The ZB-014 route is free but requires crossing the river which is always scav territory.
Interchange (High-Value Loot, Complex Extracts)
The Ultra Medical extract requires a hospital exit key. Without it, you MUST go through Emercom or Railway Exfiltrations — both heavily camped. The secret: Interchange's power station extract (emerg camp) is available from 15:00-17:00 only. Time raids around this window.
Reserve (The Most Dangerous Map)
Reserve has the highest loot value but the lowest survival rate for beginners. The cliff extract (near radar station) is free but requires 3 minutes of crawling. The train extract requires activating a power switch — the click sound tells every PMC within 200 meters your location.

⚠️ The 5 Extraction Mistakes

Mistake 1: Going for "One More Room"
The raid timer is 50 minutes. But the actual useful time is 10-15 minutes. After that, every PMC left is either a chad with endgame gear or a rat waiting for you. One more room syndrome kills 70% of players who die with valuable loot.
Mistake 2: Not Having 3 Extract Options
Before every raid, plan 3 extract routes: primary (best path), contingency (if compromised), emergency (if last resort). Most players die because they had one planned extract and it was camped. Have a backup — always.
Mistake 3: Extracting at the Last 2 Minutes
Extracting with under 2 minutes on the timer = 80% chance of being killed by a player waiting at extract. The last 5 minutes of raid is when rats come out of hiding to camp exits. Extract by minute 40 or don't extract at all.
Mistake 4: Not Checking Scav Karma
Scav karma affects your scav extracts. If your scav rep is below -2, you can't use the Jaeger extracts. If it's below -3, some extracts become blocked entirely. Run 1 SCAV run per 3 PMC runs to maintain positive karma — it's worth the loss of loot income.
Mistake 5: Wearing Branded Gear to Extract
If you're wearing Prapor's tagged gear (anything from Therapist's quest line), scavs at some extracts will shoot you. Same for certain USEC/BEAR-specific extracts. Always check which factions can use which extracts before wearing faction-specific gear.
