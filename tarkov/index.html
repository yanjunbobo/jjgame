Why You Keep Dying in Labs (Scav Problem)

After dying 50+ times in Labs: the #1 killer in Labs is other players, not SCAVs. Players sit in corners with no SCAVs to make noise. The specific counter: open your map and check where players have died (red skull markers). Avoid those areas for the first 10 minutes. I reduced my death rate in Labs from 80% to 30% by checking the map before moving.
