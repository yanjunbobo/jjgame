Loot Tier Guide — Highest Value Loot Spots Per Map | 2026

The wiki tells you WHERE loot spawns. This guide tells you WHEN to go there, in what ORDER, and which loot is actually worth your extraction time. 80% of loot spots are waste of time — here's how to filter the signal from the noise.

📊 The Loot Value Math

The ruble-per-minute rule:

Every minute in a raid costs you ~2,000 rubles in expected death risk (amortized survival rate). Therefore, a loot spot must yield 10,000+ rubles in under 5 minutes to be worth the trip. Most loot spawns are worth 2,000-5,000 rubles — not worth the risk.

The 3 Must-Grab items:

  1. LEDX (300K-500K): Spawns in 3 locations on every map. Always worth the detour.
  2. Graphics Cards (80K-150K): Reshala spawns (Shoreline, Customs) — but he's a raid in himself.
  3. Tetriz/Phones (30K-80K): Fast to grab, high value, fits in secure container.

🗺️ Map-Specific Loot Routes

Shoreline (Best ROI for Advanced Players)
Start: Road to Customs → loot the gas station (15K-40K in 30 seconds) → Resort via the cliff route (no PMC spawns before minute 8) → Hit Room 221 (LEDX spawn), Room 218 (graphics card), Room 206 (military tech). Extract via Tunnel. This route in under 15 minutes = 500K+ expected value.
Labs (Highest Risk, Highest Reward)
Labs requires a Labs access keycard (2M+ rubles) plus 500K in loadout. The loot is: 5x-10x other maps. But every PMC in Labs is endgame-geared. If you die, you lose 3M+ in investment. Only run Labs with at least 2 PMC friends and full intel on enemy positions.
