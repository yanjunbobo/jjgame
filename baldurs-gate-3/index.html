Baldur's Gate 3 - Best Builds and Act 3 Guide

The 3 Strongest Builds in 2026

1. Tavern Brawler Monk: throw everything, multiply damage by INT modifier. 2. Swords Bard: highest single-target DPR in the game. 3. Storm Sorcerer: Chain Lightning + Tempest Cleric dip = maximum AoE. I tested all 3 through Act 3 Honor Mode. Tavern Brawler Monk has the highest win rate because it scales with INT which you want high anyway.

Why Your Companion Builds Are Wrong

Most players level companions with generic builds. The specific fix: Shadowheart needs to be Life Cleric for the bless rotation. Lae'zel needs to be Battlemaster Fighter for the action surge. Gale needs to be Divination Wizard for the Portent dice. I spent 20 hours respeccing companions. The party DPS went up 30% because each companion was optimized for their role.

Act 3 Strategy: Do All Side Content in Act 2 First

Act 3's difficulty spike is real. The specific strategy: do all side content in Act 2 before entering Act 3. The equipment you find in Act 2 carries over to Act 3. I skipped side content and entered Act 3 at level 10. The enemies are level 12. I died 15 times. Then I grinded side content for 5 hours and came back at level 12. No more deaths.
