1. Weapon Tier List

All weapons tested across 50+ runs. Rankings reflect consistency (can clear on 1st try), ceiling (with perfect build), and ease of use.

🔱

Stygian Blade (Sword)

S-TIER

The Nova Smash (Special after Dash) is the best burst in the game. Aspect of Zagreus gives 50% more base damage. Aspect of Nemesis has the highest ceiling of any weapon. Most versatile weapon.

Clear Rate: 85% Skill: Low Best Aspect: Nemesis
🏹

Stellaris (Bow)

S-TIER

Aspect of Chiron + Duo Boon Artemis-Athena creates an impenetrable fortress. Every special applies MARKED, and Chiron's special always hits the marked target. Safest weapon in the game.

Clear Rate: 82% Skill: Low Best Aspect: Chiron
🪓

Minos (Axe)

A-TIER

Slow but devastating. Minos's Vicious Cycle (special after Attack) hits like a truck. Aspect of Daedalus gives +200% damage on bosses. The slowest weapon but one of the highest ceilings.

Clear Rate: 72% Skill: Medium
🔥

Pyra (Torch/Fire)

A-TIER

New weapon. Applies BURN via all actions. The damage-over-time effect is strong but requires staying close. Aspect of Hestia has insane single-target but struggles with adds.

Clear Rate: 70% Skill: Medium
💀

Oblivian (Skull/EX)

B-TIER

Ranged but slow projectile. SHADOW PRESENCE mechanic rewards proximity. Aspect of Melinoë has the best sprint attack in the game but the normal attack is weak.

⚔️

Varatha (Spear)

B-TIER

Aspect of Achilles is sleeper broken but underplayed. The Spin's wide arc clears mob rooms. Boss damage is mediocre without good boons.

2. Best Builds by Weapon

🔱 Stygian Blade — Aspect of Nemesis Crit Build

Core Boons

Athena — Divine Dash (deflects)
Ares — Blade Dash (after you dash, attack hits twice)
Duo: Artemis-Athena — CRITICAL SAGE (+25% crit chance)

Hammers (Priority)

Nova Smash — Special creates explosion (pick 1st)
Dashing Strike — Dash-Attack hits 3x (pick 2nd)
Playstyle: Dash in → Attack → Special (Nova) → Dash out. Crits with Artemis-Athena can hit 200+ per hit.

🏹 Stellaris — Chiron Fortress Build

Core Boons

Artemis — Support Fire (Special applies MARKED)
Athena — Divine Dash (mandatory)
Duo: Artemis-Athena — CRITICAL SAGE (see above)

Hammers (Priority)

Power Shot — Special hits 3x (pick 1st)
Hunter's Mark — Marked enemies take +50% damage (pick 2nd)
Playstyle: Mark everything with Special. Stay at range. Watch everything die before it reaches you.

5. Shadow Room Strategy

Shadow Rooms appear in Erebus between biomes. They're bonus rooms that offer massive rewards at high risk.

💀
Combat Shadow

Fight a mini-boss. Reward: 2 Daedalus Hampers or 600 gold.

🎁
Reward Shadow

3 choice reward. Usually 2 commons + 1 rare. Low risk.

⚠️
Danger Shadow

Extremely hard encounter. Reward: 1 Daedalus + 2 Olympian Boons. Not worth it unless you have a perfect build.

Strategy: Always take Reward Shadow. Only take Combat Shadow if you have high HP. Skip Danger Shadow unless you're speedrunning.

6. Relic (Tools of the Gods) Guide

S: Cosmic Egg

Start with 50 HP buffer. Absorbs first hit. The best early-game relic. Always take if offered.

S: Pierced Butterfly

+10% damage per encounter without taking damage. Stacks to infinity. Game-breaking if you don't get hit.

A: Adamant Arrow

+1 arrow on special. Only good with Chiron Bow or bow in general.

B: Myrmidon Bracer

Dash-Strike hits +1 time. Good with Stygian Blade's Dashing Strike build.
