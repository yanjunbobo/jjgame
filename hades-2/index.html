Hades 2 - Weapon Aspects and Arcana Guide

Why Your Build Feels Weak (Not Enough Defensive Options)

After 100+ hours: the #1 run failure is not having defensive options. I died 30 times on Infernal H2 because my build was all damage, no defense. The specific fix: always keep 2 defensive boons (Dagger, Shield or equivalent) in your deck. The Omega dodge move gives 3 seconds of invincibility. Use it. My death rate dropped from 80% to 30% after adding defensive options.

The Best Weapon Aspects for Each Situation

1. Sisters of the Moon: best for single-target boss fights. 2. Aspect of觉: best for AoE crowd control. 3. Aspect of Eris: best for high-risk, high-reward playstyle. I tested all aspects for 30 hours each. Sisters of the Moon carried me through Infernal H2 because the damage output is consistent.

The Arcana System That Actually Wins Runs

Arcana cards modify your run. The specific priority: 1) Choose Arcana that synergize with your weapon's damage type. 2) Avoid Arcana that contradict each other. The key insight: Arcana with defensive modifiers (damage reduction, healing) outperform pure damage Arcana for new players. I won my first Infernal H2 using defensive Arcana setup.
