1. Arcana Card System

Hades 2 introduces Arcana Cards — 22 tarot-style cards that modify your abilities. You collect them during runs and can equip up to 5 cards per run at the card reader in the hub (between runs).

Each card has a primary effect (always active) and a secondary effect (activates when you collect another specific card).

🃏 How to Get Cards

  1. 1. Find Tarot Tapestries in Erebus (shadow rooms)
  2. 2. Complete Eris side quests in surface biomes
  3. 3. Zagreus's Mirror upgrades unlock card slots
  4. 4. Random drops from bosses (rare)

⚡ Card Synergy Types

  1. Duos — 2 cards that synergize when equipped together
  2. Trios — 3 cards forming a combo chain
  3. Sets — 4+ cards with thematic bonuses
  4. Wild — Cards that work with anything

2. All Arcana Cards (Tier List)

🌟 S-Tier Cards (Always Equip)

The Moon (Major)

Shadow presence lasts 3x longer. Essential for Iga's Shadow Dance builds.

The Sun (Major)

Start each floor with 1 Daedalus hammer upgrade. Free power every room.

The Fool (Major)

First 5 encounters give bonus Daedalus. Rush to get this early.

Death (Major)

Critical hits deal +100% damage. With Artemis crit build = instant boss kills.

⚔️ A-Tier Cards (Situational)

The Tower

Enemies start with 20% HP reduced. Great for speedruns.

Justice

Gold drops 50% more. Economy card for late-game upgrades.

The Empress

Boon rarity +1 tier. Godly gifts become Mythical.

The Emperor

Take 15% less damage from bosses. Mandatory for high heat runs.

Temperance

Healing effects doubled. With Dionysus = near-invincible.

The Magician

Spell cooldowns -30%. Spam abilities.

🗑️ Avoid (Low-Tier Cards)

The Hanged Man

Retreat speed +50%. Mostly useless since you shouldn't be running.

Judgement

Enemies drop more gems. Gems are already plentiful.

3. Spell Combos (Daedalus + Boons)

Daedalus upgrades interact with boons to create powerful combos. These are the most broken synergies discovered by the community.

COMBO 1 🔱 ⚡

Stygian Blade + Ares Blade Dash + Artemis Critical

Step 1: Get Ares Blade Dash (dash-attack hits twice)
Step 2: Get Artemis Critical boon (25% crit chance)
Step 3: Use Daedalus "Dashing Strike" (dash-attack hits 3x)

Result: 3 hits × 2 (Blade Dash) × 2 (crit) = 12x damage per dash. Can kill bosses in 2 dashes.

COMBO 2 🏹 🌙

Chiron Bow + Artemis Marked + The Moon Arcana

Step 1: Artemis Support Fire (Special applies MARKED)
Step 2: The Moon Arcana (Marked lasts 3x longer)
Step 3: Chiron's special always hits MARKED targets

Result: Every special auto-targets the boss. No aiming required. Hold special, release, watch damage.

COMBO 3 🪓 🔥

Minos Axe + Poseidon Breakshot + Athena Deflect

Step 1: Poseidon Breakshot (knockback on special)
Step 2: Athena Divine Dash (deflect)
Step 3: Minos Vicious Cycle (special after attack)

Result: Every attack knocks enemies into walls for bonus damage. Bosses get stunlocked against arena edges.

COMBO 4 💀 🌿

Oblivian Skull + Dionysus Doom + Demeter Frost

Step 1: Dionysus Doom (poison DOT on cast)
Step 2: Demeter Frost (chance to freeze)
Step 3: Oblivian's shadow projectiles seek poisoned enemies

Result: Shadow projectiles home in on poisoned enemies. Free damage while you focus on dodging.
