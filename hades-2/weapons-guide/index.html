⚠️ The 5 Weapon Mistakes

Mistake 1: Not Upgrading the Right Aspects
Each weapon has 3 aspects. Aspect of Melinoe (Memphis Flame) is the best boss killer. Aspect of Icarus is best for wave clear. Players waste maxing the wrong aspect and lose 30% damage output.
Mistake 2: Ignoring Omega Moves
Omega moves (hold attack/special) are 50-100% stronger than normal attacks in Hades 2. Using only normal attacks = losing 50% of your damage potential. Practice omega timing for every weapon.
Mistake 3: Not Matching Boons to Weapons
Some boons synergize with specific weapons. Zeus on Memphis Flame = chain lightning on every hit. Dionysus on Circe Staff = massive poison ticks. Always read what boons synergize with your weapon.
