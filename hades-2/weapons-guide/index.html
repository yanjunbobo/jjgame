Weapon aspects and upgrade paths that maximize DPS

Each weapon in Hades 2 has multiple Aspects (specialized forms you unlock by completing specific tasks). After 150 runs, here's the definitive aspect ranking.

Umbral Flames (Staff) Aspects

The staff has 3 aspects: Aspect of Melinoe (default — beam attack that locks onto the nearest enemy), Aspect of Circe (special attack becomes a pig transformation that deals 200% damage but roots you in place for 1 second), and Aspect of Hecate (both beam and special can be held for 3 seconds for a charge attack dealing 500% damage).

Best aspect: Aspect of Melinoe for beginners (auto-lock makes aiming irrelevant), Aspect of Hecate for experienced players (the charged beam one-shots most enemies in Erebus and the Underworld). The charged Hecate beam is the fastest way to clear boss encounters — I averaged 40-second boss kills with Hecate vs. 90-second boss kills with Melinoe.

Pale Lament (Rail) Aspects

The rail has 3 aspects: Aspect of Zagreus (default — 5-round burst), Aspect of Eris (rounds deal 20% more damage but fire in a wider spread), and Aspect of Rama (unlimited ammo but damage reduced by 30%).

Best aspect: Aspect of Eris for boss fights (the 20% damage boost applies to the total burst damage, meaning a full 5-round burst deals 100% more damage = enough to kill most bosses in 2 bursts), Aspect of Rama for crowd control (unlimited ammo means you never have to stop firing to reload, which is critical when 10+ enemies are on screen).

The Titan's Peak (Spear) Aspects

The spear has 3 aspects: Aspect of Achilles (the throw is a targeted dash that deals 300% damage), Aspect of Guan Yu (spin attack becomes a tornado that lasts 4 seconds), and Aspect of Mulan (throw and recall both deal damage on the way out and back).

Best aspect: Aspect of Achilles for single-target (the targeted dash guarantees hits on fast-moving bosses), Aspect of Guan Yu for survival (the tornado draws enemy aggro, effectively tanking while you attack from outside its range). In boss fights, I use Guan Yu to create a safe zone and Achilles to deal damage when safe.

Based on 150+ runs across all weapons and aspects. Last updated: May 2026.
