1. Deck Tier List (May 2026)

Based on ranked ladder win rates and tournament data. Patch 2.5.1 balance adjustments applied.

🔥 Charizard EX / Pidgeot EX

S-TIER

The dominant deck. Charizard EX deal 120 damage per turn with energy acceleration from Bennet. Pidgeot EX searches any card. This deck is 40% of the top 100.

Win Rate: 58% Play Rate: 22% Skill: Medium

⚡ Mewtwo EX / Gardevoir

S-TIER

Mewtwo EX's Psychic Damage ability does 80 + 20 per heads (coin flip). Inconsistent but devastating when it flips 5+ heads. Gardevoir adds consistency via Magic Radar.

Win Rate: 55% Play Rate: 15% Skill: High

🛡️ Steelix EX / Metang

A-TIER

Counter deck. Steelix EX has 180 HP and reduces Mewtwo damage. Excels against the meta but folds to Charizard's fire power.

Win Rate: 51% Skill: Easy

🌊 Blastoise / Wailord

A-TIER

Water aggro with energy acceleration via Blastoise's Water Cyclone. Reliable but lacks the late-game burst of S-tier decks.

🌿 Sableye / Garbodor

B-TIER

Disruption deck. Sableye's Junk Hunt shuffles opponent's hand. Meta-dependent — dominant when everyone plays combo, useless against aggro.

6. Common Mistakes

❌ Using Professor Oak Wrong

Many players discard too many cards. Professor Oak's effect is mandatory — you MUST shuffle your hand back. Only keep your 2-3 key cards.

❌ Not Attaching Energy Turn 1

Always attach 1 energy on your first turn. Bennet on turn 2 + pre-attached energy = Charizard online turn 3. Without pre-attaching, you're a turn behind.

❌ Ignoring Prize Cards

When you're 1 prize from winning, don't KO their basic Pokemon if it means they'll draw into their EX. Set up the KO on their EX to guarantee your prizes.
