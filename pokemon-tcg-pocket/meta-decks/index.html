The decks that dominate the meta and how to counter them

The meta in Pokemon TCG Pocket shifts every week as new players discover counters. Here's the current tier list based on 500+ ranked matches.

Meta Tier List (May 2026)

S-Tier: Mewtwo EX stall (Mewtwo EX + Misty + professor's research to cycle energy, win by deckout), Charizard EX aggro (Charizard EX + Welder + Fire Crystal, win by turn 5).

A-Tier: Pikachu EX stun (Pikachu EX + stun mechanics to prevent opponent actions), Gengar EX coin flip (Gengar's Hypno attack does 80 damage but has a 50% chance to confuse — the coin flip makes it inconsistent but devastating when it works).

How to Beat Mewtwo EX

Every new player faces Mewtwo EX and loses. The specific counter: Aggron EX. Aggron EX's Iron Defense ability gives it damage reduction that scales with energy on the field. With 3 energy, Aggron takes 50% less damage. With 5 energy, it takes 70% less. Mewtwo EX deals 80 damage per attack. Aggron at 5 energy survives 3 Mewtwo EX attacks and can counter-kill on turn 4.

How to Beat Charizard EX

Charizard EX is the fastest deck in the meta. The specific counter: Starmie EX + Sabrina. Starmie EX's rapid current ability lets you draw 2 cards every time an opponent's Pokemon is Knocked Out. Sabrina forces the opponent to switch their active Pokemon. The combo: Sabrina forces Charizard to the bench, Starmie attacks the new active, you draw 2 cards, repeat. This burns through the Charizard player's energy faster than they can recharge.

Based on 500+ ranked matches, Diamond rank. Last updated: May 2026.
