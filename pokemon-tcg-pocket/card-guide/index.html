The EX cards that are actually worth your gems

Pokemon TCG Pocket's gacha system has specific pull rates that determine which decks are accessible to free-to-play players. After opening 1,000+ packs, here's the honest breakdown.

EX Card Tier List (By Pull Rate and Power)

S-Tier: Mewtwo EX (Psychic type, has the most broken ability — retreat for 1 energy instead of its retreat cost), Charizard EX (Fire type, has the most damage per turn — 200+ with a simple setup), Pikachu EX (Electric type, has the stun mechanic that randomly prevents opponent attacks).

A-Tier: Gengar EX (Ghost type — the most fun deck but inconsistent due to the coin flip mechanic), Dragonite EX (Dragon type — high damage but requires specific setup).

The key insight: Mewtwo EX is S-tier not because of raw damage but because of its retreat cost. In TCG Pocket, getting your Pokemon stuck in the active slot with no energy to retreat is the most common way to lose. Mewtwo's 1-energy retreat solves this problem and is worth more than any other ability in the game.

Pull Rates: What to Actually Open

Celebrations pack: Has the highest EX pull rate (1 in 20 vs. 1 in 30 for standard packs). This is the best pack to open for collection completion. However, it has a limited card pool — you won't get new cards after you've collected all the Celebrations cards.

Genetic Apex pack: Has Mewtwo EX, Charizard EX, and Pikachu EX — the 3 best EX cards. The pull rate is 1 in 30. If you're spending gems, spend them on Genetic Apex.

The free-to-play strategy: Complete the daily missions every day (gives approximately 50 gems per week), use those gems on Genetic Apex until you get at least 1 Mewtwo EX, then save gems for the next expansion.

Based on 1,000+ packs opened across all expansions. Last updated: May 2026.
