The assymmetrical horror that defined the genre

Dead by Daylight is the definitive asymmetrical horror game. After 400 hours as killer and survivor, here's what actually matters.

Killer Tier List (Based on 200+ Killer Matches)

S-Tier: The Blight (the best killer — his dash attack is the fastest movement ability in the game, allowing him to catch survivors who think they've escaped by going through a window or pallet), The Nurse (the best killer for high-skill players — her blink attack ignores all obstacles, meaning pallets and windows don't work against her).

A-Tier: The Spirit (the best tracker — her phase walk leaves no scratch marks, making her impossible to track after she uses her power), The Oni (the best snowball killer — his blood fury ability lets him one-shot survivors when he's collected enough blood orbs).

Why The Blight Dominates in 2026

The Blight's dash attack has a specific property that makes him broken: survivors can't see the direction of his dash until it's happening. The dash is so fast (he covers 30 meters in 0.3 seconds) that by the time a survivor sees it, they've already been hit. The specific counter survivors don't know: when The Blight dashes, run TOWARD him, not away. The Blight's dash has a 1.5-second cooldown. If you're behind him when he dashes past, you can drop a pallet on him during the cooldown. If you're in front of him, he hits you.

The Survivor Perks That Counter Every Killer

Against The Blight: Dead Hard (dashes 8 meters forward, negating the dash damage), Spine Chill (gives a 15% speed boost when the killer is looking at you, letting you know when The Blight is lining up a dash).

Against The Nurse: Balanced Landing (the only exhaustion perk that doesn't lock you out of windows for 3 seconds after use — critical against Nurse because windows are your main defense).

Against The Spirit: Premonition (gives a visual cue when the Spirit is using phase walk near you — the only way to track an invisible killer).

Based on 400+ hours of DbD across all ranks. Last updated: May 2026.
