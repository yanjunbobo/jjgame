🏆 Champion Tier List 2026

League of Legends champion meta shifts monthly with patches. This tier list reflects current Season 2 competitive and ranked performance for champion select in 2026.

S-Tier Champions

Aurora: The premier mid-lane mage after her release. Her passive afterkill mechanic provides unmatched snowball potential. The zone-control ultimate is among the best teamfight abilities. Requires practice but rewards mastery with dominant lane presence.

Naafiri: The strongest assassin in the current meta. The Darkin triple-dagger mechanic provides sustained damage impossible for other assassins to match. Her roams are devastating due to the base movement speed from dagger trails.

Briar: The most dominant jungler in the current patch. Her blood-tracker passive enables aggressive pathing impossible for other junglers. The execute ultimate makes her the best duelist in the game. Coordinated teams build around her early-game pressure.

A-Tier Champions

Ahri: The safest mid-lane pick. Her charm provides unparalleled pick potential at all stages. The recent damage buffs made her relevant in competitive play again. Her mobility makes her forgiving for mistakes.

Yone: The most consistent top-lane bully. His lane phase is dominant against melee champions. The wind-shield mechanic provides unexpected survivability. Weakness is the difficulty of the W timing against ranged matchups.

Kayn: The best scaling jungler. His choice between Rhaast and Shadow Assassin forms at 6 minutes enables adaptation to enemy team composition. The early-game weakness is mitigated by safe farming paths.

Zeri: The premier hypercarry ADC after itemization buffs. Her mobility combined with Lethal Tempo makes her unbeatable in extended fights. Requires peel from support to function.

ADC Meta

Aphelios: The highest skill-cap ADC with unmatched damage ceiling. The weapon management system rewards practice with dominant results. Weakness is the complete vulnerability during weapon transitions.

Jinx: The most beginner-friendly hypercarry. Her passive provides escape and chase mechanics that cover positioning mistakes. The rocket upgrade is the highest single-target damage in ADC pool.

Kai'Sa: The best all-rounder ADC. Her hybrid damage makes her effective against tanky and squishy compositions alike. The R gap-closer provides unexpected kill potential in lane.
