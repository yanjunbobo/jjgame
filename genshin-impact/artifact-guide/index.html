1. Artifact System Explained

Artifacts are the most important power progression system. Each character needs 2 artifact sets (4pc or mix of 2pc+2pc) plus a main stat weapon.

🎯 Substat Priority

  1. CRIT Rate / CRIT DMG — Always #1 for DPS
  2. Energy Recharge (ER) — Depends on character
  3. ATK% — Good filler stat
  4. Elemental Mastery — For transformative reactions

⚡ CRIT Formula

Target: 70-80% CRIT Rate, 140-160% CRIT DMG

Formula: Expected damage = ATK × CRIT Rate × CRIT DMG + ATK × (1 - CRIT Rate)

Rule of thumb: 1 CRIT Rate ≈ 2 CRIT DMG for equal value

2. Best Artifact Sets Ranked

S-TIER | Emblem of Severed Fate

+20% ER + 25% burst damage. Goes on almost every support and sub-DPS. Best 4-piece set for Xiangling, Xingqiu, Bennett, Raiden, Yelan, Fischl.

S-TIER | Deepwood Memories

-30% Dendro RES. Mandatory for Dendro supports like Nahida, Baizhu. If you're running any Dendro team, put this on your Dendro character.

S-TIER | Gilded Dreams

+80 EM or +14% ATK. Best 4pc for transformatives. Goes on Sub-DPS who trigger reactions. Kuki, Fischl, Beidou, Dendro Travelers.

A-TIER | Marechaussee Hunter

+15% Normal/Charged + 36% CRIT Rate when opponent is affected by Hydro. BiS for Neuvillette. Requires the target to have Hydro applied.

A-TIER | Void's Ultimate (NO)

+30% DEF + 24% ATK after using burst. Best for NO buffer supports like TTDS holders and general buffers.

B-TIER | Shimenawa's Reminiscence

+50% Normal/Charged at cost of 15 energy. Good but risky. Requires you to have energy to spend. Keqing, Ayato, Cyno.

4. Efficient Artifact Farming

⏰ Daily Resin Budget

  1. 1. 40 resin on artifact domains (1 run)
  2. 2. 40 resin on talent books / weapon mats
  3. 3. 20 resin on Ley Line Overworld

Total: ~160 resin/day (2 condensed + 1 regular). Use condensed when possible.

🎯 Domain Priority

  1. 1. Emblem + Wanderer — Most universal
  2. 2. Deepwood + Gilded — For Dendro teams
  3. 3. Marechaussee — Only for Neuvillette

Rule: Don't farm 5-star artifacts before AR55. Before then, use the 4-star artifacts from exploration and BP. The 5-star drop rate is too low to justify resin.
