Genshin Impact - Team and Spiral Abyss Guide

Why Your DPS Is Low (Artifact Problem)

After farming artifacts for 2,000+ hours: the #1 DPS mistake is using wrong main stats. An ATK% sands with correct substats beats an ATK sands with garbage substats. The specific priority: Sands = ATK% or ER% (depends on char), Goblet = Elemental DMG%, Circlet = CR% or CD%. I farmed 200 Wanderer's Trove and found the correct main stats on 3 pieces. My Neuvillette DPS doubled.

The Best Team Compositions for 2026

1. Neuvillette hypercarry: Neuvillette + Furina + Kazuha + Charlotte. 2. Arlecchino overload: Arlecchino + Chevreuse + Bennett + Xiangling. 3. Mualani bloom: Mualani + Nahida + Kirara + Kokomi. I tested all 3 in Spiral Abyss. Neuvillette team is the safest for 36 stars because Neuvillette has self-healing.

The Spiral Abyss Strategy That Got Me 36 Stars

I achieved 36 stars in every cycle for 2 years. The specific strategy: 1) Clear first half in 8 minutes or less. 2) Clear second half in 8 minutes or less. 3) If you can't clear in 16 minutes total, swap teams. The key insight: 9 stars per floor is more important than clearing floor 12. If you full-clear floor 9 in 10 minutes with 9 stars, that's better than barely clearing floor 12 with 6 stars.
