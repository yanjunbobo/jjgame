4. Who to Pull (F2P Priorities)

✅ Always Pull on Debut

Nahida (best Dendro), Furina (best buffer), Neuvillette (best DPS). These never fall off.

⚠️ Pull If You Have The Team

Archons and limited 5-stars that synergize with your existing roster. Raiden (National team), Kazuha (reverse Melt/Vaporize).

❌ Skip (Low Priority)

First rerun of niche 5-stars. C1/C2/C3 constellations on supports. Weapon banners for 4-star weapons.

5. Common Mistakes

❌ Overinvesting in 4-Stars

Focus primo on 5-star limited characters. 4-stars are easily replaced. Don't C6 your Bennet hoping it replaces a 5-star.

❌ Ignoring the National Team

The National Team (Bennett/Xiangling/Xingqiu + flex) clears Abyss for free. Build it before any other team.

❌ Pulling for Weapon Instead of Character

4-star weapons are 90% as good as 5-star for most characters. Always prioritize new characters.
