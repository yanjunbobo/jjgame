1. BB-87 Bond System

BB-87's Bond Level affects gameplay: higher bond = lower stress decay rate, faster BB-87 calm-down, and unlocks special cosmetic reactions when you talk to 87 through the pod.

How to Raise Bond Fast

1.

Talk to BB-87 at every safehouse via the pod. There are 5 different voice lines per safehouse. Rotate through them.

2.

Keep BB-87 at 0 stress during long deliveries. Use cryo spray proactively, not reactively.

3.

Complete Side Orders that involve carrying BB-87 through BT zones without alerting any BTs. These give +3 bond EXP each.

4.

Don't let BB-87 cry. Every time BB cries, bond decay -5 EXP. A single cry undoes 2 hours of bond building.

  • Easiest region

❄️ Northern Region

  • CA-21 to CA-30
  • BT-heavy zones
  • Do in late game

4. Sam's Memories (50 Total) — Ending C Requirement

Analog recordings Sam made for Lou before she was born. These are completely separate from Memory Fragments. Required for the secret ending.

Ending C Requirement: Collect all 50 Sam's Memories AND all 47 Memory Fragments. This is the only way to unlock the Beach ending.

Sam's Memories appear as floating orange lights in the world. They're always in out-of-the-way locations — behind waterfalls, in caves, on cliff edges.

Total collection time: ~8-10 hours for all 97 collectibles combined.
