1. Story Overview

Death Stranding 2 picks up three years after the original. Sam Porter Bridges is now a senior porter, and a new protagonist rae has emerged. The world has partially recovered, but new threats — the Hematic Leprosy pandemic and the resurfacing of BTs — force Sam back into action.

The narrative is less isolation-focused than the original, with more emphasis on collaboration. You can now form "Remains" — ghostly echoes of other players' journeys that help you in the world. The tone is slightly lighter but Kojima's signature cryptic storytelling remains.

2. Chapter-by-Chapter Walkthrough

CHAPTER 1

The Road to Port Knot

Tutorial chapter. Reacquaints you with controls, BB-87, and the chiral network. Straightforward deliveries — focus on learning ladder placement.

Duration: ~4 hours Missions: 8
CHAPTER 2

Heartman's Last Expedition

Introduces Hematic Zones — areas where your BB-87 can detect BTs but you take chiral damage. First real challenge.

⚡ Key Item: The Hematic Filter gadget is unlocked here. Craft 3 of these before leaving — you'll need them for the Port Knot City infiltration.

CHAPTER 3

The Beach Beyond

The Beach sequences are back and expanded. Extended cosmic horror sequences with new mechanics. This chapter is often polarizing — some love it, some find it slow.

Duration: ~6 hours Missions: 12
CHAPTER 4

Cliffs of Supra

Vertical terrain chapter. Mountain climbing with BT-heavy sections. Bring explosive bolts and keep BB-87 in cryo at all times in BT zones.

⚠️ Difficulty: This is the hardest chapter. Stockpile materials. The final climb requires 40+ ladders and 15+ climbing anchors. Do not attempt without preparation.

CHAPTER 5

The Final Chiral

Convergence of all story threads. Multiple playable characters. Final choices diverge into different endings.

Duration: ~8 hours Choices Matter

3. BB-87 Mechanics Explained

BB-87 is your baby pod — both a companion and a game mechanic. Unlike BB from the first game, 87 has been upgraded with Hematic Resonance Detection.

❤️ BB-87 Health

Monitor stress levels via the pod's heart rate. High stress = BB-87 cries = attracts BTs. Keep it calm with cryo sprays and avoid BT exposure.

📡 BT Detection

When BB-87's eyes glow pink, BTs are within 50m. When they glow red, BTs are within 20m and aggro.

🧊 Cryo Mode

Hold LB/L1 to put BB-87 in cryo stasis. Freezes stress growth but disables BT detection. Essential when crossing known safe zones.

💀 Emergency Extraction

If BB-87 dies, you have 90 seconds to reach a safehouse. Your max cargo capacity drops to 10kg and Sam's stamina drains 3x faster.

5. All Endings

🌅 Ending A: Bridge Link (Best)

The chiral network connects the entire world. Sam retires as a porter. Unlocked: Connect all cities + deliver allLost Packages to the Beach Beyond.

💀 Ending B: Sam's Last Delivery

Sam sacrifices himself to seal the final BT rift. Heartbreaking. Unlocked: Refuse to use BB-87 in the final chapter.

🌊 Ending C: The Beach

Sam enters the Beach permanently, leaving behind the physical world. Cryptic and open to interpretation. Unlocked: Collect all 50 Sam's Memories (analog recordings).

6. Pro Tips

⭐ Always Build Structures

In the early game, prioritize road building over zip lines. Roads are permanent and benefit your entire game. Zips are team-dependent.

⭐ Don't Hoard Materials

Many players over-prepare and never use their materials. Use 50% of your stock building structures for other players. Your likes compound faster this way.

⭐ BB-87 Cryo Discipline

Keep BB-87 in cryo until you hit a BT zone. Constant pink eyes = stress accumulation. Spray cryo before every BT encounter — it's cheaper than losing BB-87.
