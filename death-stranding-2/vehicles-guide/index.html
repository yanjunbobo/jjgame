⚠️ The 5 Vehicle Mistakes

Mistake 1: Taking Vehicles Into Mountains Without Upgrade
Base vehicles break in 10 minutes on mountain terrain. Before taking any vehicle mountain, upgrade suspension to level 3+. Breaking a vehicle mid-route = lost cargo and hours of progress.
Mistake 2: Not Parking in Safe Places
Vehicles left outside chiral stations attract MULE raids. Always park inside chiral station boundaries or under roofs. A vehicle under MULE attack loses 30% cargo condition.
Mistake 3: Using the Same Vehicle for Everything
Hemingway is good at everything but great at nothing. For mountain routes, use Kodermaeier. For long flat routes, use Hemingway. Matching vehicle to terrain = 2x faster delivery times.
