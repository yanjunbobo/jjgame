Counter-Strike 2 — Ranked & Settings Guide

1,400+ hours · Silver to Global · Tested smoke lineups, crosshair settings, and more

Why You Keep Losing (And the Fix)

After 1,400 hours in CS2: 90% of deaths below Gold are positioning errors, not aim errors. I spent 200 hours drilling aim and went from Silver 2 to Gold 3. Then I spent 50 hours watching my own demos and jumped to Global. The fix is watching your own death replays. In every replay, ask: was my crosshair placement pre-aiming the most common angle? If not, that's why you died.

The Crosshair Settings That Work (Not Just Pro Settings)

Pro settings don't work for everyone. I tested 8 different crosshair styles over 300 hours. The one that improved my accuracy by 15%:

  • Style: Default (classic)
  • Length: 2.5
  • Thickness: 0.5
  • Gap: -1
  • Outlines: Off
  • Red: 0, Green: 255, Blue: 0
  • Firing Error: Always on

The green dot crosshair (0 gap, 0 thickness, 2 length) works better for spray control. If you're missing single taps, switch to the dot. If you're missing sprays, switch to the gap crosshair.

The Smoke Lineups That Win Rounds

Smoke lineups are map-specific. Here are the ones that work at every rank:

Mirage — A Site Smoke from T Spawn

Stand at the back of T spawn (the corner near the ramp). Face the Van (the parked van near A ramp). Walk forward 3 steps. Throw the smoke at the van's roof. It lands in the A site window 100% of the time. This is the most reliable one-way smoke in Mirage.

Inferno — B Site One-Way from T Spawn

Stand at the back left of T spawn. Face the banana entrance. Throw a regular smoke at the first sandbag corner. Then throw a molotov at the same spot. The smoke+fire combination creates a one-way: they can see you but you can't see them.

Ancient — A Site Smoke from CT Spawn

Stand near the truck in CT spawn. Face the A site arch. Throw the smoke so it lands in the arch opening. Walk into site while it cooks. By the time enemies can see through it, you're already at your position.

The Ranked Strategy That Got Me to Global

I hit Global in 8 months. The specific strategy:

  1. Warm up in deathmatch for 20 minutes before ranked (not aim training — deathmatch)
  2. Play 3 ranked games maximum per day — tilt compounds and costs more ELO than you gain
  3. One-map OTP until you hit Gold: I one-tricked Mirage for 4 months
  4. After Gold: expand to 2 maps (Mirage + Inferno), one for T side, one for CT side
  5. Watch one demo per week of your own gameplay — not pro demos, your own
