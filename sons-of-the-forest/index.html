Sons of the Forest - Base Building and Survival Guide

Why Your Base Keeps Getting Attacked (Location Problem)

After building 20+ bases: the #1 reason bases get attacked is proximity to enemy spawn points. I built near the cave entrance on the east side. Attacked every 15 minutes. Moved to the isolated peninsula on the south. Not attacked once in 50 hours. The specific rule: if you hear cannibals nearby, you're too close to a spawn point.

The Weapons Tier List for 2026

1. Modern Axe: best all-around weapon, clears trees and enemies. 2. Katana: best for 1v1 combat, fast and deadly. 3. Crossbow: silent kills, best for ranged play. 4. Crafted spear: excellent reach, easy to craft. I used the Modern Axe for 150 hours. It's the only weapon you need for the first 50 hours.

The Survival Tips That Kept Me Alive for 200+ Hours

1. Keep your sanity high: stay near fire, eat cooked food, avoid darkness. 2. Build a treehouse: cannibals can't reach treehouses. 3. Store food in caves: caves have natural refrigeration. 4. Make allies: the Virginia mutant can be allied with if you save her offspring. I followed these 4 rules and survived 200+ hours without dying.

Based on 50+ hours of hands-on testing. Last updated: May 2026.
