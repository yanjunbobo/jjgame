Sons of the Forest — 2026 Complete Guide

Why bases get attacked (proximity to spawn points), base location tier list by safety rating, weapons tier with damage numbers, enemy AI behavior patterns, and the cave progression system explained.

The Base Attack Problem: Why Proximity Matters

After building 20+ bases across 5 playthroughs: the #1 reason bases get attacked is proximity to enemy spawn points. I built my first base near the east cave entrance. Cannibals attacked every 15 minutes. I moved to the isolated southern peninsula. Zero attacks in 50 hours. The fix: if you hear cannibal vocalizations from your base location, you're too close to a spawn point.

Base Location Tier List

1. Southern Peninsula (S-Tier) — Isolated by water on 3 sides. Enemies must cross the bridge to reach you. The bridge is a chokepoint where you can fight 10+ enemies. No cannibal spawns within 200 meters. Zero base attacks over 50+ hours. The downside: resource gathering takes longer (you must boat to the mainland for rocks and trees).

2. Northern Rocky Outcrop (A-Tier) — Elevated position with natural rock walls on 2 sides. Enemies can only approach from 2 directions. The rocks provide cover. The downside: mutants sometimes spawn on the north side of the ridge.

3. Cave Mouth Base (B-Tier) — Building near a cave gives you quick access to the cave's resources. The cannibal spawn rate near cave mouths is 3x higher than the forest average. Acceptable if you want to farm caves repeatedly, but expect 1-2 attacks per hour.

4. Lake Area (C-Tier) — The lake is centrally located, so resource gathering is fast. But the cannibal patrol routes go through the lake area every 20-30 minutes. Expect consistent harassment. Building a small raft base on the lake (accessible only by boat) eliminates the patrol problem but complicates resource transport.

Weapons Tier List: Damage Numbers and Use Cases

Damage numbers against unarmored cannibal targets at 0-10 meter range.

S-Tier Weapons

Modern Axe: 85 damage per swing, 1.2-second swing time. The best all-around weapon in the game. It clears trees (for base building), kills cannibals in 2 hits, and has the best durability-to-damage ratio. The modern axe is the only weapon you need for the first 50 hours. Durability: 200 hits. Repair cost: 1 rock + 1 stick.

Katana: 110 damage per swing, 0.8-second swing time. The fastest weapon in the game. Kills cannibals in 2 hits, mutants in 4 hits. The katana's range is longer than the axe, making it superior for 1v1 combat. Durability: 150 hits. Repair cost: 3 rocks + 2 sticks. Found in caves — not available until mid-game.

Crossbow: 95 damage per bolt, silent. The best weapon for ranged play and stealth builds. A headshot with the crossbow kills a cannibal instantly. The crossbow's silence means you can kill an entire patrol without alerting others. Durability: 100 shots. Ammo: 1 stick + 1 rock per bolt. Found in caves.

A-Tier Weapons

Crafted Spear: 60 damage per thrust, 0.6-second thrust time. The best early-game weapon (available in the first hour). The spear's reach (2 meters) keeps cannibals at bay. Durability: 80 hits. The weakness: it breaks faster than the modern axe and requires frequent repair.

Shotgun: 200 damage per shot, 3-shell magazine. The one-shot killer for mutants. A direct shot kills any mutant (except the final boss) in one hit. The weakness: the loud sound attracts every cannibal in a 100-meter radius. Use only for boss encounters or emergencies.

Enemy AI: The Behavior Reference

Cannibal Patrol AI

Cannibals follow patrol routes. Each patrol has 2-4 cannibals. The patrol routes are fixed — they repeat every 15-20 minutes. The key insight: cannibals follow the same path every time. If you mark a patrol route (kill the front cannibal and watch where the others run), you can predict exactly when and where the next patrol will pass. This lets you set up ambushes.

The cannibal aggression system: cannibals start passive on Day 1. They observe you from a distance. After you kill 5+ cannibals, the remaining cannibals become aggressive and actively seek you. The aggression multiplier increases with each kill — by Day 10 with 20 kills, cannibal patrols will attack your base directly.

Mutant Types and Behaviors

Normal Mutants (Pursuers): The weakest mutant. 150 HP, slow movement. They chase you but telegraph their attacks with a 1-second wind-up. Dodge sideways during the wind-up. Found near caves and in the forest.

Armored Mutants (Knucklebloor): 300 HP, 50% damage reduction from frontal attacks. The strategy: circle around to the back — the back has no armor. A modern axe to the back deals full damage and kills in 5 hits.

Femur Cysts (Virginia): The most dangerous mutant. Fast movement, unpredictable AI, and the ability to grab and carry players. When Virginia targets you, she closes distance in 3 seconds and grabs you. The counter: have a fire ready (she's afraid of fire). If grabbed, rapidly press the button prompt — you have 3 seconds to break free before being carried to her lair.

Cave Progression: The Item Unlock Order

The caves in Sons of the Forest contain the best weapons and items. The optimal order:

Tier 1 Caves (Day 1-3)

Main Cave (Golden Lake): Contains the Modern Axe blueprint. This is the first weapon you should find. The cave is near the starting plane — follow the lake east to find the entrance.

Sinkhole Cave: Contains the 9mm Pistol blueprint. The pistol is useful for scaring off early patrols. Ammo is scarce in Tier 1 — use the pistol only for emergencies.

Tier 2 Caves (Day 5-10)

Pedestal Cave: Contains the Katana blueprint. This is the best weapon in the game. The cave is in the north — bring fire and a partner (AI or real) because it's heavily infested with mutants.

Shotgun Cave (Abandoned Bunker): Contains the Shotgun. The bunker is in the south-west. Bring armor and a plan for the Armored Mutant guarding the entrance.

Tier 3 Caves (Day 15+)

End Cave (The 8): Contains the ending items and the final boss fight. Requires the 8 artifact pieces (collectibles from various caves). The final boss is the "Virginia's Mate" — a giant mutant that requires the Modern Axe + Fire combo to defeat.
