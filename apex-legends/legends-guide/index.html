Apex Legends All Legends Tier List 2026 - Complete Rankings | PlayVault

Every Apex Legends legend ranked. Complete tier list with Bangalore, Newcastle, Valkyrie analysis. May 2026.

🏃 All Legends Ranked 2026

Apex Legends features 25+ Legends, each with unique abilities that create complex synergy and counter relationships. This comprehensive guide ranks every legend based on current Season 20+ meta, covering Assault, Recon, Controller, and Support classes.

Recon Legends

Valkyrie (S): The gold standard for recon mobility. Her ultimate enables complete squad repositioning in one activation. Synergizes with every playstyle. Weakness: no defensive tools if caught mid-rotation.

Crypto (A): Hidden powerhouse in coordinated teams. The drone provides real-time enemy positions without exposing Crypto. Drone EMP combo with Wattson ult is championship-proven. Weakness: requires team coordination to utilize effectively.

Bloodhound (B): The most beginner-friendly recon. Their scan reveals enemies through walls for 1.5 seconds. The ultimate provides triple movement speed. Weakness: Audio signature is the loudest in the game during Beast of the Hunt.

Seer (B-): Wallhack through tactical is powerful but requires precise timing. The ultimate provides zoning during final circles. Weakness: No mobility options and tactical can be countered by Bangalores.

Assault Legends

Bangalore (S): After her rework, Bangalore provides the best all-around Assault package. 30% sprint speed while firing is a game-changer. The smoke + passive combo enables aggressive pushes others can't attempt.

Ash (A): The passive intel from death boxes is underrated. Her ultimate provides rotation capability without sacrificing combat readiness. Weakness: Tactical requires line-of-sight, limiting use cases.

Mad Maggie (B+): The passive tracking of recent damage is valuable information. Her ultimate disruption against fortified positions is situational but powerful. Weakness: No self-revive passive limits aggressive play.

Fuse (B): Grenade-centric kit is devastating against grouped enemies. The ultimate height advantage in close-quarters is significant. Weakness: No vertical mobility tools, slowest legend movement speed.

Horizon (B-): Gravity lift remains one of the best tactical abilities. The ultimate is championship-level crowd control. Weakness: Gravity lift was significantly nerfed, making her less dominant than in previous seasons.

Controller Legends

Newcastle (S): The mobile shield mechanic changed defensive play. Dragonfly drone revive through walls is unique and powerful. Weakness: Shield health was reduced in Season 20, requiring more careful positioning.

Wattson (A): The most devastating defensive legend in organized play. Intercepting ordnance and regenerating fences creates layered defense. Weakness: No offensive capability, dependent on team protection during setup.

Gibraltar (B+): The arm shield provides passive advantage in every 1v1. Gun Shield is the best passive in the game. Weakness: Large hitbox makes him the easiest target at range.

Caustic (B): Gas areas block enemy pathing through doorways. The visual cover is excellent for reviving. Weakness: Gas deals self-damage during certain scenarios, and most teams have a Bangalore counter.

Rampart (C): Sheila is devastating in static defense. Amped cover provides unbeatable positions in final circles. Weakness: Complete lack of mobility makes her only viable in specific compositions.

Support Legends

Newcastle (S-tier in Recon above): Unique dual-class placement due to Dragonfly revive mechanic.

Lifeline (A): D.O.C. healing drone provides sustained passive healing. The combat revive is irreplaceable in solo queue. Weakness: No mobility tools and passive drone can be destroyed.

Gibraltar (see Controller above): Gun Shield passive makes him a Support-Controller hybrid.

Mirage (B): The decoy tactical is excellent for misinformation. Bamboozle ult is underrated in chaotic firefights. Weakness: No utility against coordinated teams who check every position.

Octane (B-): 30-second Stim enables aggressive play without health penalty. Jump pad provides team mobility. Weakness: No defensive tools, loud audio signature.

Loba (C+): Black Market is incredible in late-game for arming the squad. The tactical teleport is powerful escape tool. Weakness: Ultimate charges slowly, bracelet requires precise positioning.

Wraith (C): Phase Walk remains excellent for 1v1 and escape. Portal is the best rotation ability historically. Weakness: No meaningful utility in Season 20 meta, outclassed by Valkyrie.

Pathfinder (C-): The grapple is the highest-skill-ceiling ability in the game. Mastered Pathfinder dominates lobbies. Weakness: Passive scan is inferior to Crypto's drone in organized play.

Vantage (D+): Unique echo mechanic is interesting but situational. Sniper-based kit doesn't fit current meta. Weakness: No utility for team play, lowest win rate in Masters lobbies.

Alter (D): Despite the intel denial being theoretically powerful, practical application shows below-average performance. Weakness: No combat effectiveness, purely utility-dependent.
