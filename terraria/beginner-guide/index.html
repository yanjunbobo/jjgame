📅 Day 1-7 Survival Guide

Day 1 (Morning): Cut trees, gather 100+ wood. Build a small 6x6 house with a door, light source, and table. Place it at least 20 blocks from spawn.
Day 1 (Afternoon): Explore the surface. Find the Merchant NPC location. Collect 20+ grass seeds for later farming.
Day 1 (Night - IMPORTANT): STAY INSIDE. Zombies and skeletons spawn at night. Wait until dawn before going out.
Day 2-3: Mine. Find copper/tin ore (surface), then iron (underground, level 15+). Build a Furnace. Craft iron tools.
Day 4-7: Go deeper (level 30+). Find silver/tungsten. Build better armor. Fight the Eye of Cthulhu on Day 3 night (if HP > 200).

⛏️ Mining Priority (Level Guide)

  • Levels 1-15: Copper, Iron, gems. Build a 6-block-wide staircase down. Collect 50+ of each ore.
  • Levels 16-30: Silver, Gold. Build parallel staircases. Collect 100+ of each.
  • Levels 31-50: Meteorite (if crashed), Hellstone (deep). Hellstone requires lava walking boots (Obsidian Skull from 20 obsidian).

⚠️ The 5 Beginner Mistakes (Terraria Wiki)

Mistake 1: Building Base at Spawn (always griefed)
New players build their first house at spawn. The moon lord and events spawn at spawn. Build 30+ blocks away minimum.
Mistake 2: Not Making a Chest System Early
Organize loot immediately. Use chests labeled by material. When you die and respawn, you'll thank your past self.
Mistake 3: Fighting Wall of Flesh Too Early
The Wall of Flesh activates Hardmode and triples world difficulty. Make sure you have Molten armor (30+ platinum hours) before entering the underworld.
Mistake 4: Wasting Money on Potion Pub
Potion Pub costs 50 gold. That's 10% of your early game wealth. Craft potions yourself: 1 iron ore + 1 mushroom = healing potion.
Mistake 5: Digging Straight Down
The #1 cause of death in Terraria: digging straight down into lava or falling into caves. Always dig stairs or use platforms when descending.
