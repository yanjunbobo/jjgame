📅 Hardmode Progression Timeline

Week 1 Hardmode (Immediately after WoF):

  • Destroy 3 Orbs at the Underworld (or 1 Orbs + 3 altars)
  • Mine Adamantite/Titanium ore (spawns on surface after orb destruction)
  • Build Adamantite armor (set bonus: +25% damage)
  • Get Titan Gauntlet (best pre-Mechanic fists in game)

Week 2-4 Hardmode:

  • Find Mechanic NPC (underground, requires 1 empty house)
  • Build Dynamite Kit (from Mechanic, 10 gold) for faster mining
  • Defeat The Twins (recommended level: Adamantite armor + ranged weapon)
  • Get Daedalus Stormbow + Holy Arrows for Skeletron Prime

Month 2 Hardmode:

  • Defeat Skeletron Prime + Destroyer (recommended: Hallowed armor)
  • Fish forCraken (Hardmode Summoner weapon) in the ocean
  • Build chlorophyte farm for endgame weapons

⚠️ The 5 Hardmode Mistakes

Mistake 1: Not Making a Jungle Farm Before Hardmode
The Jungle is the most valuable biome post-WoF. Brimstone Elementals drop Flames that are essential for crafting. Make a jungle farm BEFORE breaking the wall.
Mistake 2: Destroying All Orbs/Altars at Once
Every orb destroyed spawns a Corrupt/CrimsonBiome (spreads). Destroying all 3 orbs at once creates massive corruption you can't contain. Destroy 1 per session and immediately build containment walls.
Mistake 3: Fighting The Twins Before Getting Adamantite Armor
The Twins have 28,000 HP combined. Pre-hardmode armor = 18 defense max. Adamantite armor = 36 defense + 25% damage boost. You need both to survive the laser attacks.
Mistake 4: Not Building a Hoik Farm Early
Hoik (1-wide tunnels) make infinite item farms 10x faster. Build one for light mobs, metal detectors, and potion ingredients. Every hour spent building a hoik farm saves 50+ hours of grinding.
Mistake 5: Waiting Too Long to Fight Plantera
Plantera spawns after 3 Mechanical Bosses are defeated. Waiting too long means the Jungle spreads and hardmode enemies take over. Fight Plantera within 3 days of getting Mechanic — you'll thank yourself.
