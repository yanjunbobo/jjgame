📊 Boss Order & Recommended Loadouts

Pre-Hardmode Bosses
Eye of Cthulhu (Level 10+): Lead Broadsword + 100 HP + Wooden Platform. Strategy: build a 30-block-wide arena. Stand on the platform, slash when it dives, move sideways when it swoops. Reward: Demonite Ore + Terror/proof you can play.
Eater of Worlds (Level 15+): Better tools from Eye + 150 HP. Strategy: USE YOURWORLD'S NATURAL CAVES. Don't fight in open areas — use cave networks as chokepoints. The boss is longer than your screen, so lure each segment through tight passages.
Skeletron (Level 20+): Gold armor (15 defense) + Fiery Greatsword. Strategy: This is a DPS race. Stand still, don't panic, maintain damage. The hands are a distraction — go straight for the head. When you hear the "roar" (telegraph for spin attack), jump to avoid.

⚠️ The 5 Boss Mistakes

Mistake 1: Fighting Multiple Bosses at Once
After you kill Skeletron, the Dungeon Guardian spawns if you enter the dungeon before killing Plantera. This is a one-shot killer. Kill Plantera FIRST, then enter the dungeon. The Dungeon Guardian cannot be killed with any weapon in the game.
Mistake 2: Not Using Summoner Armor Pre-Hardmode
The Spider armor (summoner, from Spiders in the Underground) gives your minions +25% damage before hardmode. Most players ignore summoner pre-hardmode and struggle with boss fights. Spider armor is easier to get than Melee armor and makes bosses trivial.
Mistake 3: Fighting Plantera Without the Gardener
Plantera spawns in the Jungle. The Gardener (from the Merchant) lets you pick up active Plantera bulbs. Without it, you fight Plantera wherever it spawns. With it, you can create a flat arena in the jungle and fight on your terms. Always buy the Gardener before entering hardmode jungle.
Mistake 4: Not Having 400+ HP for Moon Lord
Moon Lord has 4 damage phases and the death beams deal 40 damage per hit. At 200 HP, you die in 5 hits. At 400 HP with Witherless Armor and Ironskin potion, you survive 12+ hits. Never fight Moon Lord below 350 HP.
Mistake 5: Using Pre-Hardmode Weapons in Hardmode
Night's Edge (the best pre-hardmode sword) deals 40 damage. The Seashell (pre-hardmode, crafted) deals 80 damage. In hardmode, even the weakest hardmode enemy has 50 HP. Your pre-hardmode weapons deal 20-40 damage. This is not enough — you must upgrade weapons immediately.
