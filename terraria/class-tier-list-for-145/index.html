Terraria's progression is one of the most satisfying in gaming. After 300 hours across 1.4.4 and 1.4.5, here's the guide to every phase.

The Class Tier List (For 1.4.5)

Pre-Hardmode S-Tier: Summoner (the Imp staff + 13 summons = the safest playstyle — your summons deal damage while you move, meaning you take 50% less damage than melee or ranged characters), Melee (high damage but requires standing still, making you take 2x the damage).

Hardmode S-Tier: Ranger (theChlorophyte Shotbow + Ichor arrows = the best general-purpose damage in hardmode), Summoner (the Stardust Dragon Staff = the best single-target damage in hardmode, 100+ DPS with the right setup).

The Boss Order That Makes Hardmode Easy

The most common mistake: players fight Skeletron Prime first in Hardmode. This is wrong. The correct order: Destroyer first (easiest — use the Hallowed Flail or Daedalus Stormbow), then Skeletron Prime (requires the best armor to survive), then Plantera (requires specific arena setup).

The specific reason Destroyer first: he's a worm, meaning the fight is purely about not getting hit while your weapons DPS him. With the right weapons (Daedalus Stormbow + Holy Arrows), you deal 150 DPS and he dies in 2 minutes. Skeletron Prime requires the Necromancer's Scroll + best armor + good dodgers. Destroyer just requires good weapons.

The Summoner Build That Dominates Pre-Hardmode

The Imp Staff + 13 summons + the Papyrus Scarab (a mount that gives 15% summon damage) = the most broken pre-hardmode setup. In 50 boss fights with this setup, I died exactly once (to Skeletron Prime because I didn't dodge his lasers). Every other boss was trivial.
