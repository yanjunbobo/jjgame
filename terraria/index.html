⚔️ Boss Progression & Class-Specific Endgame Builds

Terraria's boss progression determines your character's power curve. This guide covers the optimal boss order, class-specific endgame builds for all four classes, and the specific weapons that carry through each phase in 2026.

Boss Order & Progression

King Slime: First boss. Drops 10-15 platinum. Gear up with Gold or Platinum armor. The Ninja gear from King Slime is the best pre-hardmode ranged gear.

Eye of Cthulhu: Second boss. Build an arena 80 blocks wide with platforms at 3 heights. The Vampire Knives from this boss carry through early hardmode.

Brain of Cthulhu / Eater of Worlds: Third boss. Choose based on corruption/crimson spawn. The Tizona (from Brain) is the best sword until late hardmode.

Skeletron: Fourth boss. Unlocks the dungeon. Build a skybridge before fighting—Skeletron's spin attack is impossible to dodge without mobility.

Wall of Flesh: Hardmode gateway. Enter hardmode with the Pwnhammer for maximum utility. The Hardmode ore tier starts immediately after.

Melee Endgame Build

Zenith: Best sword in the game. Crafted from 6 previous swords. Requires Solar Fragment from Moon Lord. This single weapon carries you through all remaining content.

Solar Eruption: Secondary weapon. The chain flail with CC is essential for Expert Mode bosses. The AoE damage from the whip portion is significant.
