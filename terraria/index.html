⚠️ Hardmode Mistakes (Wiki Edit History Analysis)

Mistake 1: Not Breaking Altars (you can't get Chlorophyte)
After defeating the Wall of Flesh, break 3 Demon Altars with a Pwnhammer. Each altar gives you one ore type (Cobalt, Mythril, Adamantite). Not breaking them means you never get Chlorophyte in Hardmode.
Mistake 2: Fighting Destroyer First (you need Summoner gear)
The Destroyer has 80,000 HP and spawns probes that drain defense. Before Hardmode boss #1, build aarena with campfires + heart lanterns. Summon minions with 5+ minions and a Flask of Party.
Mistake 3: Mining During Blood Moon (you die for no reason)
During Blood Moon, enemies spawn at 4x the normal rate. If you're underground during Blood Moon, you will die without the right gear. Plan your mining sessions for day time only in Hardmode.
