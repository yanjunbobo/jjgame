⚔️ Class Builds & Progression

Terraria's four class system defines playstyle for every playthrough. This guide covers optimal loadouts for each class through all progression phases, from pre-hardmode to Moon Lord in 2026.

Melee Build

Pre-Hardmode: Start with Gold Broadsword for the reach. The Nimbus Rod provides ranged capability while melee develops. The Seedler at Plantera is the best pre-hm melee weapon.

Post-Plantera: The Solar Eruption is the best melee weapon for most of Hardmode. The Flairon provides superior crowd control. The Meowmere is the best throw weapon.

Endgame: The Zenith is the absolute best melee weapon. The Star Wrath is the best non-crafted alternative. The Zenith's 200%+ melee speed bonus from True melee is unmatched.

Summoner Build

Pre-Hardmode: The Finch Staff is the first summon. The Slime Staff provides passive summon damage. The Spider Staff at Spider Caves is the best pre-hm option.

Hardmode: The Destroformabilizer is the best pre-Plantera summon. The Pirahna Gun provides ranged summon backup. Post-Plantera, the Tempest Staff dominates.

Endgame: The Stardust Dragon Staff is the best single summon. The Stardust Cell Staff provides additional burst. Multiple summons enable content impossible with single-summon builds.
