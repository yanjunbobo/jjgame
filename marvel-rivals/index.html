🦸 Game Overview & Team Synergy Basics

Marvel Rivals is a 6v6 hero shooter featuring Marvel's most iconic characters. This guide covers game fundamentals, role classifications, and basic team synergy principles for new players in 2026.

Role Classifications

Duelist: High damage dealers who excel at eliminating enemy players. Examples: Iron Man, Spider-Man, Hulk, Thor. Duelists are the primary damage source but lack support capabilities.

Strategist: Healers and support characters who sustain the team. Examples: Mantis, Adam Warlock. Strategists enable longer engagements through healing and buff application.

Vanguard: Tanks who create space and protect allies. Examples: Captain America, Peni Parker. Vanguards are the frontline that enables damage dealers to operate safely.

Team Composition Requirements

Every team needs at least one Vanguard for frontline presence. At least one Strategist is recommended for sustain. The remaining slots are flexible Duelist picks based on enemy composition.

The optimal composition is 2 Vanguard + 1 Strategist + 3 Duelist. This provides enough frontline to create space, enough healing to survive, and enough damage to eliminate enemies.

Basic Synergy Principles

Synergy comes from ability combinations that create value beyond individual performance. Iron Man's sustained damage pairs with Mantis's healing for extended damage phases. Spider-Man's disruption pairs with Thor's crowd control for elimination windows.

Counter-pick is more important than meta picks. If the enemy has a strong Iron Man, bring a Spider-Man to flank and eliminate him. Raw counter-play outperforms pure individual strength.
