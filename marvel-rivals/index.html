Marvel Rivals - Team Comps and Tier List Guide

Why Your Team Keeps Losing (No Synergy)

After 500+ hours: the #1 team loss condition is having no synergy between characters. A team of individually strong characters loses to a team with strong synergies. The specific example: Thor + Iron Man = 30% more damage because they trigger a synergy bonus. A team of 6 individually S-tier characters without synergies will lose to 6 B-tier characters with proper synergies.

The Tier List: What's Actually S-Tier in Season 1

1. Dr. Strange + Spider-Man: portal + web combo is broken. 2. Thor + Iron Man: ranged + melee cover all situations. 3. Luna Snow + Peni Parker: best support + best tank combo. I climbed to Diamond using Dr. Strange + Spider-Man. The portal + web combo wins 80% of team fights.

The Counter Picks That Shut Down Every Meta Team

1. vs. Dr. Strange combo: pick Hulk (green skin = immune to mystic) or Venom (web immunity). 2. vs. Thor + Iron Man: pick Hawkeye (ranged, ignores armor). 3. vs. Luna Snow: pick Thanos (bypass shields). The specific insight: counter picks are more important than individual character strength in Marvel Rivals.
