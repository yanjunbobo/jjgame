🦸 Counter-Pick Strategy & Team Synergies

Marvel Rivals is won through counter-picking and team composition more than individual mechanical skill. This guide covers the complete counter-pick system, team synergy charts, and optimal hero selections for every matchup in 2026.

Counter-Pick Matrix

Iron Man Counters: Spider-Man is the best counter—he flanks while Iron Man focuses frontlines. Peni Parker's web nest zones Iron Man from optimal damage positions. Captain America's shield bash interrupts Iron Man's sustained damage.

Adam Warlock Counters: Thor's lightning disrupts Warlock's passive charging. Hulk's massive HP pool outlasts Warlock's damage. Peni Parker's stun stops the healing channel.

Spider-Man Counters: Mantis's stun interrupts web combos. Adam Warlock's shield prevents the burst. Captain America's shield bash punishes overextension.

Team Synergy Chart

Core Trio: Captain America + Iron Man + Mantis. The most balanced composition. Cap engages, Iron Man provides sustained damage, Mantis sustains.

Dive Comp: Thor + Spider-Man + Adam Warlock. Aggressive dive potential with Warlock's sustain enabling deep plays. Requires communication to execute properly.

Poke Comp: Iron Man + Doctor Strange + Mantis. Range dominance with zone control. Doctor Strange's portal enables team repositioning while Iron Man maintains pressure.

Map-Specific Picks

Nidavellir: High-ground heroes (Iron Man, Thor) dominate due to the vertical map design. Avoid close-range heroes in the open areas.

Tokyo 2099: The enclosed spaces favor rushdown comps. Spider-Man and Hulk excel in the narrow corridors.
