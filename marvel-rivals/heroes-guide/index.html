🦸 All Heroes Abilities Guide

Marvel Rivals features 30+ heroes across the Marvel universe. This guide covers every hero's abilities, role classification, and synergy recommendations for team composition in 2026.

Duelist Heroes

Iron Man: Primary: Repulsor Beam (continuous damage at range). Secondary: Unibeam (cone damage). Ultimate: Proton Cannon (massive area damage). Iron Man dominates open maps with his sustained damage output. The key is maintaining distance—close-range combat weakens his effectiveness significantly.

Spider-Man: Primary: Web Shooters (rapid close-range damage). Secondary: Web Bomb (AoE stun). Ultimate: Maximum Spider (team web-slinging transport). Spider-Man is the premier flanker, excelling at disrupting backline damage dealers. Use web swing for map mobility and ambush from unexpected angles.

Hulk: Primary: Gamma Slam (melee AoE damage). Secondary: Gamma Burst (ranged burst damage). Ultimate: Worldbreaker (massive frontal AoE + knockback). Hulk is the premier tank buster. His damage output against tanks is unmatched, but his large hitbox makes him vulnerable to focus fire.

Thor: Primary: Mjolnir Strike (melee damage). Secondary: Lightning Bolt (ranged damage). Ultimate: God of Thunder (lightning storm over large area). Thor's versatility makes him a safe pick in any composition. His lightning provides both single-target and AoE damage.

Strategist Heroes

Mantises: Primary: Lifeweaver (healing pulse). Secondary: Rest (single-target heal over time). Ultimate: Crystal Guidance (team damage boost). Mantis is the primary healer with the highest HPS. Position behind tanks for maximum healing throughput.

Adam Warlock: Primary: Soul Harvest (damage + self-heal). Secondary: Karma (damage reflect shield). Ultimate: Rift (mass healing for allies). Warlock rewards aggressive play—landing hits builds his passive heal. The ultimate is one of the best team-saving abilities in the game.

Vanguard Heroes

Captain America: Primary: Shield Bash (melee combo). Secondary: Stars and Stripes (ranged bouncing attack). Ultimate: Super Soldier (team damage resistance + rush). Captain America is the most well-rounded tank. His shield provides point control while his mobility enables aggressive engages.

Peni Parker: Primary: Web Shooter (rapid fire). Secondary: Web Blast (stun). Ultimate: Spider's Rest (massive AoE stun + damage). Peni excels at controlling chokepoints. Her nest provides area denial while her mobility enables rapid repositioning.

Team Synergy Recommendations

Composition 1: Captain America + Iron Man + Mantis provides balanced frontline damage with consistent healing. Cap engages while Iron Man provides ranged damage from behind. Mantis keeps everyone alive.

Composition 2: Thor + Spider-Man + Adam Warlock creates aggressive dive potential. Thor's crowd control enables Spider-Man's assassination attempts. Warlock sustains the dive with his self-healing passive.

Composition 3: Hulk + Peni Parker + Mantis dominates defensive positions. Hulk absorbs damage while Peni's nest provides area control. Mantis ensures sustained healing during extended engagements.
