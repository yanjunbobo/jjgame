🦸 Hero Tier List 2026

Marvel Rivals tier list reflects current meta performance in both competitive and quick play. This ranking considers team utility, individual damage, and ease of execution across all skill levels in 2026.

S-Tier Heroes

Iron Man: The most impactful damage dealer in the current meta. Sustained beam damage combined with massive ultimate potential makes him a priority pick. His weakness is vulnerability to flankers and the need for team protection to maximize damage time.

Adam Warlock: The best support in the game. His self-sustain through Soul Harvest combined with the best team ultimate in the game makes him indispensable. Coordinated teams build around his passive charge mechanic for maximum value.

Captain America: The gold standard for Vanguard play. His shield control and team ultimate provide utility no other tank can match. He enables team compositions that would be otherwise impossible due to his protective presence.

A-Tier Heroes

Spider-Man: The premier flanker after recent mobility buffs. His ability to disrupt backline supports and damage dealers is unmatched. Requires skill to execute but rewards practiced players with consistent eliminations.

Thor: The most versatile hero in the roster. His lightning provides both single-target and AoE damage while his ultimate provides team-wide control. Works in virtually any composition.

Mantises: The highest raw healing output. Her consistency makes her the go-to healer for teams that want reliable sustain. Her weakness is limited defensive tools against divers.

Hulk: The best tank buster after damage buffs. His matchup against other Vanguards is dominant. His weakness is the large hitbox making him vulnerable to coordinated focus fire.

B-Tier Heroes

Peni Parker: Excellent at chokepoint control but struggles in open maps. Her ultimate is among the best in the game but her base kit requires specific map conditions to excel.

Doctor Strange: Strong ultimate but inconsistent base kit. Portal mobility is valuable but his damage output doesn't match other damage dealers.

Winter Soldier: Solid all-around performer without exceptional strengths. The drone provides valuable utility but his damage ceiling is lower than other duelists.
