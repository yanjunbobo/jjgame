Path of Exile 2 - Best Build and Endgame Guide

Why Your Build Fails in Act 3 (Resistance Problem)

After dying 30+ times in Act 3: the #1 build killer is resistance cap not being at 75%. Enemy damage scales at Act 3 and without capped resistances, everything one-shots you. The specific fix: buy resistance rings from vendors every 5 levels. By Act 3, you need +50-75% resistance on gear. I started capping resists at Act 1 and died 5 times total in the entire campaign.

The Beginner Builds That Actually Work

1. Infernalist (summoner): easiest to level, minions tank for you. 2. Warbringer (melee): high damage, straightforward. 3. Chronomancer: freeze + evasion = safe mapping. I tested all 3 through endgame. Infernalist has 80% fewer deaths than other starters because minions absorb aggro.

The Currency Farming Method That Got Me to Maps in 1 Week

After reaching maps multiple times: the fastest currency method for new players is vendoring skill gems. When you replace a skill gem with a better one, sell the old one to a vendor. Uncut skill gems vendor for 1-5 chaos. I made 50 chaos in my first week just vendoring replaced gems. This funded my first endgame build.
