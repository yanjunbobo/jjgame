💀 Best Starter Builds 2026

Path of Exile 2's six character classes each offer multiple viable starter builds for endgame progression. This guide covers the most reliable builds for new players and experienced exiles alike in Early Access 2026.

Warrior Class Starters

Slam Earthquake: The most beginner-friendly Warrior build. Earthquake deals massive area damage with built-in AoE scaling. The playstyle involves maintaining Fortify through melee hits while spamming earthquakes on grouped enemies. Clear speed is exceptional due to ground effect stacking. Defensive layers include Fortify, armor, and high life pool.

Boneshatter Juggernaut: High damage with excellent survivability. The trauma mechanic deals bonus damage for each stack, rewarding sustained combat. The Juggernaut ascendancy provides unstoppable and perfect accuracy scaling. Weakness is the self-damage component requiring careful management.

Ranger Class Starters

Lightning Arrow Deadeye: The fastest mapping build in the current meta. Lightning Arrow chains through enemies, creating devastating clear patterns. The Deadeye ascendancy provides projectile duplication for exponential damage. Movement speed is exceptional, enabling rapid endgame farming.

Toxic Rain Champion: The most tanky DoT build available. Toxic Rain's ground pods dealChaos damage over time without requiring direct hit confirmation. The Champion ascendancy provides fortify on hit and free fortify mastery. Clear speed is good but not exceptional.

Witch Class Starters

Spark Hierophant: The best spell-casting starter. Spark projectiles pierce through enemies with the Hierophant buffs. The belt shrine provides permanent 40% more damage. Defensive layers include energy shield and the Sanctuary of Thought keystone.

Cold DoT Occultist: The premier Chaos and Cold hybrid. Vortex provides consistent damage application while Bane applies curses. The Wicked Ward keystone enables permanent energy shield. Excellent for Map boss fights with consistent DoT damage.

Build Selection Guide

Choose Slam Earthquake if you prefer melee with AoE clearing. Choose Lightning Arrow if you prioritize speed and farming efficiency. Choose Spark Hierophant if you enjoy spell-casting from safety.

The key to starter builds is requiring minimal unique items. All recommended builds function with rare gear through Act 10 and into early Maps. The transition to endgame builds requires specific unique items that should be your farming goal.
