💰 Currency & Farming Guide

Path of Exile 2's currency system is the most complex in ARPG history. This guide explains every currency type, optimal farming strategies, and how to accumulate wealth efficiently in Early Access 2026.

Core Currency Types

Scrolls of Wisdom: The basic ID scroll. Always in high demand. Farm by running Acts with Wisdom bonus modifiers on Maps. Keep 500+ in reserve at all times.

Orbs of Transmutation: Used to upgrade magic items to rare. The primary crafting currency for early progression. Abundant in Acts and Maps. Spend freely on gear upgrades through Act 8.

Orbs of Alteration: Reroll magic item modifiers. Essential for crafting specific modifier combinations. Higher value than Transmutation—use selectively on items with 2-3 desired modifier slots.

Orbs of Alchemy: Upgrade rare items and add one random modifier. The bridge between magic and rare item crafting. Significant value for chaos recipe early in leagues.

Chaos Orbs: The primary trade currency. Used to reroll rare items completely. Worth approximately 80-100 Wisdom scrolls in trade. Always save 20+ for emergency gear upgrades.

Divine Orbs: Reroll implicit modifiers on items. Extremely rare and valuable. Worth 80-120 Chaos Orbs. Never use on items below 3-modifier rare items.

Farming Strategies

Chaos Recipe: Exchange full rare equipment sets for Chaos Orbs. Requires specific item level ranges. The most reliable early-league farming method. Run Desert for efficient set collection.

Map Clear Speed: Speed through low-tier Maps collecting valuable base types. Jewelry and specific weapon bases worth 2-5 Chaos each. Always pick up identified items with 3+ useful modifiers.

Unique Farming: Certain Maps have elevated unique drop rates. Tower Map and Primordial Pool Map are known high-value unique locations. Run these when available in your Map pool.

Divination Card Farming: Specific Divination Cards drop from specific content. "The Hoarder" drops in Act 8-10. "Destined" cards drop from Act bosses. Collect and sell complete sets for significant profit.

Economy Tips

Price your items at 1 Chaos floor. If something might sell for more, list it for 2-3 Chaos. Items that don't sell in 2 days reduce to 1 Chaos or vendor.

Always run Maps with 20%+ quality for bonus item quantity. The item quantity modifier dramatically increases drop rates of valuable items.

The best investment in early league is Map sustain. Having 20+ Maps banked prevents progression stalls and enables pushing to higher Map tiers for better returns.
