Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

This is a plain-language description of what happens when you visit PlayVault. We keep it short because legal boilerplate helps nobody.

What We Collect

We don't collect personal data. PlayVault is a static content site. We don't have a login system, we don't ask for your email, and we don't store any information about individual visitors. There are no user accounts, no tracking profiles, and no behavioral data collection.

The only external services loaded on this site are Google AdSense (for monetization) and Tailwind CSS (for styling). These services may collect their own data according to their respective privacy policies. Google has their own privacy policy, which you can read at policies.google.com/privacy.

Cookies

We don't set our own cookies. Google AdSense may use cookies for ad personalization if you have personalized ads enabled in your Google account settings. You can opt out of personalized advertising through the Google Ads Settings page.

Third-Party Links

Some guide pages link to external resources — YouTube channels, community forums, tool sites. We don't control those sites and we're not responsible for their privacy practices. When you click a link to an external site, that site operates under its own privacy policy.

Children's Privacy

PlayVault is not directed at children under 13. We don't knowingly collect information from anyone under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal data, please contact us through the contact form and we'll delete it.

Changes to This Policy

If we change how we use Google AdSense or add new services that collect data, we'll update this page and note the revision date above. For minor wording changes that don't affect your rights, we may not announce them.

Your Rights

Because we don't collect personal data, there's not much to exercise. You have the right to browse this site without providing any information to us. If you're in the EU or UK, you have additional rights under GDPR — but since we don't process your data, there's nothing to exercise beyond browsing.

If you have questions about this policy, the contact page is the best way to reach us.
