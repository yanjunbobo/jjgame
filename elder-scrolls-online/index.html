Elder Scrolls Online - Class and Build Guide

Why Your DPS Is Low (It's Not Your Class)

After testing every class at 50 CP: the #1 DPS mistake is not weaving light attacks between abilities. A light attack deals 20-30% of your total damage but most players cast 5 abilities in a row without a light attack. My parse went from 25K to 45K simply by weaving a light attack after every ability. That's 80% more damage for the same effort.

The Champion Point System Explained

CP is not about spending points - it's about min-maxing the exact nodes you need. The optimal setup for DPS: 100 points in Lethalness (weapon damage), 60 points in Spell Erosion (penetration), 40 points in Elemental Expert (damage bonus). I wasted 200 CP on the wrong nodes before learning this. Use the ESO console to test different allocations.

The 3 Builds That Dominate in PvP

1. MagDK (Magicka Dragonknight): AoE burst, best for large-scale PvP. 2. StamSorc (Stamina Sorcerer): mobility, best for gank play. 3. Necro: sustain and resurrection utility. I tested all 3 in cyrodiil for 50 hours each. MagDK dominates large fights, StamSorc dominates small ganks.
