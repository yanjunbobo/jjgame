💨 Essential Smoke Spots — Every CT Site Setup

Mirage — A Site

The 3-smoke A site setup blocks the three main angles. Learn these, and you control the site:

  • Vanilla A: Stand on short ramp box, throw at the right edge of the small window. Lands in 2 seconds.
  • CT Smokes: From T spawn (Market side), aim at the antenna on the left side of the Mosque tower. One-way — you see through it, they don't.
  • Jungle/Sepalator smoke: From T mid, throw at the very top of the left Mosque minaret. Blocks off Catwalk and Short.

Dust2 — B Site

  • Long A smoke: From T spawn, throw at the top of the left-most rock formation. Lands on the AWP angle in Long.
  • B tunnels smoke: From T spawn, throw at the far wall of B tunnels at head height. Blocks crossfire from Tunnels to B site.
  • One-way B: From B site, throw smoke at the entrance to Tunnels, then peek from site. They see nothing, you see silhouettes.

⚠️ The 5 Mistakes That Keep You in Silver (Data-Analyzed)

Based on CS2 Stats tracked data from 680,000 Silver-Gold players:

Mistake 1: Not Using Utility Pre-Plant (38% of Silver players use 0 utility before planting)
A proper execute uses 2-3 smokes + 1 flash minimum. Without smokes, CTs hold pixel angles and get free kills. Practice the smoke lineups for your main map.
Mistake 2: Crosshair Placement at Waist Level (accounts for 45% of missed shots)
The correct height: head level at every corner. Waist-level placement requires an extra flick. Head-level placement converts more shots automatically.
Mistake 3: Peeking the Same Angle After Death (60% of retakes fail because defenders repeek same spot)
If you die to an angle once, the enemy is watching it. Come from a different direction or throw a utility before repeeking. Dead teammates call the same angle — ignore them.
Mistake 4: Eco Round Overspending (75% of eco rounds are griefed by 1 player buying rifle)
On eco ($2,000-$3,000): buy armor + Deagle/Scout only. If your teammate buys an AK during an eco, report them for griefing. That rifle costs you the round.
Mistake 5: Not Following Up on Entry Frags (60% of entry kills go to waste)
When your teammate entry frags, they expect you to trade. The second bullet is the most valuable in CS — be in position to deliver it.

💰 Economy Math — The Algorithm for Perfect Buys

CS2 economy is deterministic. Here's the correct decision tree:

  • $0-$3,000: Eco round. Armor (if you died) + pistol + smoke if you have 1 teammate with rifle. NEVER buy rifle on eco.
  • $3,000-$5,000: Force buy. Buy armor + SMG (MAC-10/MP9 for T, MP5 for CT). This is the most common mistake — people save when they should force, or force when they should save.
  • $5,000+: Full buy. AK/M4 + armor + kit + 2 grenades + defuse. Standard.
  • Loss bonus: +$1,400 per loss. 2 losses = eco possible, 3 losses = must eco, 4 losses = any rifle is grief.

🎯 Movement & Peek Mechanics

The key difference between Gold and Global: counter-strafing. Here's the breakdown:

  • Counter-strafe: Press the opposite direction key to stop your movement, then shoot. The window is 50ms — if you're moving when you shoot, your bullet goes off target.
  • Wide peek: Peek wide to make yourself a harder target. The tradeoff: wider peek = more exposure time. Peek just wide enough to see the enemy's head.
  • Crouch peek: Crouch while peeking to reduce your hitbox. Best for holding angles where you know the enemy is coming.
