2. Essential Grenade Binds

Key binds make nade lineups instant. No more scrolling through inventory mid-round.

🔥 Smoke Binds (Slot 4)

// Bind smoke to mouse wheel down
bind "mousedown" "slot4"

// Or bind to a key
bind "f" "slot4"

// Quick-throw (drops nade immediately)
alias "+quicknade" "slot4; +attack"
alias "-quicknade" "-attack"
bind "g" "+quicknade"

💣 Flash Binds (Slot 5)

// Self-flash bind (always useful)
alias "+selflash" "slot5; +attack"
alias "-selflash" "-attack"
bind "v" "+selflash"

// Throw at current angle
bind "z" "slot5"

💥 HE Grenade Bind (Slot 3)

// Quick-throw HE
alias "+quickhe" "slot3; +attack"
alias "-quickhe" "-attack"
bind "c" "+quickhe"
Note: Don't spam grenades at the start of rounds. Save them for execute phases. A well-timed smoke is worth more than a random HE that deals 15 damage.

🛒 Full Buy (Budget: $4,500+)

AK / M4A4 / M4A1-S + Armor + Kit + 2 Grenades. This is the standard round after you've won 2+.

Rule: If you can't full buy, save. Two players with rifles > 5 players with half-buy SMGs.

🔫 Half Buy / Eco ($2,000-$3,500)

UMP-45 + Light Armor + Flash. Or buy armor + grenade and save for next round. The UMP-45 is the best eco gun in CS2 — it one-shots at close range.

💸 Full Eco (Under $1,500)

P250 / Deagle + No Armor + Flash. Pistol round only. Never buy MAC-10 or MP9 on eco — the P250 is more likely to get you a kill.

Golden Rule: In a 4v5 or 3v5, always eco. The odds of winning a round down players with inferior guns is not worth the risk. Save for the next full buy where you have equal equipment.

4. Pro Tips

⭐ Pre-Aim Corners

Before peeking any corner, pre-aim at head height where the enemy will appear. Don't look at the floor and start shooting when you see them — you're already aimed at their face.

⭐ Smoke Timing

Throw smokes 0.5 seconds before you peek, not after. A smoke thrown after you've peeked gives enemies time to hear and adjust. The smoke should be landing as you're swinging.

⭐ Counter-Strafing

To shoot accurately while moving, tap the opposite movement key for 50ms as you stop. This kills your momentum and unlocks full accuracy instantly. Practice in workshop map "Training Aim" for 15 minutes daily.

⭐ Sound Whoring

Sound is free information. Walk near walls to hear footsteps. Enemies who run sound like they're next to you. Crouch-walking gives zero sound. Use this to know when they're close without looking.

5. Common Mistakes

❌ Buying Without Your Team

Solo buying when your team is ecoing is the #1 throw. Wait for your team or communicate "eco this round, full next." One player with an AK vs 5 with SMGs is a bad trade.

❌ Overpeeking

Going for the "extra kill" after you've won the round is statistically more likely to lose the round than playing safe. Every overpeek has a 50% chance of killing you and losing the round for your team. Just hold the angle.

❌ Not Using the Kit

The defuse kit costs $400 and saves the round if you get clutched on. Always buy a kit on CT side if you have $400+. The "I'll save it" mindset loses rounds.
