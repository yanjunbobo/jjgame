🎯 When to Use Each Rifle

AK-47 (T Side): The standard. One headshot, 4 body shots. The spray pattern is: 5 bullets straight up, then shift right. Practice in workshop: recoil_master 1234.
M4A4 vs M4A1-S (CT Side): M4A4 has 30 bullets and higher DPS but louder. M4A1-S has 20 bullets, is quieter, and has less recoil. M4A1-S is better for holding angles; M4A4 is better for aggressive peeking.
AWP: One shot, one kill at any range (with armor). The rule: if you're not hitting 70%+ accuracy with the AWP, you're better off with an AK. AWP players should hold common angles and let enemies come to them.

⚠️ The 3 Weapon Mistakes That Lose Rounds

Mistake 1: Spray Transferring Wrong
Most players spray all bullets at one target. The correct spray transfers to the second target after the first drops. Practice: in aim_botz, set to random position, spray 10 bullets at target 1, transfer to target 2. This wins 1v2s.
Mistake 2: Not Using the Right SMG for the Side
MAC-10 ($1,050) is T-side (higher damage, louder). MP9 ($1,250) is CT side (cheaper, armor piercing). Using the wrong SMG costs you 10-15% of your damage output.
Mistake 3: Deagle on Eco When You Should Use Scout
The Scout (SSG 08, $1,700) one-shots to the head at any range. On eco rounds where you have $2,000+, Scout is a better choice than Deagle because it rewards accuracy more consistently.
