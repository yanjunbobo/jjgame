⚠️ The 5 Economy Mistakes

Mistake 1: Not Tracking Enemy Money
By round 6, you should know if enemies can afford an AWP ($4,750). If they saved 2 rounds after winning, they have $5,000+. Adjust your strategy: push aggressively if they eco, play conservative if they have money.
Mistake 2: Full Saving When Team Can Buy
If you have $4,500 and your team has $4,800 average, a force buy works. Full saving ($7,000+ bank) when the team force buys = you arrive with a rifle while they have $2,000. Communicate your money to the team every round.
Mistake 3: Not Spending All Your Money on the Final Round of Halftime
At 7-5 or worse going into halftime, buy everything. You reset to $4,000 after halftime regardless. Spending all your money before the reset = maximum firepower on the crucial rounds.
Mistake 4: Armor on Eco With $1,500 Budget
Light Armor ($400) + SMG ($1,200) = $1,600. If you have $1,500, you can't afford both. Skip armor on eco — SMGs are accurate enough without it. The $400 buys you utility next round.
Mistake 5: Not Selling Your AWP When You Can't Afford It
The AWP costs $4,750. If you buy it and die, you get $0. If you sell it pre-round, you get $3,562. If you can't afford a rifle next round, SELL your AWP and buy an AK. 90% of players hold the AWP and create a 4v5 for their team.
