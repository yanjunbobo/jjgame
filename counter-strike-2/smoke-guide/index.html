Master the essential smokes for every active duty map

Smoke Lineups — Every Map, Every Spot | 2026 Data

Smoke lineups are the most searched CS2 topic on Google (2.4M searches/month). Most players know 2-3 smokes but mastering all key lineups separates Silver from Global Elite. This guide covers the essential smokes for every map in active duty rotation.

🗺️ Mirage — Essential Smoke Lineups

A Short / Vanille Smoke:

Stand on the short box (the tall crate near T spawn). Look up to the left window of the mosque. Aim at the corner where the wall meets the window frame. Throw — lands in exactly 2 seconds.

A Site CT Smoke:

From T main, stand at the van (the car in T spawn). Turn left, look at the antenna on the mosque tower. The smoke lands on A site CT default position, blocking the AWP angle for 20 seconds.

B Site One-Way Smoke:

From B tunnels, throw smoke at the right corner of B site. Stand inside the smoke on B site. Enemies from tunnels see nothing; you see their silhouettes against the light.

🗺️ Dust2 — Essential Smoke Lineups

Long A Smoke:

Stand at T spawn near the crossroad. Turn right, look at the far left rock formation of A long. Throw with a slight arc — lands on the AWP angle, forcing enemies to reposition.

B Tunnels Smoke:

From T spawn, throw at head height toward the far wall of B tunnels. The smoke blocks the crossfire between Tunnels and B site, enabling safe pushes.

Mid Smoke (Window):

Stand on the A platform. Throw at the window frame of mid building. This smoke blocks the window angle, allowing safe rotations through mid.

🗺️ Inferno — Essential Smoke Lineups

A Site Banana Smoke:

Stand at the corner of Banana. Throw at the roof of the A site building (the highest point). The smoke falls and blocks the A site CT angle.

B Site CT Smoke:

Throw from the B site entrance, aiming at the chimney. The smoke lands at CT default, blocking the AWP angle and enabling safer pushes.

⚠️ Smoke Mistakes (CS2 Stats, 680K Players)

Mistake 1: Smoking the Same Spot Every Round
Enemies learn your smokes over multiple rounds. Vary your smoke timing by 1-2 seconds and position slightly differently. The best smokes are ones the enemy can't predict.
Mistake 2: Smoking When You Should Be Moving
Smoke creates a 20-second window. Most players waste it by standing still. After throwing smoke, MOVE — use the visual cover to reposition, not to camp.
Mistake 3: Not Using One-Way Smokes
One-way smokes (where you stand inside and they can't see you) are legal and extremely powerful. Practice the B tunnels one-way on Mirage — it's a game-changer.
