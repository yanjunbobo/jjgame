📊 The 5 Roles in CS2

Entry Fragger (EF)
First into site, takes the most risk. Role: information gathering. If you die as entry, you've done your job if your team gets 2+ kills from the info you provided. Best agents: Jett (Dash), Raze (Satchel), Omen (Paranoia).
AWPer / Rifler
Primary sniper. Controls long angles and crossfires. Rifler backs up the AWPer with movement and second-entry. Best agents: Chamber (Trademark), Skye (Flash), Sova (Recon).
Support / Anchor
Holds the most important angle, provides utility for the team. Anchor dies first on site but the trade must be worth it. Best agents: Cypher (Cage), Killjoy (Turret), Sage (Slow).
Lurker
Creates distraction and takes unexpected angles. The lurker's life is worth less than the information they provide. When the lurker dies from an unexpected position, the enemy team rotates unnecessarily.
IGL (In-Game Leader)
Calls the round, manages economy, and makes mid-round calls. The IGL should be the calmest player on the team — emotion = bad calls. The IGL doesn't need the best aim, they need the best game sense.

📢 Communication Protocol

Call Everything Once — The "1-Second Rule"
You have 1 second to call what you see. "One A short" is good. "They're rotating" with no confirmation is bad. Every call needs: location, number, weapon/utility status if known.
Economy Calls — The "Eco / Force / Full Buy" Call
The IGL calls eco/force/full. If you disagree, call a timeout. Don't half-buy — a half-buy (Cloak + light armor + SMG) loses 90% of the time against a full buy. Either eco (Sheriff + light armor + abilities) or full buy.
