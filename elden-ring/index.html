⚔️ Shadow of the Erdtree DLC Boss Guide

Shadow of the Erdtree is the most challenging content in FromSoftware history. This guide covers every major DLC boss, the specific weaknesses of each encounter, and the builds that trivialize otherwise brutal fights in 2026.

Radahn, Consort of the Abyss

The Hardest Boss in the Game: Phase 1 is a gank with Miquella and Radahn. Phase 2 adds sacred light beams that track. The fight has a 45-minute average clear time even for experienced players.

Strategy: Use Comet Azur with the Flaming Strike ash on a Colossal Weapon. Stand at the designated glitch spot where Radahn's targeted attacks miss. This makes Phase 1 trivial. Phase 2 requires either Comet Azur again or significant mimic tear investment.

Build: 60 Strength, 30 Faith. Use the Greatsword with Flaming Strike ash. The fire damage stacks with STR scaling.

Midra, Lord of Frenzied Flame

The Madness Boss: The frenzy meter builds through proximity and hits. Staying at mid-range avoids most frenzy generation while keeping you in attack range.

DLC Build Recommendations

STR Colossus: 60 STR, 25 DEX. Guts Sword with Lion's Claw. The armor penetration from Lion's Claw bypasses DLC boss defenses. This is the safest carry build for all DLC content.
