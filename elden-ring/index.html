⚔️ Best PVE Builds & DLC Tips

Elden Ring's Shadow of the Erdtree DLC introduces the most challenging bosses in FromSoftware history. This guide covers the best PVE builds for DLC content and proven strategies for every major boss encounter in 2026.

Best PVE Builds

Strength Colossus: 66 Strength with Greatsword or Guts Sword. The Colossal weapon damage multipliers make Strength the highest PVE damage stat. The Iron Greatsword's ash of war (Lion's Claw) provides armor penetration. Endgame bosses have 20%+ absorption to standard physical—Lion's Claw bypasses this.

Int Scaling Moonveil: 80 Intelligence with Moonveil katana. The Transient Moon slash deals massive sorcery damage with physical-like range. The Moonveil's ash of war is the fastest Int weapon art in the game.

Bleed Arcane: 45 Arcane with Mohgwyn's Sacred Spear. The Blood Exultation buff adds 20% damage when enemies have blood loss. The Sacred Spear R2 is the fastest blood loss applicator.

DLC Boss Strategies

Radahn, Consort of the Abyss: The hardest DLC boss. Phase 1 requires range management. Phase 2 adds light beams that track. The strategy is patience—wait for Phase 2 jump attack, punish with Comet Azur, repeat.

Midra, Lord of Frenzied Flame: The maddening flame boss. The frenzy mechanic builds through proximity. Stay at mid-range to avoid madness buildup while punishing recovery windows.
