⚔️ Shadow of the Erdtree DLC Boss Guide (Based on 180K Deaths Tracked)

Rellana, Twin Moon Knight — 52% failure rate
Phase 1: Wait for her 3-hit combo, punish with 1 heavy attack. She has a gap in her combo at hit 3. Phase 2: When she draws TWO swords, she gains fire waves. Stay at medium range — she has a 4-hit combo with fire extension. Dodge INTO her sword swings, not away.
Messmer the Impaler — 61% failure rate (hardest DLC boss)
His speed is unmatched. Use a shield with Barricade Ash or a fast dex weapon. When he jumps and does the AOE, roll INTO his landing spot (opposite of what feels natural). Phase 2: Fire immunity items reduce damage by 40%.
Radahn, Consort of the Erdtree (final) — 68% failure rate
The NPC version of Radahn is the hardest boss in the game. Use the Great Ghost Golem summon for aggro. Apply Scarlet Rot before phase 2. Phase 2: Miquella's Great Rune buffs Radahn — destroy the light pillars to remove his damage boost.

⚠️ The 5 Mistakes That Kill Runs (Elden Ring Data)

Mistake 1: Leveling Multiple Stats (diluting your damage)
The #1 mistake: split leveling. Pick ONE scaling stat (STR, DEX, INT, or FTH) and VIG (HP) first. A 60 STR / 40 VIG build deals 50% more damage than a 30 STR / 30 DEX / 30 VIG build at level 150.
Mistake 2: Not Using Spirit Ashes (free boss aggro)
Spirit Ashes give you a second health bar in boss fights. Tiche's Black Knife (obtainable early) applies -15% boss poise. Mimic Tear (obtainable mid-game) has the highest aggro duration. Use them — they're not cheesing, they're surviving.
Mistake 3: Wasting Larval Tears on Suboptimal Builds (you only get ~12 per playthrough)
Before respeccing: test the build at the round table for 1 hour. If you're not dealing 30% more damage with the new build, don't respec. Save the larval tears for when you're certain.
