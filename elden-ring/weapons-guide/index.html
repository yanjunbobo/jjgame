⚠️ The 5 Build Mistakes

Mistake 1: Leveling Too Many Stats
Focus on 2 damage stats. Spread allocations waste 40% of level-up efficiency. Pick VIGOR + primary damage stat + END only.
Mistake 2: Not Using Mimic Tear Ashes
Mimic Tear (sold by NPC at Rose Church) is the best Spirit Ash in the game. It has 50% of your HP and uses your weapons. At level 80+, Mimic can solo most bosses. Always have it equipped.
Mistake 3: Bleed Build With Less Than 45 Arcane
Bleed scales with Arcane at 45 soft cap. Below 45, Bloodhound Fang and Rivers of Blood do 40% less bleed buildup. Get 45 Arcane before investing elsewhere.
Mistake 4: Not Upgrading Your Weapon to +18-20
A +0 weapon vs +20 weapon = 3x damage difference. An unupgraded Moonveil does less damage than a +20 Iron Greatsword. Always max upgrade your primary weapon before attempting late-game bosses.
Mistake 5: Heavy Armor on Bleed Build
Bleed builds want to be agile. Heavy armor slows you down, reducing dodge window. Use the Bloodhound Armor (7.0 weight, high defense) or文句 robes for maximum dodge effectiveness.
