📊 Boss Order & Recommended Levels

Tier 1 — Limgrave + Southern Caelid (Level 1-30)

  • Margit, the Fell Omen (recommended level: 20-25)
  • Red Wolf of Radagon + Rennala (Academy, level 30)

Tier 2 — Liurnia + Caelid (Level 30-60)

  • Starscourge Radahn (recommended level: 40-50)
  • Royal Knight Loretta + Sorcerer Sellen (level 35+)
  • Full Moon Queen Rennala (level 40+)

Tier 3 — Altus + Leyndell (Level 60-100)

  • Mohg, Lord of Blood (underground, level 80+, use Mimic Tear)
  • Morgott the Omen King (Leyndell, level 80-90)

Tier 4 — Mountaintops + Haligtree + Farum Azula (Level 100+)

  • Fire Giant (Mountaintops, level 100+)
  • Malenia (Haligtree, level 130+, hardest boss in game)
  • Radagon + Elden Beast (finale, level 140+)

⚠️ The 5 Boss Progression Mistakes

Mistake 1: Skipping Ranni's Quest for Moonveil
Moonveil is the best katana in the game and Ranni's quest gives it at level 40-50. Skipping it means you miss one of the strongest weapons for 40+ hours.
Mistake 2: Not Upgrading Weapon Before Mohg
Mohg has 8,000 HP and the Bleed effect. A +10 weapon does 40% more damage than +5. Always upgrade your main weapon to max before major bosses.
Mistake 3: Fighting Malenia at Level 100
Malenia's Waterfowl Dance one-shots at any level below 60 VIGOR. Recommended: 50-60 VIGOR, weapon +18-20 before attempting.
Mistake 4: Summoning Two Players for Margit
More phantoms = more boss HP. Two phantoms = 3x boss HP. Solo Margit with Spirit Ashes is easier than co-op.
Mistake 5: Digging for Runes in Limgrave Instead of Progressing
The fastest rune farm is Mohgwyn Palace (40K runes/hour). Stay in main quest until level 60, then farm at Mohgwyn if needed.
