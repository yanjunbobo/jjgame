Automatic farms that work in 1.21.x

The key to Minecraft late-game is automation. The moment you have an iron farm and an XP farm, you stop worrying about resources and start focusing on building. Here are the farms I've built and tested in 1.21.x.

The Iron Farm: Village-Based Design

The iron golem spawning mechanics in 1.21 require a village with at least 3 villagers. The design: build a 16x16 dark room at y=64, place 3 villagers with different professions inside, and build a ceiling 3 blocks above the villagers (so golems can spawn in the space between). Iron golems spawn at a rate of 1 per 20 minutes with 3 villagers.

The specific trick: give the villagers workstations (lecterns) to lock their professions. A villager with a lectern won't lose their profession. Lock 3 villagers as tool smith, weapon smith, and armorer, then trade with them to get discounts. This also gives you access to discounted diamond tools and armor.

The Enderman XP Farm: 20 Levels Per Hour

The most efficient XP farm in Minecraft is the Enderman farm in the End. Design: build a 64x64 platform in the void in the End dimension at y=100, with water and a killing chamber in the center. Endermen spawn at a rate of 70 per spawn cycle in this design. With a looting sword and AFK at the kill switch, you earn 20 levels per hour.

The specific configuration: 4 layers of platforms, each 3 blocks apart vertically, all with water pushing toward a central AFK spot. Each layer spawns Endermen independently, giving you 4x the spawn rate of a single-layer farm.

The Raid Farm: 30 Levels Per Hour

The raid farm exploits the village raid mechanic. Build a 32x32 village with 3 villager beds in a 1-block-deep pool. When a raid triggers (use a Bell to start it), the villager is in the pool and can't be reached by the raid mobs. The raid mobs die from a lava blade above the pool. This gives you all the loot from a raid (Emeralds, Ominous Banner, Hero of the Village effects) without the danger.

All farms tested in Minecraft Java 1.21.x. Last updated: May 2026.
