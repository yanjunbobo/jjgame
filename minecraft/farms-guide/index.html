📊 Best Farm Designs by Type

Gold Farm (Passive Income)
Location: Nether, above Y=200. Design: Pigmobiles near zombie pigman. Yield: 100+ gold/hour with zero risk. Materials: 64 obsidian, 1 water bucket.
XP Farm (Fast Levels)
Design: Spawn platform (80 blocks) + drowned spawner. Use afk timer alt-account. Yield: 3-5 levels/hour while semi-afk.
Iron Farm (Village Economy)
Location: Any. Design: 8-10 villagers in 1×1 holes, 1 golem spawn area above. Yield: 1 iron block/hour. Required for anvil repairs and tools.
Crop Farm (Wheat/Carrot/Potato)
Design: 9×9 farmland with bone meal, water at center. Fully afkable with right clicker. Yield: 1,000 crops/hour.

⚠️ The 5 Farm Mistakes

Mistake 1: Building Iron Farm at Y=0
Iron golems only spawn in villages with 10+ villagers AND valid beds. If you build an iron farm in the wrong dimension or at the wrong height, it will produce nothing.
Mistake 2: Not Using Spawn Chunks
Spawn chunks (within 128 blocks of spawn) keep loaded when you're far away. Build your afk farm in spawn chunks. If you build it 1,000 blocks away, it stops when you walk off.
Mistake 3: AFK at the Wrong Height
For XP farms, you must be exactly at the right height (Y=128 for most designs). Standing 1 block off = mobs don't spawn and you get 0 XP. Use coordinates.
