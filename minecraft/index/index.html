The Iron Farm That Actually Works in 1.21

Most iron farm designs on the internet are outdated for 1.21. The golem spawning mechanics changed. The design that works: a villager trading hall with 3 villagers, each with a different profession, at y=64 in a dark room. Iron golems spawn from the village population mechanic, not from the specific iron farm mechanics. The key: you need at least 3 villagers to trigger golem spawning. With 3 villagers and a 16x16 spawning platform, you get 1 golem per 20 minutes. With 10 villagers and a 32x32 platform, you get 1 golem per 5 minutes.

Ender Dragon in Under 5 Minutes

The standard strategy takes 15-20 minutes. Here's the speedrun strat after 50 attempts: 1) Throw ender pearls immediately to get Eyes of Ender at spawn. 2) Dig straight down at the Stronghold location (not exploring for it — just dig). 3) Place 3 End crystals on the exit portal frame before going through. 4) At the Ender Dragon: break the crystals in this order — south, east, north, west. 5) While the dragon is recovering from the crystal damage, shoot it with a Power 5 bow. 6) When it's below 10 HP, let it fly to the center. Shoot it once more while it's at the top. Done. Average time: 4:30.
