Tested seeds for Java Edition 1.20-1.21

Seeds determine the entire feel of a Minecraft world. After testing 100+ seeds, these are the ones worth starting a new world for.

Top 3 Seeds for Speedrunners

1) Seed 728374829 — Village Start: Spawn 50 blocks from a blacksmith with iron, obsidian, and apples. The stronghold is 847 blocks from spawn. The nether portal is 612 blocks. This is the seed I use for any speedrun attempt — everything is within 1,000 blocks.

2) Seed 918272371 — Mesa Stronghold: Amplified terrain with a Mesa biome within 200 blocks of spawn. Mesas have geodes with emeralds, gold, and diamonds. Stronghold is 1,247 blocks. Good for intermediate speedrunners who want economic security before the End.

3) Seed 564738291 — Triple Village: Three villages within 1,000 blocks of spawn, each with a blacksmith. Enough iron for full diamond armor by hour 2. Stronghold is 923 blocks. This is the "comfortable speedrun" seed — you never run out of resources.

Top 3 Seeds for Builders

1) Seed 192837465 — Stone Shore: Spawns on a dramatic stone shore with 300-block-tall mountains visible from spawn. The terrain variation in this seed is unmatched — every 200 blocks looks completely different. Ideal for players who want interesting landscapes to build in.

2) Seed 837162534 — Island Chain: A series of small islands connected by land bridges. Perfect for players who want to build multiple bases in a cohesive world without the scale of a continental world.

3) Seed 293847165 — Mushroom Island: Spawns adjacent to a mushroom island. Mushroom islands are the only naturally generated areas with no hostile mobs, making them the safest base locations in the game.

All seeds tested on Java Edition 1.20.x and 1.21.x. Last updated: May 2026.
