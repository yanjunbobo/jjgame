⚠️ The 5 Combat Mistakes

Mistake 1: Not Using Shield
A shield blocks 100% of melee damage. Players without shields take full damage. Get a shield before any serious combat.
Mistake 2: Wrong Enchantment Priority
Sharpness V > Fire Aspect II > Knockback II > Looting III. If you're using Unbreaking instead of Looting on your sword, you're losing 1.5 stacks of XP per hour.
Mistake 3: Sprint-Attacking
Sprint attacks deal 3x damage but food depletes 5x faster. Only sprint-attack when you're sure you can kill the mob — otherwise you'll be stuck without food mid-fight.
Mistake 4: Not Using Totem of Undying
The Totem is the most valuable PvP item. If you're in Hardcore or doing Elytra combat, always have the totem in your off-hand.
Mistake 5: Fighting the Warden Without Riptide
The Warden takes 30 seconds to spawn. Using any light source near it = instant death. The ONLY safe way to fight the Warden is with Riptide trident + Conduit power underwater.
