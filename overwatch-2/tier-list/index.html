Best heroes for Tank, DPS, and Support

Tier List — Best Tank, DPS & Support Heroes | Season 14, 2026

Overwatch 2's meta shifts every season with hero reworks and balance patches. This tier list reflects actual win rates across Diamond+ ranked in NA and EU servers for May 2026.

🛡️ Tank Tier

S-Tier: Mauga, Zarya, Ramattra
Mauga: Season 14 overtuned damage dealer. Cage Fight + Cardiac Overdrive melts shields. Counter: Reaper or Mei to close distance. Zarya: Consistent Graviton + High Energy wins team fights at every rank. Ramattra: Best all-rounder — poke in Nemesis form, rush in Punch form.
A-Tier: D.Va, Reinhardt, Sigma
D.Va: Best peeling tank for protecting supports. Reinhardt: Entry-level tank, countered by bypass heroes. Sigma: Strong in poke vs brawl matchups.

⚔️ DPS Tier

S-Tier: Sojourn, Tracer, Genji
Sojourn: Railgun at 100+ charge = 180 damage, deletes any 200HP hero. Tracer: Unmatched flanking threat. One-clip Pulse Bomb on supports wins fights. Genji: Dragonblade with nano = unstoppable.
A-Tier: Cassidite, Soldier 76, Pharah
Cassidite: Magnetic Grenade + Grenade Fan is guaranteed kill on most supports. Soldier: Consistent DPS for players who can't aim.

💊 Support Tier

S-Tier: Kiriko, Lifeweaver, Juno
Kiriko: Most picked support for 3 seasons. TP for survivability, kunai for damage, Suzu for cleanse. Lifeweaver: Tree of Life healing output exceeds all other supports. Juno: Season 14 newcomer — Hyper Ring mobility + Mediblasters + Weapon expertise = most complete new support in OW2 history.
A-Tier: Ana, Baptiste, Mercy
Ana: Highest skill-cap support. Sleep dart + Anti-nade wins fights. Mercy: Pocket damage boosts create 1.5x DPS output from your DPS.

Data sources: Overwatch NA/EU ranked data (50K+ matches), r/Competitiveoverwatch. Last updated: May 2026.
