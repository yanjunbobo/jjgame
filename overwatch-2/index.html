Overwatch 2 - Rank Climb and Meta Guide

The 4 Mistakes Keeping You in Your Rank

1. Playing too many heroes. One-tricks climb 40% faster than flex players. 2. Ignoring the kill feed. Muting comms and watching the kill feed for 10 seconds at round start tells you which team is winning. 3. Using ultimates reactively. Proactive ults within 5 seconds of fight start win 73% more. 4. Peeling for the wrong teammate. Peel support first if both are low, DPS if support is safe.

Why Support Is the Best Role for Climbing

In coaching 30+ players, support has the highest win rate influence below Diamond. Reason: if your tank dies, the whole team dies. If you're the support and you keep your tank alive, you effectively carry. I played support for 100 hours and climbed from Platinum to Diamond. Same mechanical skill, different role.

The Mauga Problem: Stand at Cage Edges

Mauga's Cage Fight blocks movement abilities but not projectiles. The specific counter: stand at maximum range at the cage edge, don't enter the cage, shoot through the walls. 90% of Diamond players don't know this. They run into the cage and die. Rule: if Mauga uses Cage Fight, you speed boost out, not in.
