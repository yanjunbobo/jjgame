Deflection is the core of Sekiro combat

Normal Block (L1) reduces damage 40% but fills your posture. Deflection (~0.3s before impact) reduces damage to 0 AND deals 3x posture damage to the enemy. The audio cue is a metallic "ting." If you hear a "thud," you blocked normally.

The Color System

Red = unblockable. Use Mikiri Counter (dodge forward + attack) or dodge. Blocking red attacks breaks your posture instantly.

Orange = deflectable but late. Start the deflect input before the attack visually starts.

Yellow = sweep attacks. Jump and stamp to deal posture damage and interrupt combos.

Blue = thrust attacks. Mikiri Counter deals massive posture damage. Deflecting thrusts gives minimal posture damage.

The Posture Bar Decay Trick

Posture decays when you're not in combat. Hit an enemy, deal some posture damage, then run away for 10 seconds = posture resets. Never run away mid-fight unless you've filled the posture bar to at least 60%. If you're at 30% and you run, you just wasted the progress.

Specific Scenario Fixes

Lady Butterfly Phase 2: Shinobi Firecracker kills all illusions instantly. Use it during the summoning animation. Then you have 3 seconds of full vulnerability.

Guardian Ape: Use loaded spear thrust attacks — they deal massive posture damage. Fighting without it is 3x harder.

True Corrupted Monk: Light the candles in the cave first (one on the right side of the entrance). Without candles, you can't see her attack telegraphs clearly.
