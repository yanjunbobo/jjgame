Boss Guide — Every Boss Posture Mechanic & Strategy | 2026

Sekiro has 22 bosses and every one requires a specific strategy. The "swing until they die" approach fails at the first real boss. Here's the boss-by-boss breakdown.

📊 Boss Strategy

Gyoubu Oniwa (Tutorial Boss)
Phase 1: He charges with a spear and shield — deflect it. When he charges, run to the side — he misses. Phase 2: He calls his horse. Firecracker when he charges = free hit. Key: don't lock onto him.
Lady Butterfly (First Real Wall)
Phase 1: Wait for her to attack first — deflect everything. Attack twice after deflecting a double-swipe combo. Phase 2: She summons illusions. Shinobi Firecracker instantly kills illusions and deals 60 posture damage.
Genichiro Ashina (The Real Test)
Phase 1-3: deflect thrust counters, Mikiri counter thrusts, jump-stomp bow attacks. Phase 4 (Isshin's posture): Lightning reversal deals massive posture damage — get him to Phase 4 quickly and use lightning to close out.
Isshin Sword Saint (Final Boss)
4 phases. Phase 1-2: deflect spear + gun, run from gun. Phase 3: Lightning reversal. Phase 4: Ichimonji double — after deflecting it, there's a 1.5 second window where he's vulnerable. Hit him once, retreat. 4-5 cycles = dead.
