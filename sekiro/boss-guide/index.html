Every boss from tutorial to Sword Saint

Every boss in Sekiro is designed to teach you one specific skill. If you're dying to a boss, you're missing that skill, not because the boss is unfair.

Gyoubu Oniwa: Learn to Fight Unlocked

Don't lock onto him. Locking restricts your camera. Gyoubu's horse charges from multiple angles. Fight unlocked so you can see both the horse and his spear attacks simultaneously. Phase 2: when he charges, run sideways, not backward.

Lady Butterfly: Wait, Then Strike

She is not aggressive enough to kill you if you play defensively. Attack strings have 2-3 second rest windows. Attack during those windows and retreat immediately. Phase 2 fix: Shinobi Firecracker kills all illusions instantly. Use it during the summoning animation. Then you have 3 seconds of complete vulnerability.

Genichiro: The Boss That Teaches You Everything

Phase 1: Mikiri Counter the opening thrust, deflect the 3-hit combo, attack once, retreat. Phase 2: Lightning Reversal — jump early enough that the arrow hits you in the air, not on the ground. Phase 3: Move laterally from the bow fire, don't deflect it. Phase 4: Get him to Phase 4 quickly and use lightning reversal aggressively — it deals massive posture damage.

Isshin Sword Saint: Phase 4 Is the Real Final Boss

Phases 1-2: deflect spear combos, run sideways from gun shots. Phase 3: Mikiri Counter the Ashina Cross (red attack). Phase 4: The Ichimonji double. If you block it normally, your posture breaks. If you deflect it, Isshin is stunned 1.5 seconds. Deflect, hit once, retreat 3 steps, repeat. 4 cycles = dead.

Based on 200+ attempts against Isshin Sword Saint across multiple save files. Last updated: May 2026.
