Dota 2 - Hero Tier and MMR Climb Guide

The 0:00 Ritual That Changed My Lane Win Rate

Before the horn, I check the enemy support's inventory. 4 Tangoes + 1 Stout Shield = they're going aggressive. This habit improved my laning win rate by 15%.

Why Support Is the Best Role for Climbing

In coaching 30+ players, support is the highest-ROI climb. Your carry will make positioning mistakes constantly. If you're the support, you can ward the areas they walk into and they won't die. They get free farm. You effectively carry the game by covering their mistakes.

The Itemization Decision That Costs 500 MMR

If enemy has 2+ Intelligence cores with low armor: build Desolator first. -6 armor applies before your physical damage lands. If enemy has high burst damage (Lion, Lina, Nyx): build BKB first. The 10-second magic immunity at level 1 BKB is enough to survive the initial burst.
