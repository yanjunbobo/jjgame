Hero Tier List — Best Heroes for Each Role | Patch 7.37, 2026

Dota 2 Patch 7.37 changed the meta significantly — the map got larger, Roshan respawn time increased, and Supports got new tools. Here's the tier list based on OpenDota win rates across Ancient+ brackets.

📊 Role Tier List (Patch 7.37)

Carry (Pos 1) S-Tier: Phantom Lancer, Juggernaut, Troll Warlord
Phantom Lancer: Split-push king. Diffusal rework = 8+ illusions that deal meaningful damage. Juggernaut: Easiest carry to win with in solo queue — Omnislash is "press R and win." Troll Warlord: Siege machine. Battle Trance = fastest tower-taking in the game.
Mid (Pos 2) S-Tier: Invoker, Lina, Puck
Invoker: Highest skill-cap, highest reward — 3 S-tier spells from a pool of 10. Lina: Burst queen — Laguna Blade deals 650 pure damage at level 3.
Support (Pos 4/5) S-Tier: Rubick, Lion, Crystal Maiden
Rubick: Spell Steal turns enemy ultimates against them — broken against Tidehunter Ravage and Enigma Black Hole. Lion: Finger of Death = highest single-target damage in the game at level 3.
Offlane (Pos 3) S-Tier: Axe, Tidehunter, Mars
Axe: Counter Helix scales with armor — Desolator + Helix = 300+ damage per proc. Tidehunter: Ravage = 3 second AOE stun at level 3. Mars: Arena + Spear combo traps enemies permanently.

⚠️ The 5 Hero Mistakes

Mistake 1: Not Reading Enemy Support's Items at 0:00
Before the horn, check enemy support's inventory. 2 Tangoes + 1 Salve = weak lane. 4 Tangoes + 1 Stout = aggressive. First 2 minutes of lane decided before the horn if you read items.
Mistake 2: Fighting Without Buyback Ready at 30+ Minutes
At 30 minutes, any team fight you lose without buyback = you lose the game. Always have buyback gold ready unless actively sieging.
Mistake 3: Picking Carries Who Need Farming into a Heavy Gank Lineup
If your team has 3 melee cores and enemy has Bloodseeker + Spirit Breaker + Riki, you're going to lose every lane. Pick carries who can fight early: Juggernaut, Sven, Ursa.
