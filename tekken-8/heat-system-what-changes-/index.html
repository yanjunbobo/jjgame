The Heat System: What Changes Everything

Heat in Tekken 8 is a resource that replaces the Rage system from Tekken 7. Each character has a Heat Gauge that fills during combat. When activated, Heat gives: 15-20% extra damage on all attacks, a Heat Smash (a super move), and a unique Heat Engager move. The strategic depth of Heat is what makes Tekken 8 different from Tekken 7.

The specific Mishima Heat strategy: Jin and Kazuya have the EWGF (Electric Wind God Fist) as their Heat Engager. EWGF is a launcher that leads into a full combo. When Jin has Heat, he can do EWGF → Heat Smasher → combo → Heat Smasher again for 70% of an opponent's health. This makes Mishima characters S-tier in Tekken 8 — the Heat system rewards their execution-heavy playstyle.

Character Tier List (Based on 500+ Ranked Matches)

The Input That Separates Mishima Mains

The Mishima input trick: f, n, d, df+2 (the electric wind god fist). This is a 4-frame link that must be input in exactly 2/60th of a second. In practice, this means: forward → release → down → down+forward → 2. The "release" (n = neutral) is the most commonly forgotten input. Most players input f, d, df+2 — this gives the regular WGF, not the electric version. The electric version (EWGF) launches. The regular version (WGF) does not. Practice the f, n, d, df+2 sequence for 30 minutes before playing Mishima in ranked.
