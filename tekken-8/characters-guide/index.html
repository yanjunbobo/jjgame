Tekken 8 tier list. Devil Jin, Bryan, King, Steve best characters. Season 3 meta. May 2026.

👊 Character Tier List 2026

Tekken 8's roster spans 48 playable characters. This tier list reflects current tournament performance, ranked win rates, and matchup complexity following the Season 3 balance patches in 2026.

S-Tier Characters

Devil Jin: The most complete character in Tekken 8. His hellsweep mixup remains the best low-option in the game. The 10-hit combos provide consistent wall carry. His df2 launcher is among the most oppressive neutral tools. Season 3 left his core tools untouched—he dominates at every skill level.

Bryan Fischer: The highest damage output in the game combined with dominant neutral. The taunt 18-hit combo is the most devastating punish in the roster. His hatchet kick mixup forces respect in every round. The only weakness is his lack of panic tools.

King: The giant swing mixup is still unmatched for 50/50 situations. Improved combo damage in Season 3 elevated him to top tier. The chain throws are the most confusing mixup in the game for opponents unfamiliar with break sequences.

Steve Fox: The most rewarding character for player skill. His 1-frame punishment is unmatched. The string-based offense requires precise timing but rewards practice with dominant results. Season 3 buffs to his df2 made him even more threatening.

A-Tier Characters

Kazuya Mishima: The pure Mishima experience. Electric Wind God Fist remains one of the most iconic and powerful tools. The wavedash approach is unmatched for approach capability. Requires significant execution to reach full potential.

Lili de Rochemonteil: The counter-hit specialist. Her whiff punishment is the best in the game. The royal stance mixups provide excellent low/high options. The weakness is limited panic tools when pressured.

Heihachi Mishima: The most aggressive Mishima variant. His electrics deal more damage than Kazuya's. The 1+2 throw is among the most damaging throws. The weakness is the lack of a safe df2 launcher.

Law: The most beginner-friendly high-tier character. His mixup game with the flip kick is excellent. The DSS stance provides unique positioning options. Season 3 buffs to his combo damage pushed him into A-tier.

Jun Kazama: The best defensive character in the game. Her parry system converts blocked moves into launchers. The df1 string is among the best pokes. Her lack of exceptional throws keeps her out of S-tier.

B-Tier Characters

Paul Phoenix: The highest burst damage potential. Demoman combos deal 100+ damage consistently. The qcb2 launcher is among the most rewarding punish tools. The weakness is the lack of a strong keep-out tool.

Claudio Serafino: The star burst system provides unique pressure options. The extended combos from starburst are excellent. Weakness is the limited mixup capability in pressure situations.

Armor King: Similar to King but with different throw game. The dark upper provides excellent mid-check. The weakness is slightly worse frame data than King overall.

Alisa Bosconovitch: The transforming mechanics provide versatility. She can switch between rushdown and defensive styles. Weakness is the lack of exceptional tools in either style.
