👊 Character-Specific Combo Guide & Frame Data

Tekken 8's generic combo guide isn't enough—you need character-specific optimized routes. This guide covers the optimal combos for the top 10 characters, frame data reference tables, and punishment charts for Season 3 in 2026.

Devil Jin - Optimal Combo

Standard launcher: df2. Combo: df2 → 1 → electric → hellsweep → 4 → f,n,d,df2 → 1,2,1 → b1+2 → 1+2. Total damage: 88. This combo maximizes wall carry while maintaining the hellsweep ender for wake-up mixups.

Wall combo: df2 → 1 → electric → hellsweep → 4 → f,n,d,df2 → 1,2,1 → b1+2 → electric → d+2,1,4. Damage: 72 but wallsplats for maximum wall damage.

Bryan - Optimal Combo

Standard: df2 → ff2 → b1+2 → 1+2,1,2+4. Damage: 85. The 10-hit taunt combo deals 120+ damage but requires specific timing on the taunt hits. Practice the taunt combo separately.

Whiff punish: CH d+2 → 1,2 → qcb1 → 1 → b1+2 → 1+2,1,2+4. Damage: 95. This is the highest damage whiff punish in the game.

Punishment Chart (Common Moves)

-10 on block: df1, d+4 — use your 10-frame punish (jab, d+1)

-12 on block: ws4, b+1 — use your 12-frame punish (df2 or ws1)

-14 on block: ff2, f+2 — use your 14-frame punish (ws2 or hook)

-15 on block: hellsweep, FC d+2 — use your 15-frame punish (df2, FC df2)

Online Optimization

Reduce input delay by enabling "Input Lag Compensation" in system settings. This cuts ~2 frames of input lag. Combined with a gaming monitor at 120Hz+, you gain significant advantage over players with default settings.

For WiFi players: use a wired connection. WiFi introduces variable frame delay that makes timing-based combos inconsistent. Even a 10-meter ethernet cable significantly improves online performance.
