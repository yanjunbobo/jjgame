Tekken 8 - Character and Combo Guide

Why You Keep Losing (Not Knowing Frame Data)

After 500+ hours: the #1 reason players stay at low ranks is not knowing frame data. If your opponent is -15 and you don't punish, you lose damage. The specific habit: after each match, look up the frame data for the move that killed you. I did this for 100 hours. My punish rate went from 20% to 60%. Punishing correctly adds 30% more damage per round.

The Best Characters for Beginners

1. Kazuya: the fundamentals character. If you learn Kazuya, you learn Tekken. 2. Jin: easier than Kazuya, slightly less damage. 3. Lars: easiest to use, good movement. I recommend starting with Lars because his moves are forgiving. Once you understand movement, switch to Kazuya for fundamentals.

The Combo Strategy That Maximizes Damage

Most players drop combos. The specific fix: practice your 10-hit combo 100 times until it's muscle memory. My combo routine: 10 clean reps of the same combo in practice mode before playing online. If I can do 10 clean reps, my combo execution in matches is 80%. If I can't do 10 clean reps, my execution is 40%. The key insight: fewer combos done correctly beats many combos done poorly.
