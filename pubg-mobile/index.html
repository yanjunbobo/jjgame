⚠️ Rank Push Mistakes (Tencent Rank Data)

Mistake 1: Hot Dropping in Ranked (you lose 100+ points per death)
In Diamond+ ranked, hot drops cost 100+ points per death. The correct strategy: drop at the edge of the plane (named locations). Loot fast, rotate to zone center. Play for top 10 — that alone is +200 points.
Mistake 2: Wrong Sensitivity (your aim is off because of this)
Camera Sensitivity (no scope): 30-50. ADS Sensitivity: 25-40. Scope 4x: 15-20. Scope 8x: 5-10. Higher sensitivity = faster aiming but less precision. Start at 50% of these values and adjust down if you overshoot.
Mistake 3: Peeking Ridge Lines Incorrectly (you give free headshots)
The correct peek: crouch-walk to cover, then stand up to shoot. Standing while moving to cover gives away your position. The enemy sees you first if you stand up before reaching cover.
