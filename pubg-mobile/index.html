PUBG Mobile - Sensitivity and Rank Guide

Why Your Aim Is Inconsistent (No Gyro)

After 1,000+ hours: the #1 aim improvement is enabling gyro controls. I played stick-only for 200 hours and had 15% accuracy. Enabled gyro and practiced for 50 hours. Accuracy went to 35%. The specific settings: gyro sensitivity 300, ADS sensitivity 40. This is the closest to PC-aiming in mobile.

The Sensitivity Settings That Work

General: 300 (high for reaction speed). ADS: 40 (slow for precision). Gyro: 300 (responsive but not twitchy). I tested 4 different sensitivity presets over 100 hours. The 300/40/300 combo gave the best balance of reaction and precision.

The Rank Push Strategy That Got Me to Ace

I hit Ace rank in 2 months. The specific strategy: 1) Hot drop for first 50 games to learn combat. 2) Rotate to safe drops at Silver. 3) Focus on placement over kills at all ranks. The key insight: placement points multiply. A 5th place finish gives more points than 10 kills. I went from 0.8 K/D to 1.5 K/D and maintained it by focusing on positioning.
