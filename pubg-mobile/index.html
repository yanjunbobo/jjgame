🎯 Sensitivity & Pro Tips

PUBG Mobile's control scheme separates casual from competitive players. This guide covers optimal sensitivity settings, ADS techniques, and ranked strategies for Conqueror-level play in 2026.

Sensitivity Settings

General: 300-400 DPI on mobile. The sweet spot is 350 DPI for balance between precision and speed. Higher DPI enables faster 180-degree turns but reduces fine-adjustment control.

Camera: 300-350 for general movement. This affects hip-fire tracking speed. Set free look separately at 200 for maximum control during ADS.

ADS: Start at 30-40 and adjust based on weapon recoil. SMGs work at 25-30, ARs at 35-45, Snipers at 15-25. The goal is minimum adjustment while tracking moving targets.

Ranked Strategies

Early Game: Land at named locations with vehicles nearby. The goal is full loot in 2 minutes with vehicle escape before zone compress. Prioritize level 3 gear over weapon attachments.

Mid Game: Rotate to zone center at least 2 minutes before zone closes. Position at the highest ground available within the zone. Establish vision of likely approach angles.

Late Game: Hold angles rather than peeking. The player who peeks first dies to the player's team holding angles. Save grenades for final zone compression.
