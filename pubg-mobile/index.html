🎯 Map-Specific Strategies & Vehicle Combat

PUBG Mobile's maps have fundamentally different optimal strategies. This guide covers Erangel, Miramar, and Sanhok map-specific tactics, vehicle combat mechanics, and the specific rotations that separate top-10 finishers from average players in 2026.

Erangel Strategy

Early Game: Land at Mylta Power for guaranteed loot + vehicle spawns. The central location provides equal rotation options to all zones. After loot, establish position at the next zone center.

Mid Game: Hold high ground at Sosnovka military base or Georgopol hills. These positions have natural chokepoints that funnel enemies into kill zones. Avoid the central fields where you're exposed from all directions.

Late Game: Prioritize vehicle for final circle. On Erangel, final circles often form at the island or Georgopol. A vehicle provides the escape option when the zone compresses.

Vehicle Combat

Taking Vehicle Damage: A moving vehicle takes damage from bullets, but the occupants take damage only from explosions. Shoot tires to disable, then wait for the occupants to exit.

Combat Driving: When in a vehicle, the driver can shoot SMGs while the passenger uses ARs. Drive in circles around enemies in cover—the rotating angle prevents them from aiming while you pepper them with gunfire.

Ram Strategy: A moving vehicle always wins against a stationary vehicle. If an enemy is in a car and you're on foot, you're dead unless you reach cover before they reach you.

Sanhok Strategy

Land Boot Camp or Quarry: These are high-loot areas with natural vehicle access. Boot Camp has the highest player density—survive the hot drop and you're already top-20.

Rotation: On Sanhok's small map, rotations are fast. Hold center positions and third-party fights. The smaller map means encounters happen within 30 seconds of movement.
