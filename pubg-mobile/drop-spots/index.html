⚠️ The 5 Drop Mistakes

Mistake 1: Hot Dropping Without a Plan
Hot drops (School, Georgopol) have 15-20 players per zone. 80% of hot droppers die in the first 2 minutes. If you hot drop, have a specific building/room mapped out and drop straight there. Winging it = death.
Mistake 2: Not Checking Plane Path Before Dropping
The plane path determines how many players land where. If the plane crosses Pochinki, 30 players will drop there. Check the path first, then drop at a less-contested high-loot location.
Mistake 3: Dropping at the Edge of the Map for "Safety"
Edge drops (Kameshki, Slaughterhouse) seem safe but have 2 problems: (1) you spend 5 minutes running to zone, (2) late zones always push you to the center anyway. Always drop within 1km of the first zone center.
Mistake 4: Not Having a Vehicle Route Ready
After looting, you need a vehicle. In Erangel, always know where the nearest vehicle spawn is (garages, gas stations). Not having a vehicle when the first zone starts = death by zone damage.
Mistake 5: Looting Too Long
Optimal loot time: 2-3 minutes. If you're still looting at 5 minutes, you're too slow. Take what you need: AR + scope + 2x,一级甲, and move. Extra loot won't help you if you're dead by zone 2.
