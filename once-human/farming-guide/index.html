The circular route for maximum yield per hour

The most efficient farming in Once Human is about routes, not individual locations. After 200 hours, I can tell you exactly which route gives the best Starchrome per hour.

The Northern Circuit: 2,400 Starchrome Per Hour

Start at the Foundry (north), move south to the Waterfront, then to the Old Sawmill, then back north. This circular route takes 25 minutes and yields 1,000 Starchrome on average. That's 2,400 per hour. The key: always do public dungeons with a group. Solo dungeons yield 400-600 Starchrome. Group dungeons yield 800-1,200 per player. The group bonus is the difference between 1,000 and 2,400 per hour.

Material Farming: The 3 Hotspots

1) The Foundry: Highest concentration of metal nodes. Useful for building materials and weapon parts. I spend 30 minutes here after every Starchrome run to stock up on repair materials.

2) The Waterfront: Best location for cloth and fabric materials. These are required for armor crafting and are the bottleneck for armor progression. I spend 20 minutes here per session.

3) The Old Sawmill: Best location for wood and organic materials. These are used for Stamina food crafting. I spend 15 minutes here every session.

Why You Should Never Farm Alone

Once Human has a party bonus system: farming efficiency increases by 10% for each additional party member up to 4. A 4-person party farms 40% faster than a solo player. This means the fastest way to earn Starchrome is not to optimize your solo route — it's to find 3 other players and run the route together. I joined a farming guild specifically for this reason. My Starchrome per hour went from 1,200 to 2,600 within a week of joining.
