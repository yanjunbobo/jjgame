⚠️ The 5 Farming Mistakes

Mistake 1: Not Completing Daily Challenges First
Daily challenges give 50-100% bonus rewards. Always complete 3 daily challenges before farming. They take 20 minutes and give you 2x the resources you'd get from random farming.
Mistake 2: Ignoring the Memory Dust Cap
Memory Dust caps at 500. Above 500, you stop earning it. Spend it every day at the Convergence Board (weekly reset). Waiting until you have 2,000 = 1,500 wasted Memory Dust.
Mistake 3: Not Having a Dedicated Farm Build
Switch to your gathering build (high luck stat, gathering tools) before farming. Using a combat build while farming = 50% fewer resources per hour.
