🔫 Best Weapon Builds & Territory

Once Human's weapon mod system and territory mechanics create deep build customization. This guide covers optimal weapon builds, the Eternal Palace progression, and territory control strategies in 2026.

Best Weapon Builds

SMG - Tactical Vector: The best SMG for general use. The high fire rate with manageable recoil provides consistent damage. The Tactical Suppressor keeps threat level low. Mod slots favor Crit Rate and Crit Damage.

AR - SCAR-L: The best all-around AR. The damage per bullet is the highest among ARs. The mod system enables customization for any situation. The extended barrel is mandatory for ranged viability.

Sniper - AWP: The best boss-killing weapon. One-shot to the head on most enemies. The slow fire rate is compensated by the damage output. The detachable magazine mod reduces reload vulnerability.

Territory Control

Priority: Control at least 2 high-tier territories before attempting Memory Bosses. The resource generation from territories funds the gear progression. The daily upkeep cost requires consistent income.

Defense: Build walls and traps in chokepoints. The enemy AI pathfinds through structures—use this to funnel enemies into kill zones. The turret placement should cover approach angles, not spawn points.
