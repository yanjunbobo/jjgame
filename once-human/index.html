⚠️ Territory Control Mistakes (r/OnceHuman Analysis)

Mistake 1: Claiming Territory Without Defenders (you lose it in 1 hour)
Territory without a defender (a Deviation you place) decays. Place the Bearable Bear or Friendly Bee before claiming territory. These provide passive defense and resource generation.
Mistake 2: Not Sharing Territory (PvP zones shrink)
In contested areas, if your territory overlaps with another player's and you dont share permissions, both territories decay 2x faster. Always share territory with nearby players.
