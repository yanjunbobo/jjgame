🧠 Memory Boss Tactics & Territory Defense

Once Human's Memory Bosses are the endgame progression gate. This guide covers every Memory Boss tactic, territory defense strategies, and the specific weapon builds optimized for boss encounters in 2026.

Memory Boss Tactics

Environment Manager M-870: The first major Memory Boss. Weak to fire damage. The boss spawns adds that must be killed before focusing the main boss. Use area denial weapons (AR with incendiary rounds) to handle adds while damaging the boss.

Devouring Orpheus: The second Memory Boss. Weak to lightning damage. The boss teleports and creates mirror images. The real boss is always the one not casting at you. Focus fire on the real copy immediately.

Lazarus: The third and hardest Memory Boss. Weak to void damage. The boss phases between physical and energy immune. Switch damage types when the immunity activates.

Territory Defense

Chokepoint Setup: Build walls at natural chokepoints in your territory. The enemy AI will path through the wall gaps, funneling them into your kill zones. Traps should cover the chokepoint approach, not the chokepoint itself.

Turret Placement: Place turrets at elevated positions covering approach angles. Ground-level turrets are vulnerable to melee enemies. The optimal height is 3-4 blocks above ground level.
