⚠️ The 5 Build Mistakes

Mistake 1: Not Prioritizing Crit Rating to 60% First
Below 60% crit rating, you're wasting 40% of your damage potential. Priority: Crit Rating 60% → Attack Power 5000+ → Attack Speed.
Mistake 2: Using Rifle on Close-Quarters Build
Rifles have 2x damage falloff at 20m+. In close-quarters PvP, a Crossbow or SMG will out-DPS a rifle 3:1.
Mistake 3: Ignoring Set Bonuses
The "Sniper" 4-piece set gives +30% sniper headshot damage. Random mix of gear = random bonuses.
