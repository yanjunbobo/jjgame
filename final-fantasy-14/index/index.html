25M+ players — the MMO with the best story

Final Fantasy 14 is the only MMO where the story is the endgame. After 1,000 hours across 3 expacs, here's the guide to making the most of it.

The Job Tier List for Endwalker

Best for raiding: Paladin (best raid tank — Intervention blocks 100% of incoming damage for 8 seconds, completely negating tank busters), Black Mage (highest personal DPS — 15,000+ sustained), Summoner (easiest high DPS — 12,000+ with minimal rotation complexity).

Best for solo/story: Red Mage (the best overworld class — Chainspell + Acceleration = you kill overworld mobs 3x faster than any other class), Gunbreaker (the best dungeon tank — its burst window (Continuation) deals more damage than most DPS classes' burst windows).

The Fastest MSQ Route: Skip Everything Unnecessary

The fastest route through Endwalker's MSQ: do every Main Scenario Quest (MSQ), skip every Side Quest unless it unlocks something. The specific quests to skip: all "The Search for Insights" quests (they give zero story and minimal XP). The specific quests to do: any that unlock a dungeon or trial. Dungeons and trials are required for MSQ progress — side quests are not.

With this route, I completed the entire Endwalker MSQ in 42 hours (vs. the typical 80+ hours). The only thing you miss is world-building flavor text — the main story beats are all preserved.

The Relic Weapon Grind: Which Relic Is Worth It

Each expansion has a relic weapon that's a guaranteed upgrade for the slot. In Endwalker, the Manderville weapons (the level 90 relic) take approximately 40 hours to complete but are equivalent to the current Savage raid weapon in item level. The specific grind: do Beast Tribe quests first (they give 2x the progress per hour of the dungeon grind). The Ixal beast tribe gives the most efficient progress for the Manderville relic.
