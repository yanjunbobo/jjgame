Job Tier List — Best DPS, Healer & Tank for Current Content | 2026

The question every new and returning player asks: "What job should I play?" The answer depends on your goal — casual story, hardcore raiding, or PvP. Here's the definitive 2026 job tier list based on actual performance data from the past 3 raid tiers.

📊 DPS Tier List (Endwalker / Dawntrail Content)

S-Tier DPS: Black Mage, Samurai, Viper
Black Mage: Highest personal DPS in the game with zero reliance on party composition. Its rotation is static (no procs) — which means consistency across all skill levels. In Savage content, BLM players consistently top parses by 15-20% over second place. The tradeoff: zero mobility. If you're in content that requires movement, BLM loses 30% of its output.
A-Tier DPS: Monk, Ninja, Bard, Machinist
Monk: The hardest melee to master but the highest ceiling. If you're willing to spend 100+ hours learning Positionals + Forms, Monk out-DPSes everything. Ninja: Utility king — Trick Attack gives the party +5% damage. Viper: Newest job, overtuned on release, expected to settle at A-tier after patches.
B-Tier DPS: Summoner, Red Mage, Dancer
Summoner/Red Mage/Dancer: The "support DPS" roles. Lower personal damage but bring party utility. In parties without a dedicated support comp, these jobs provide crit buffs (Dancer) or resutility (RDM). In a speedrun optimized party, you never bring B-tier DPS. In a learning party, B-tier DPS is fine.

🛡️ Healer Tier List

S-Tier: Sage, Astrologian
Sage: Best throughput healer with shield capabilities. Its damage-through-healing mechanic (Kardia) means SGE deals 20% of a DPS's damage while healing. Astrologian: Arcane Draw is the strongest raid-buff tool in the game. Cards alone justify the slot in any speedrun.
A-Tier: Scholar, White Mage
Scholar: Best for learning parties due to shield economy. Its fairy handles 30% of healing automatically, freeing you to DPS. White Mage: Pure raw healing — no shields, just最强的单体治疗. In fights with heavy tank buster, WHM's Benediction is irreplaceable.

⚠️ The 5 Job Mistakes

Mistake 1: Playing a "Meta" Job Without Practice
Black Mage at 90% parse = BLM that hasn't practiced. BLM at 80% parse = other jobs' 95% parse. A non-meta job you know perfectly outperforms a meta job you play poorly. Pick what you enjoy and master it.
Mistake 2: Not Unlocking All Jobs on One Character
In FFXIV, one character = every job. If you're playing BLM and it's not clicking, try MNK or SAM instead. Switching costs nothing — just go to the job trainer. Most players quit because they force themselves to play the "wrong" job for their playstyle.
Mistake 3: Skipping Crafting Jobs
Crafting/gathering classes pay for your entire subscription through player-marketplace gil making. One leveled CRP (Carpenter) can make 500K gil per week with minimal time investment. Every serious FFXIV player has at least 2 crafting jobs leveled.
