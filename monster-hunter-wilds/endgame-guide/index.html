1. Story Completion Checklist

Before diving into endgame, ensure you've completed these story beats. Each unlocks something valuable:

✓
Complete Chapter 5

Unlocks High Rank investigations and Augmented Weapon upgrades.

✓
Unlock All Smithy Upgrades

Enables armor sphere crafting from monster materials.

✓
Complete 3 Apex Investigations

Unlocks decoration melding at the Elder Melder.

✓
Research Level 50+ for Key Monsters

Higher research = better decoration drop rates in investigations.

2. High Rank Progression Path

High Rank (HR) post-game follows a specific progression. Skip steps and you'll waste hours farming materials you don't need.

1

HR 20-30: Jagras set + Quelm weapons

The base HR set is sufficient. Focus on unlocking Investigation hunts via the Guild Hall.

2

HR 30-50: Mixed set from Aptonoth farming

Farm Aptonoth in Windward Plains for +12 Attack Boost materials. Create a mixed set with Attack Boost 4 + Weakness Exploit 2.

3

HR 50-70: Apex investigations unlock

At HR 50, Apex monster investigations unlock. These are your primary decoration farming source.

4

HR 70+: Endgame build crafting

With decorations, you can now build meta sets. Focus on getting Attack Boost 6, Weakness Exploit 3, Critical Eye 4+.

3. Decoration System Explained

Decorations are the most important progression item in late-game. They slot into armor pieces and provide additional skill points. The difference between a build with decorations and without is 30-40% more damage.

Decoration Tiers

Common (Blue) ~60% drop rate
Rare (Purple) ~25% drop rate
Very Rare (Gold) ~12% drop rate
Legendary (Red) ~3% drop rate

Key Decorations to Farm

Attack Jewel 4
+2 Attack Boost. Get 4 for Attack Boost 6.
Tender Jewel 4
+2 Weakness Exploit. Get 3 for WX 3.
Venom Jewel 4
+2 Challenger. Great for meta builds.
Expert Jewel 1
+3 Critical Eye. Rarest decoration.

4. Best Farming Routes

🦴

Route 1: Aptonoth Bone Farming (30 min/hr)

Fastest way to get armor spheres and early decoration materials. Aptonoth spawns in large groups in Windward Plains Area 2.

Route:

Start at Camp 1 → Run to Area 2 (30 seconds) → Kill 8 Aptonoth → Fast travel back → Repeat.

Reward: ~40 Dragonvein Bone per 30 min + research points.

💎

Route 2: Mining Circuit (50 min/hr)

For decoration materials and rare ores. Focus on Crystal locations in Iceshard Cliffs.

Route:

Camp 2 → Mine 6 Crystal nodes → Cross bridge to second cliff area → Mine 4 more nodes → Return via fast travel.

Reward: ~15 Rare Decoration materials + 30 Dragonvein Ore.

⚔️

Route 3: Apex Investigation Farming (Best ROI)

Most efficient decoration farming. Use Investigation Bonus (purple icon) for +50% decoration drop rate.

Strategy:
  • • Always pick 3-fade (15 min) investigations for fastest clear time.
  • • Target monsters you're research level 50+ for.
  • • Bring Meowcentrates for bonus research points.
  • • Capture (not carve) for +20% rare material chance.
🎰

Route 4: Elder Melder Grinding

Use excess materials to meld decorations at the Elder Melder. Trade 10 common decorations for 1 rare guaranteed.

Optimal Melding:

Stack 100+ common decorations → Trade in batches of 10 → Expected: 1 rare per trade + chance for Very Rare.

5. Weapon Augmentation

At HR 50+, you can augment weapons using Augmentation Materials from Apex monsters. Each augmentation adds +5% base damage and unlocks a new upgrade tier.

Augmentation Tiers

  • • Tier 1: +5% damage, requires Apex Claw
  • • Tier 2: +10% damage, requires Apex Horn
  • • Tier 3: +15% damage, requires Apex Fang
  • • Tier 4: +20% damage, requires Apex Gem

Priority Augmentations

  • • 1st: Your main hunting weapon
  • • 2nd: A ranged weapon for flying monsters
  • • 3rd: Support weapon for status builds

Tip: Save Apex Gems for your final weapon. Only one weapon needs full Tier 4 augmentation for the meta. All other weapons benefit more from decoration quality.

7. Apex Monster Hunts

Apex monsters are the toughest challenges in Wilds. Each Apex has unique materials for decorations and augmentations.

Apex Zoreos

Fire weakness. Drops: Attack decorations, Fire augments.

Best hunted with Water Bow + Haste Coat.

Apex Hydraxes

Thunder weakness. Drops: Critical decorations, Water augments.

Best hunted with Thunder Dual Blades.

Apex Vaalgorath

Ice/Dragon weakness. Drops: All decoration types, Dragon augments.

Use Ice Long Sword. Flash bombs essential.

Apex Naelucer

Thunder weakness. Drops: Status decorations, general augments.

Easiest Apex. Good for learning mechanics.

8. Time-Saving Tips

1.

Use Radial Menu — Map your commonly-used items (potions, flash bombs, whetfish) to radial shortcuts. Saves 2-3 seconds per use, which adds up over hundreds of hunts.

2.

Pause during loading — Open the menu during quest prep screen to pause material gathering timer. Use this to prep without feeling rushed.

3.

Optimal fast travel — Always return to base camp after capturing, not to quest complete. This loads the next hunt screen 30 seconds faster.

4.

Investigation rerolling — If your investigation has bad modifiers (no faints allowed, small area), exit to title and reload. You might get better modifiers.
