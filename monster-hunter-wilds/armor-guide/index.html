1. Low Rank Armor Sets

🦎

Aknosom Feather Set

Available: Chapter 2-3

The best Low Rank set overall. Aknosom is the first Large Monster you hunt. High defense, solid skills, and easy to upgrade.

Skills
Recovery Up 2 • Stamina Surge 1
Materials
Aknosom Scale x15, Feather x10, Beak x3
🌿

THORNBUSH Set

Available: Chapter 3+

For SnS and Bow users. Poison-focused. The Thornbush Dagger (from smithy) applies poison on every hit. Great for beginners.

Skills: Poison Attack 3 • Marathon Runner 2
⚔️

Readyman Set (Mixed)

Crafted from multiple monsters

For Charge Blade and Great Sword. Mix Aknosom chest with other pieces for Attack Boost 4 + Weakness Exploit 2. This mixed set carries you through all of Low Rank and into early High Rank.

⚡ Pro Tip: Don't fully upgrade the Aknosom set. Upgrade only the chest and helm, then mix with drops from other monsters. Saves 40% of your material farming time.

2. High Rank Transition Guide

High Rank unlocks after completing Chapter 5. Your Low Rank armor is instantly obsolete — monsters deal 2x damage and have 30% more HP.

The good news: you can capture monsters instead of carving to get their High Rank materials. Always cap when available.

First High Rank Set

The Quemm Warrior Set from the Smithy (unlocked automatically). It's the only set available in early High Rank.

Upgrade path:
Quemm I → II → III → IV → V

Second High Rank Set

After hunting 3 Large Monsters in High Rank, the Jagras Set becomes craftable. Jagras is easy to kill and gives solid skills.

Skill:
Attack Boost 3 • Recovery Speed 2

3. Essential Skills Priority

In order of priority. Only stack these skills — ignore everything else in early game.

#1
Weakness Exploit 3 — +50% affinity on weak spots. Best damage skill in the game.
#2
Attack Boost 4+ — +5% raw damage, more at higher levels.
#3
Critical Eye 4+ — +20% affinity at level 4. Stack with Weakness Exploit.
#4
Stamina Surge 3 — For bow/Dual Blades/Long Sword. Faster stamina regen.

4. Fast Farming Spots

🌾 Great Wirebug Farming

Go to the Wounded Hollow area. There are 6 Great Wirebug spawns. Collect them, carve, and return to base. 10 minutes = 60 Wirebug tails. Best early HR material.

🦴 Bone Farming

In Iceshard Cliffs, the bone deposit near camp 2 respawns every 3 minutes. Just run in, pick up, fast travel back. Great for smithing early game weapons.

💎 Rare Ore Nodes

Mine Dragonvein Ore in the volcanic area on your second visit. One node cycle (all nodes) gives ~8 rare ores. Reload the area by going to camp and back.
