⛏️ Skull Cave Floor Strategy (The Key to Iridium)

Optimal Descent Pattern:

  • Skip all levels below 100 — the ore spawn rate for Iridium doesn't become consistent until level 120+
  • In Skull Cave, each staircase costs 25 Omni Geode + 1 Coal. Craft stairs only when necessary
  • The best skull cave floors: 120-130 (Iridium + Omni Geodes), 140-150 (iridium + gems)
  • Use Bombs: Mega Bombs ($600 from Dwarf) destroy 5x5 area — 1 hour of mega bomb mining = 4 hours of pickaxe mining

Essential Items for Skull Cave:

  • Yoba Mega Bombs (100+) — the faster you clear nodes, the deeper you go
  • Spicy Eel + Pumpkin Soup (+34 luck food) — 10% better drops
  • Escape Ramps — always craft escape ramps on floors 90-100
  • Stairs — carry 10+ stairs for skipping infested/magma dangerous floors

⚠️ The 5 Mining Mistakes

Mistake 1: Mining Without Lucky Food
Spicy Eel (+34 luck, 50g from the Oasis on Fridays) increases ore spawn rate by 10%. Over 100 levels, that's 10 extra ore nodes. Worth the 50g every time.
Mistake 2: Not Using Bombs in Skull Cave
Pickaxe mining in skull cave = 1 node per 30 seconds. Mega bomb = 5 nodes in 1 second. If you're not using bombs, you're wasting 150x your time.
Mistake 3: Staying on Levels Below 120 for Iridium
Level 115 has 5% iridium spawn rate. Level 125 has 20%. The difference between spending 3 hours vs 30 minutes getting 50 iridium is 10 levels of depth.
Mistake 4: Not Upgrading Pickaxe to Gold Before Level 80
Gold pickaxe mines iron nodes in 0.5 seconds vs 2 seconds with copper. The upgrade costs 2,500g and saves 75% of your mining time. Always upgrade to Gold before mining level 40+.
Mistake 5: Opening Omni Geodes Without Metal Detector
Omni Geodes contain random ores. Without the Metal Detector (1,000g, from Dwarf), you have no idea if a geode contains Iridium or just Copper. Always check first.
