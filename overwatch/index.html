Overwatch 2 replaced the original Overwatch in October 2022 — all Overwatch 1 players received Overwatch 2 for free. If you're looking for Overwatch 2 guides, please visit our Overwatch 2 guide page. For the original Overwatch experience (now legacy), here's a brief history and what it meant to gaming.

Overwatch 1 vs Overwatch 2

What Changed in OW2
5v5 replaced 6v6, 1 tank per team, new heroes (Sojourn, Junker Queen, Kiriko, Lifeweaver, Juno, Mauga, Ramattra rework)
What Stayed the Same
Core heroes (Tracer, Genji, Reinhardt, Mercy, etc.), map pool, the fundamental shooter feel
