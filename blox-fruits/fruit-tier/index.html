⚠️ The 5 Fruit Mistakes

Mistake 1: Buying Fruits from the Shop
The in-game shop sells fruits at inflated prices. Dragon Fruit costs 3,500 Robux in the shop but trades for 500 Robux value in player trades. NEVER buy from the shop unless you're spending Robux intentionally to support the developer.
Mistake 2: Eating a Common Fruit When You Have a Good One
Once you eat a fruit, it's permanent. Eating Rocket Fruit to fill hunger = losing your inventory slot. Only eat fruits you've confirmed are "trash tier" and have no trading value.
Mistake 3: Not Checking Trade Values Before Trading
"Blox Fruits trading values" changes every week with updates. A fruit worth 1 million belly last month might be worth 200K this month. Always check the official tier list before trading.
Mistake 4: Focusing on PvP Fruits in Early Game
PvP fruits like Dough and Venom are dominant in PvP but weak in PvE. If you're below level 700, use a PvE fruit (Dragon, Leopard) to grind faster. PvP fruits are for players who've already completed the game.
Mistake 5: Farming Fruits Incorrectly
The most efficient fruit farming method: join a server with the fruit you want, find its location on the Blox Fruits map wiki, and camp at that spawn point. Check spawn timer: most fruits respawn every 30-60 minutes.
