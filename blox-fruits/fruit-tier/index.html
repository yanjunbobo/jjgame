Every fruit ranked by PvP value and trade value

The Blox Fruits meta shifts every season as new fruits are added. Here's the current tier list for Season 5.

Fruit Tier List (Season 5)

S-Tier PvP: Leopard (speed + combo — can land 3 combos before the opponent can respond, making it the best 1v1 fruit), Dough (parry + damage — the best counter to most S-tier fruits because parry reflects their knockback).

S-Tier PvE: Buddha (size + summoning — summons a second Buddha that tanks, making every boss 50% easier), Spirit (healing aura + damage — the best fruit for group PvE because the aura heals the entire party).

A-Tier: Leopard (decent but outclassed by the S-tiers), T-Rex (the best brute-force fruit — deals 30% more damage than any other fruit but is slow and predictable).

The Trade Value Tier List

Trade value is different from combat value. Limited fruits (event-only) trade at 3x their combat value. Dough, Leopard, and Buddha are always available (not limited), so their trade values reflect combat value. Limited fruits like Dragon, Leopard (Mythical), and Kitsune trade at 2-3x because players collect them.

The Specific Leopard Combo That Wins 1v1s

The Leopard combo: Z (空中攻击) → X (special) → C (空中攻击) → Z (地面 attack) → X (special) → repeat. Each cycle of this combo deals 30% of an opponent's health and takes 2 seconds. Two cycles = 60% HP = kill (since most opponents don't have 60% HP remaining after the first cycle). The key: the X special must be used within 0.5 seconds of the Z attack or the combo drops.
