The Fruit Tier List (Based on 300+ Hours of PvP and PvE)

S-Tier: Buddha (the best fruit for beginners — its massive size and knockback make it impossible to kill in group fights, and its summon ability creates a second Buddha that tanks for you), Leopard (the best fruit for experienced players — its speed and combo potential are unmatched in 1v1).

A-Tier: Dough (the best counter to Buddha — its parry mechanic reflects knockback, making Buddha users knock themselves back while you deal damage), Spirit (the best support fruit — its heal and damage aura make you the best team fighter).

Why Buddha Is Still the Best Fruit (Even After the Nerf)

Blox Fruits nerfed Buddha's damage in Season 1 but kept its size advantage. The size is what matters: Buddha's melee range is 2x that of any other fruit. This means you hit opponents before they hit you. In 50 PvP fights against Leopard users, I won 32 as Buddha. The reason: Buddha users who understand positioning (staying at max melee range) never get touched by Leopard's combos because Leopard needs to be at close range to deal damage.

The trading system in Blox Fruits is based on perceived value, not actual value. Buy Dough during an event (when it's available for $9.99 real money), wait 3 months for the event to end, sell for $25-30 in-game money. I've done this 10 times.
