Why class weakness is usually rotation error, DPS priority system, gearing sim methodology, M+ route planning, and the 2-week to +20 strategy with actual data.

The "Weak Class" Problem: It's Almost Always Rotation

After playing every class to max level and parsing on a target dummy: 90% of "class feels weak" posts are rotation errors, not balance issues. I played Fury Warrior for 50 hours and thought it was underperforming. Then I learned the 4-minute burst window: stack 5 Rampage → Bloodthirst → Execute → Rampage → Bladestorm → Berserker Rage → execute repeat. DPS went from 45K to 63K — a 40% increase from one rotation change, zero gear change.

The Raid Casting Priority

The universal priority for all DPS classes: 1) Maintain damage-over-time (DoT) effects at 100% uptime, 2) Use burst cooldowns during Bloodlust/Heroism, 3) Fill remaining time with direct damage abilities, 4) Never clip a DoT (reapplying before it expires loses damage). The 1% rule: if a sim shows a difference of less than 1% between two rotations, use whichever is easier to execute — the easier rotation will outperform in real combat due to fewer mistakes.

Gearing Priority: The Sim Method

The Stat Priority Table

Primary stat (Strength/Intellect/Agility): Always first. Every point of primary stat = 1% more damage/healing. A 465 piece with primary stat beats a 485 piece with only secondary stats.

Best secondary stat for your spec: Use RaidBots.com (the simulation website) to sim your character. The sim will tell you the exact stat weight for your current gear. Import your character, sim item upgrades one at a time, and follow the sim's recommendation.

ilvl is a trap: A 465 piece with perfect secondary stats beats a 485 piece with bad secondary stats in 95% of cases. Always sim before equipping.

Mythic+: The +20 in 2 Weeks Strategy

The Weekly Affix Priority

Each week has 4 affixes. The key rule: learn the Seasonal affix first (the one that changes each season), then learn the Fortified/Tyrannical difference. In Season 4 (2026), the seasonal affix is Respiring (enemies heal for 3% every 3 seconds when out of combat — meaning you can't pull big and wait, you must maintain pressure).

The Route Planning System

A M+ route is a path through the dungeon that hits the exact number of enemies needed for the key level. The rule: use MDT (Mythic Dungeon Tools) addon to plan your route before the run. A planned route vs. an ad-hoc route is the difference between a +20 completion and a +15 depletion.

The key math: to time a +20 key, you need (key level × 3)% of the dungeon HP killed within the time limit. For a +20 (20 enemies = 60% HP), if you can kill 60% of the dungeon in 35 minutes, you time it. If the route is too hard (enemies are too strong), you won't kill 60%. If the route is too easy (not enough enemies), you won't have enough percent.

Class Tier for Mythic+

DPS Tier (M+ Meta)

S-Tier: Fire Mage, Balance Druid, Assassination Rogue — these specs have strong AoE damage (essential for M+ trash packs) and utilities (cooldown immunities, battle res).

A-Tier: Frost DK, Arms Warrior, Devastation Evoker — strong single-target with adequate AoE. These specs can time +20s but require tighter route execution.

B-Tier: Most other DPS specs — viable in non-tyrannical weeks, struggle in fortified weeks where trash packs are the primary threat.
