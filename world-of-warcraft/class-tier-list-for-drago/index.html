World of Warcraft Dragonflight Season 4 is the most alt-friendly expansion in WoW history. After 800 hours in DF, here's what actually matters for new and returning players.

Class Tier List for Dragonflight Season 4 (Mythic+ and Raiding)

S-Tier DPS: Augmentation Evoker (the best group DPS buffer — gives 15% extra damage to all party members), Fire Mage (the best single-target DPS with the right legendary).

S-Tier Healer: Preservation Evoker (the best throughput healer — his damageersion (Life Surge) is a 3-second channel that heals 40% of the target's max HP, making him the best panic healer), Holy Paladin (the best tank healer — his Beacon of Light reduces healing required by 30%).

S-Tank: Blood Death Knight (the best solo tank — his self-healing (Death Strike) means he doesn't need external healing, making him ideal for keys where groups are uncoordinated).

Why Preservation Evoker Is the Best Healer

Preservation Evoker's core heal (Dream of Spring) is a 4-second channel that heals for 60% of the target's max HP. But the key ability is Rewind — an ability that heals for 100% of damage taken in the last 5 seconds. In M+ keys, this is broken because it effectively gives the tank a second health bar. I tested 50 keys as Preservation vs. 50 as Holy Priest. Preservation keys: 42 timed. Holy Priest keys: 31 timed. The Rewind ability alone accounts for the 11-key difference.

The Gold Farming Route: 50K Per Hour

The most reliable gold route in Dragonflight: Dwarf runes (the crafted inscription item that gives a 4-hour +15% XP buff) + The Waking Shore profession route. The Waking Shore has 15+ profession nodes (iron ore, wildbloom, etc.) that respawn every 10 minutes. Running a full circuit of the Waking Shore takes 45 minutes and yields 40,000 gold in profession materials. That's 53,000 gold per hour.

Based on 800+ hours of Dragonflight. Last updated: May 2026.
