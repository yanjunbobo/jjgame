DOORS - Hotel 50 and Speedrun Guide

Why You Keep Dying at Hotel 50 (Not Enough Vitamins)

I died at Hotel 50 over 30 times before figuring out the pattern. The specific issue: you're using vitamins too early (Door 30-40) when you should save them for Door 60-90. Vitamins give +20% speed and +20% damage for 30 seconds. If you use them at Door 30, they expire by Door 50 and you're slow for the hardest stretch. Rule: save vitamins until Door 60 minimum.

The Entity Patterns That Save Runs

Rush: when lights flicker, face the wall. The Rush passes behind you. Screech: when you hear the screech sound, hide in a locker immediately. Ambush: eyes on the ceiling at doorways. When eyes appear, you have 1.5 seconds before it drops. Hunt: when the door turns red, RUN. Don't look back. I memorized these patterns for 50 hours. My Hotel 50 completion rate went from 30% to 85%.

The Speedrun Strategy That Got Me Sub-20 Minutes

After 200+ hours of DOORS, I achieved a sub-20 minute run. The specific route: 1) Don't search every room - only jackpot rooms (rooms with hints, books, or special items). 2) Rush every door 1-50. 3) At Door 50, check if it's Randy (the bad entity) - if yes, reset. 4) Doors 50-100 at careful speed. The key insight: a fast Door 49 is better than a slow Door 49. Speed > thoroughness.
