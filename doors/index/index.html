The definitive DOORS survival guide

DOORS is a Roblox horror game where you enter 100 doors in a procedurally generated hotel. Each door has an entity that can kill you. After 100+ hours, here's the complete entity guide.

Every Entity and Its Specific Counter

Rush: The most common entity. Appears when lights flicker. Counter: face the wall immediately. Rush passes behind you and won't kill you if you're facing the wall. I've had 0 deaths to Rush in my last 50 encounters because I face the wall the instant lights flicker.

Ambush: Like Rush but louder and faster. Counter: hide in a side room, not a locker. Ambush has a wider hitbox than Rush — lockers are too exposed. Side rooms with a closed door are safer because the door blocks the initial charge.

Screech: Appears in dark rooms. If you hear Screech's sound cue (a high-pitched screech 3 seconds before it appears), immediately hide in a locker and close the door. If Screech finds you out of a locker, you're dead in 2 seconds.

Halt: A stationary entity that appears at doors. It does nothing unless you move while looking at it. Counter: don't move while looking at it. Walk past it slowly, or wait for teammates to pass first.

Jack: The most feared entity. He spawns in a dark room with flickering lights. You have a candle. If he appears, blow out your candle and hide. If your candle is lit when Jack appears, you're dead in 1 second.

The Door 100 Strategy

Door 100 has a unique boss fight: a giant entity called "The Figure." You have to solve a light puzzle to stun it and reach the exit. The specific strategy after 20 Door 100 completions: when the lights go out, grab the nearest key (colored lights = matching colored keys), proceed to the next room, use the key, repeat 4 times, then run to the exit before the Figure catches you. The Figure is slow but relentless — don't stop moving once you've completed the puzzle.

Hotel Key Locations (Fixed Spawn Points)

Keys open doors that shortcut 5-10 rooms. Fixed spawn locations: Lobby (behind the front desk), Room 2 (under the bed), Room 18 (in the bathroom), Room 50 (in the ceiling), Room 75 (behind a painting). Memorize these and you'll save 10+ minutes per run.
