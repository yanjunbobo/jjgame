Stamina / Resin Guide — Optimal Daily Routine & What to Farm | 2026

"Zenless Zone Zero stamina" or "resin" is the #1 bottleneck for every player. "How to spend resin efficiently" has 1.4M monthly searches. The difference between smart and stupid resin spending: 2x your progress per week. This guide gives you the exact daily routine.

📊 Resin Regeneration & Cap

  • Resin cap: 160 (can hold up to 160 without spending)
  • Regeneration rate: 1 Resin every 8 minutes (180 minutes = full recharge)
  • Condensed Resin: Store 2x content for 1 Resin (requires 1 Condensed Resin craft)
  • Refined Resin (paid): 60 Primogems for 30 bonus Resin — NOT worth it for F2P

⏰ Optimal Daily Resin Routine

Morning (Login 5 min): Spend 160 Resin on: 160 domains (your best artifact set) OR ley line blossoms for experience/mora. Do NOT spend Resin on anything else in the morning.

Afternoon (Optional): If you earned Resin naturally (60-80 Resin), spend on whatever you're short on. If you have Fragile Resin saved, use 1 (40 Resin) on the weekend for double artifact drops.

Weekend Priority: Only farm artifacts on Saturday and Sunday. weekdays: ley lines + weapon materials. This is because artifact domains have a chance at double drops on weekends.

⚠️ The 5 Resin Mistakes

Mistake 1: Farming Artifacts Before AR 45
Before AR 45, artifact domains give 2-3 star artifacts that are replaced within 2 days. The Resin cost is the same but the return is 5x worse. Farm weapons and talents first.
Mistake 2: Not Using Condensed Resin
Condensed Resin doubles the content you can do in one Resin. But it expires in 7 days. Always have 2 Condensed Resin ready before your daily login — otherwise you're wasting 50% of your Resin efficiency.
Mistake 3: Spending Resin on Wrong Day
Weekdays: weapon materials + talent books. Weekends: artifact domains. Using Resin on artifacts on Tuesday = missing the Saturday/Sunday 2x drop bonus.
Mistake 4: Hoarding Fragile Resin Indefinitely
Fragile Resin expires at level 60 cap. Don't save Fragile Resin for more than 2-3 weeks. Use them during events that boost specific domain drops.
Mistake 5: Wasting Resin on Overflow Resources
If you're at 160/160 Resin and it's bedtime, you lost 160 Resin worth of progress. Always spend your Resin before bed, even if it's just ley lines. There's no overnight Resin retention.
