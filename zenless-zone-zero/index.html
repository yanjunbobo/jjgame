🎯 S-Rank vs A-Rank & F2P Team Guide

Zenless Zone Zero's S-rank gacha makes character selection critical for F2P players. This guide covers which S-ranks are worth pulling, the best A-rank alternatives, and the F2P team compositions that compete with S-rank teams in 2026.

S-Rank Priority

Jane Doe (S): The highest personal DPS in the game. Her disorder build with Qingyi deals more damage than any other F2P-accessible combination. Worth pulling even as F2P if you have any interest in optimization.

Zhu Yuan (S): The most versatile Anomaly character. Works in virtually any team composition. The S-rank identity provides significant stat boosts that A-rank characters can't match.

Skip Characters: Any S-rank that overlaps with a strong A-rank alternative. Burnice is inferior to the A-rank Soldier/Anby for Electro-Physical. Save pulls for Jane or Zhu Yuan.

A-Rank Alternatives

Grace (A): The best A-rank DPS. Her Electric anomaly application is competitive with S-rank characters. Built-in crit rate passive reduces relic requirements.

Qingyi (A): The best stunner in the game bar none. Her stun buildup is faster than any S-rank stunner. Pair her with any DPS for instant team viability.

Koleda (A): The best A-rank stunner for fire teams. Her assist counter is fast and provides energy regeneration. The ultimate is one of the fastest stun windows in the game.

F2P Team: Grace + Qingyi + Lucy

This team is F2P-accessible and competitive. Grace applies Electric anomaly, Qingyi stuns, Lucy provides healing and crit rate buff. The team handles all content up to and including Shiyu Defense 7 without S-rank characters.
