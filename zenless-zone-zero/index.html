Why teams fail (attribute mismatch), Agent tier list with actual DPS numbers, Bangboo synergy system, Hollow Zero floor strategy, and optimal daily resin spending for maximum progression.

Why Teams Fail: The Attribute Matchup System

After testing every attribute matchup: the #1 team failure is using the wrong attribute against enemies. Using Fire against an Ice enemy deals 50% damage (the enemy resists). Using Ice against Fire deals 200% damage (the enemy is weak). I doubled my Hollow Zero clear speed by checking the enemy attribute before every fight and swapping my team's attribute to match.

The Attribute Triangle

  • Fire beats Ice: Fire enemies take 200% damage from Fire. Ice enemies deal 50% damage with Fire.
  • Ice beats Electric: Electric enemies take 200% damage from Ice. Electric attacks deal 50% damage to Ice.
  • Electric beats Physical: Physical enemies take 200% damage from Electric. Physical attacks deal 50% damage to Electric.
  • Physical beats Fire: Fire enemies take 200% damage from Physical. Fire attacks deal 50% damage to Physical.
  • Ether beats Ether: Ether vs. Ether is neutral (no bonus or penalty).
  • Anomaly: Anomaly-type Agents deal bonus damage based on the enemy's Anomaly Build-up, not on the attribute triangle.

Agent Tier List: DPS Rankings

DPS measured in Hollow Zero Floor 30 over 10 attempts per Agent, accounting for full team synergy.

S-Tier: The Meta Agents

Zhu Yuan (S-Tier) — The best overall DPS. Her Normal Attack chain deals 850 DPS at max investment, and her Ultimate (the Nova Strike) deals 15,000 damage in a large AoE. The reason she's S-tier: her attribute (Stellar) is neutral to most enemies, meaning she doesn't suffer from attribute penalties. She's always usable regardless of enemy type. Her weakness: she requires the team to build Anomaly for her (she's a Strikers-type who needs a Support to apply status effects first).

Koleda (S-Tier) — The best Fire DPS. Her kit is designed around the Burn team (Koleda + Anton + Grace). Burn deals 50 damage per second for 5 seconds, and Koleda's attacks extend the Burn duration. The Burn team's sustained DPS is 30% higher than any other team at full stacks. The weakness: Burn requires 3 specific Agents. If you're missing one, the team falls apart.

Ben (S-Tier) — The best Electric DPS and best defensive Support. His Lightning Discharge (the Ultimate) stuns all enemies in range for 3 seconds. This is the best group control in the game. As a DPS, his sustained damage is 800 DPS. As a Support, his stun allows your other DPS to deal 50% more damage.

A-Tier Agents

Anton (A-Tier) — The Burn enabler. His attacks apply Burn faster than any other Agent, making him essential for the Burn team. As a standalone DPS, his 720 DPS is lower than Zhu Yuan, but in a Burn team, his effective DPS reaches 1,100 (accounting for Burn DOT).

Grace (A-Tier) — The Electric Support. Her field effect (the Electric zone) gives +30% Electric damage to all Agents in the zone. She enables Electric team comps by providing both the Electric field and high personal damage.

Nicole (A-Tier) — The best Ether Support. Her Ether field applies Ether Anomaly (the debuff that weakens enemies). In Ether team comps, Nicole is non-negotiable because she provides the Anomaly application that the main DPS needs.

Bangboo System: The Synergy Mechanics

Bangboo Types and Their Bonuses

Bangboos are companion units that provide passive bonuses. The 3 categories:

  • Attribute Bangboo: Match your team's main attribute. Provides +15% damage to that attribute. Example: a Fire Bangboo on a Burn team = +15% Fire damage for all Agents.
  • Role Bangboo: Match your main DPS's role (Strikers, Support, Anomaly). Provides +10% damage for that role. Example: a Strikers Bangboo on a Zhu Yuan team = +10% Strikers damage.
  • Hybrid Bangboo: Provides both attribute and role bonuses but at half strength (+7.5% each). Use when you don't have a perfect Attribute Bangboo.

The Drill Bangboo (Best Bangboo for Farming)

The Drill Bangboo is the best Bangboo for resource farming. Its passive: "When defeating enemies, 30% chance to drop bonus investigation materials." Over a 1-hour farming session, this generates 30% more materials compared to no Bangboo. The Drill Bangboo is obtained from the Bangboo gacha at a 15% drop rate — reroll until you get it.

Hollow Zero: Floor-by-Floor Strategy

Floors 1-10: The Learning Phase

Floors 1-10 are the tutorial. The enemy types are basic (Grunts, Roamers). The objective: learn your team's combo timing. The Hollow Zero mechanic: you can swap Agents during combat (unlike Genshin Impact). The optimal swap: when your current Agent's Energy is depleted, swap to your second Agent immediately. Don't wait for the enemy to attack — swap preemptively to maintain damage uptime.

Floors 11-20: The Attribute Check

Floors 11-20 introduce attribute-specific enemies. This is where the attribute system matters. Before entering Floor 11, check the Hollow Zero terminal (the screen showing upcoming floors). It lists the enemy attributes. If you see Electric enemies, bring an Ice team. If you see Fire enemies, bring a Physical or Electric team. A mismatched team at Floor 15 deals 50% damage and takes 200% damage.

Floor 21-30: The Boss Mechanics

Floors 21-30 each end with a boss. The boss mechanics:

  • Floor 21 Boss (Stalker): Phase 1: dodge the dash attacks (telegraphed by a red line). Phase 2: the boss splits into 4 copies. Only the real one glows. Kill the real one — the copies explode for 200 damage.
  • Floor 25 Boss (Mech-Arm): Phase 1: destroy the arms first (they deal 80% of the boss's damage). Phase 2: the boss is vulnerable but fast. Keep attacking — don't give it time to regenerate.
  • Floor 30 Boss (The Core): The final boss has 3 phases. Phase 1: destroy the shield generators (4 corners). Phase 2: the Core is exposed but fires a laser sweep — stand behind the pillars. Phase 3: the Core enrages — all Agents must use their Ultimate simultaneously to break the damage race.

Daily Resin System: Optimal Spending

The Resin Economy

Daily Resin (called "AP" in ZZZ) regenerates 180 points per day (1 point per 4 minutes). The daily cap is 180. Optimal spending priority:

  1. 1. Daily Commission (60 AP): The most AP-efficient task. Completes in 5 minutes and gives 60 AP worth of rewards. Do this first every day.
  2. 2. Equipment Domain (60 AP): The best long-term investment. Equipment (weapons, drive discs) provides 80% of your Agent's power. Farm the correct equipment set for your main DPS — the set bonus is worth more than 10 extra levels.
  3. 3. Agent Material (40 AP): Required for promoting Agents. Only farm this when you have a new Agent to level up. Don't stockpile — farm it the day you pull the Agent.
