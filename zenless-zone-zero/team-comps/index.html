⚠️ The 5 Team Building Mistakes

Mistake 1: Running 3 Strikers (no anomaly coverage)
Strikers deal raw damage but can't apply elemental effects. Always have at least 1 Anomaly agent in your team.
Mistake 2: Not Matching Element Types
ZZZ has a 30% elemental damage bonus when all 3 team members share the same element. Always prioritize element matching over raw stats.
Mistake 3: Undervaluing Support Agents
Nicole's Ether field alone adds 60% critical damage to Zhu Yuan. Always keep 1 dedicated support in your team.
Mistake 4: Ignoring Drive Disc Sets
Drive Disc sets give 20-30% damage bonuses. Using random discs instead of set bonuses = 25% less damage.
Mistake 5: Not Having a Dedicated Stunner
Stunners fill the Stun gauge 2x faster than other classes. In high-difficulty content, the stunner determines how fast you can burst enemies down.
