Team compositions for every game mode

The team composition system in ZZZ is about element synergy and role coverage. Here's the complete breakdown.

The Role System

Stunners: Apply stun to enemies. Stun is a meter that, when filled, stuns the enemy for 3 seconds. During stun, all damage dealt is 40% higher. Stunners are the most important role — without them, you can't kill anything above level 50. Anby is the best F2P stunner. Ellen is the best premium stunner.

DPS: Deal the actual damage. The best F2P DPS is Billy (charged shots deal 3x damage and can be cancel-comboed). The best premium DPS is Jane (her Ex Special deals 200% more damage than any other DPS in the game).

Support: Provide buffs and debuffs. Nicole is the best F2P support (her grab pulls enemies together, making AOE damage 3x more effective). Soukaku is the best premium support (her ice buff gives +30% damage to ice-affected enemies).

The Element Synergy System

ZZZ has 4 elements: Electric, Ice, Fire, and Physical. Enemies have elemental weaknesses. When you hit an enemy with their weakness element, you deal 30% more damage and fill the stun meter 50% faster. The specific rule: always have at least 2 elements in your team. If the enemy is weak to Electric, you want at least 2 Electric agents. If they're weak to Ice, 2 Ice agents.

The Best All-Around Team: Anby-Ellen-Soukaku

This team covers every situation: Anby stuns (Electric), Ellen DPS (Ice), Soukaku support (Ice buffer). Against any enemy, at least 2 of your 3 agents will be hitting a weakness. This team clears every Hollow Zero floor up to level 100 without specific counters needed.
